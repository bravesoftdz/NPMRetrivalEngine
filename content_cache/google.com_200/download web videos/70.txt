     <h2 class="title">What Is React?</h2> 
     <p>ReactJS is a JavaScript library that has become the tool of choice for easily building dynamic user interfaces. The power is in the <em>Virtual Dom</em> — when your application's data changes, React figures out which parts of your document need to be changed, and immediately update only those parts.</p> 
     <p>Hundreds of thousands of developers from small agencies to large companies use React to deliver top notch experiences in their apps and dynamic website components.</p> 
     <p>Like who? Facebook.com is made up of thousands of React components. Instagram web viewer is entirely built in React. The brand new Netflix movie browsing experience is powered by React. And many, <a href="https://github.com/facebook/react/wiki/Sites-Using-React" target="_blank">many more...</a></p>
