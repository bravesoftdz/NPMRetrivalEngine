      <h2>All you need to know about<br> Absolutely No Worries package</h2> 
      <p class="intro">The Absolutely No Worries package is a unique solution for all GoOpti passengers traveling to or from an airport. It was designed to make your journey with GoOpti as comfortable and stress-free as possible.</p> 
      <h3>What does it include?</h3> 
      <p>In case you miss your flight because your GoOpti transfer got delayed due to unpredictable events stated in tour Terms and Conditions, we find and buy you <strong>a new flight ticket</strong> to the same destination and of the same class to make sure you continue your journey as quickly as possible. If needed, we also find and pay for overnight <strong>accommodations.</strong></p> 
      <p>In case the airline carrier notifies you about a rescheduling of your flight at least 48 hours in advance, we arrange <strong>a new GoOpti transfer</strong> to the airport. If you flight gets delayed at arrival or is rerouted to land at another airport, we arrange a new GoOpti transfer or another replacement transfer for free.</p> 
      <h3>How to buy and use the package?</h3> 
      <p>You can select the Absolutely No Worries package only when booking transfers to or from airports. You can buy the package only for all passengers at once in one booking. The package cannot be added or bought after the booking has been made. If you buy the Absolutely No Worries package together with a return transfer, you will get <strong>the package for the return direction for free!</strong></p> 
      <p>In case you need a new flight ticket or a replacement service, call our call center (+386 1 320 4530) as soon as possible or send an e-mail to <a href="mailto:info@goopti.com" target="_top">info@goopti.com</a>. We will do our best to find a solution to your problem as quickly as possible. You can read the full terms and conditions of the Absolutely No Worries package <a target="_blank" href="https://www.goopti.com/en/legal/terms-conditions#no-worries">here</a>.</p>
