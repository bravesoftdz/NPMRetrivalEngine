     <div>
       PSA Travel recommends booking on Qantas or Hawaiian Airlines for flights that fit in with the conference itinerary. If you are planning your own travel before or after the conference then other airlines may be more suitable and PSA Travel can advise you of the best options available. 
      <br>
      <br>Please note that in order to purchase a return airfare, you must depart from and return to Australia on the same airline. Following are the flights we recommend for each portion of the program, which represent both the best and most economic options available.&nbsp;&nbsp; Special airfares are available from time to time which we will offer as alternatives when they provide better value. 
      <br> 
      <h3>Flights from Australia </h3>
      <strong>Pre-Conference Hawaii</strong> 
      <br>Depart Sydney for Honolulu on 20 May on either: 
      <br>1. Qantas Airways flight QF3 at 7.30pm connecting with Hawaiian Airlines flight HA258 to Kona. 
      <br>
      <br>2. Hawaiian Airlines flight HA452 at 9.25pm connecting with Hawaiian Airlines flight HA128 to Kona. 
      <br>
      <br>Connecting flights to Sydney are required from other Australian cities. 
      <br>
      <br>
      <strong>Pre-Conference Yosemite</strong> 
      <br>Depart Sydney for San Francisco on 21 May on Qantas Airways flight QF73 at 1.00pm, arriving in San Francisco at 9.30am the same day. 
      <br>
      <br>
      <strong>Main Conference San Francisco <br></strong>Depart Sydney for San Francisco on 27 May on Qantas Airways flight QF73 at 1.00pm, arriving in San Francisco at 9.30am the same day. 
      <br>
      <br>
      <strong>Flight from Hawaii to San Francisco</strong> 
      <br>Depart Honolulu for San Francisco on 26 May on Hawaiian Airlines flight HA12 at 1.30pm, arriving in San Francisco at 9.40pm the same day. 
      <br> 
      <h3>Flights to Australia <br></h3>
      <strong>Main Conference San Francisco <br></strong>Depart San Francisco on 3 June on either: 
      <br>1. Qantas Airways flight QF74 at 11.25pm to Sydney arriving at 7.00am on 5 June. Onward connections from Sydney are required for other cities. It is possible to go via Los Angeles to get direct flights to Melbourne and Brisbane however this may result in a higher fare. 
      <br>
      <br>2. Hawaiian Airlines flight HA11 at 9.10am to Honolulu connecting with HA451 to Sydney arriving at 7.30pm on 4 June and with HA443 to Brisbane arriving 7.45pm on 4 June. 
      <br>
      <br>
      <strong>Extended Main Conference Alaska <br></strong>1. If you travelled from Australia on Qantas then depart Anchorage on 8 June on Alaskan Airlines flight AS142 at 2.25pm to Los Angeles arriving at 8.40pm and connecting with QF12 at 10.30pm to Sydney, QF96 at 11.55pm to Melbourne or QF16 at 11.20pm to Brisbane. All flights arrive in Australia early morning on 10 June.
      <br>
      <br>2. If you travelled from Australia on Hawaiian Airlines, depart Anchorage on 8 June on Alaskan Airlines flight AS828 at 2.50pm to Honolulu arriving at 6.55pm. An overnight stay in Honolulu is required (at own expense) before the onward flights of HA451 to Sydney at 12.45pm arriving at 7.30pm on 10 June or HA443 to Brisbane at 1.30pm arriving 7.45pm on 10 June. 
      <br>
      <br>
      <strong>Post Conference Cruise</strong> 
      <br>Depart Vancouver on 17 June on Qantas code-share flight QF4558 at 2.12pm to Los Angeles arriving at 7.15pm and connecting with QF12 at 10.30pm to Sydney, QF94 at 10.10pm to Melbourne or QF16 at 11.20pm to Brisbane. All flights arrive in Australia early morning on 19 June. 
      <br> 
      <h3>Supplementary Pricing Information <br></h3>A number of airfare supplements are applicable depending the travel packages you purchase. Please note the base Main Conference Package includes return economy class group airfares on Qantas Airways, which are competitively priced, but have limited availability, so we suggest you book soon to take advantage of the best pricing available.
      <br>
      <br>
      <strong>Travelling just to Mainland USA </strong> 
      <p style="margin-right: 0px" dir="ltr"><strong><em>San Francisco Main Conference package <br></em></strong>The travel package includes return economy class airfares from Sydney, Melbourne and Brisbane flying Qantas via Sydney, connecting with the direct service to San Francisco. No supplements apply. <br><br><strong><em>San Francisco and Alaska combined package, plus the cruise</em></strong><br>The travel package includes return economy class airfares as per the Main Conference travel package and also includes a return flight from Vancouver to Australia via Los Angeles, flying Qantas direct non-stop from LA to Sydney, Melbourne or Brisbane. No supplements apply. <br><br><strong><em>San Francisco and Alaska only combined package<br></em></strong>The travel package includes return economy class airfares as per the Main Conference travel package but will require the purchase of an additional air ticket from Anchorage to San Francisco or Los Angeles to connect with a Qantas flight to Australia. Pricing is from $600 per person depending on the type of airfare purchased. <br><br><strong>Hawaii combined with Mainland USA</strong><br>The travel package includes return economy class airfares as per the Main Conference travel package but requires the purchase of a flight from Honolulu to San Francisco from $667 per person.</p>To qualify for group airfares you must travel on the group outbound dates for each package, but you may return at a later date. If you extend your travel a surcharge of $50 per person will apply, plus there may be additional airfare surcharges applicable. 
      <br>
      <br>
      <strong>Premium Economy Class on Qantas</strong> 
      <br>Travel in greater comfort on Qantas from $2,600 supplement 
      <br>
      <br>
      <strong>Business Class</strong> 
      <br>Business Class seats are available from $6,650 supplement
