       <div class="col-xs-12 pageBodyText"> 
        <p>Are you looking for the ultimate webshop module to build your next Umbraco ecommerce solution - then end your search. Tea Commerce provides the necessary tools to&nbsp;<strong>develop a powerful ecommerce solution</strong>&nbsp;on the Umbraco platform without prior .NET experience. You can implement the Umbraco ecommerce solution using only XSLT / Razor, MVC, HTML, CSS and JavaScript.</p> 
        <p>Tea Commerce makes it a breeze to accept online credit card payments within your Umbraco ecommerce solution. You simply select the desired payment gateway, configure its settings and your Umbraco ecommerce solution is ready to recieve online payments. Tea Commerce supports a wide range of payment gateways out of the box.</p> 
        <p><a href="http://our.umbraco.org/projects/website-utilities/tea-commerce" target="_blank" title="A true Umbraco ecommerce package">Download Tea Commerce for your next Umbraco ecommerce project</a></p> 
        <h2>A true Umbraco ecommerce</h2> 
        <p>Tea Commerce is&nbsp;<strong>a true Umbraco ecommerce</strong>&nbsp;package that is fully integrated into&nbsp;<a href="http://umbraco.com/" target="_blank">Umbraco</a>. Tea Commerce builds on the strong and great principles of Umbraco and once you know these, you know Tea Commerce. See this list of&nbsp;<a href="/products/tea-commerce/" title="Tea Commerce">detailed feature descriptions</a>.</p> 
        <p>To create a modern webshop it requires that the designer has complete freedom. With Tea Commerce it's possible to create&nbsp;<em>interactive ecommerce solutions</em>&nbsp;of tomorrow. Our Umbraco ecommerce uses the latest JavaScript techniques which gives designers and developers the creative space they need.</p> 
        <p>If your Umbraco ecommerce solution requires features that Tea Commerce doesn't offer out of the box, it is easy to extend these through the well&nbsp;<a href="#" title="Documentation">documented</a>&nbsp;API. The API gives you the possibility to expand the cart process, order flow, specific business rules, etc.</p> 
        <h2>Get inspired by others work</h2> 
        <p>With Umbraco and Tea Commerce it is possible to created a variety of different looking ecommerce sites. Take a look at the&nbsp;<a href="/customers/" title="Customers">showcases</a>&nbsp;to get a glimse of how others have used Umbraco as their ecommerce platform.</p> 
        <h2>Using existing Umbraco packages for your webshop</h2> 
        <p>Tea Commerce is build using the Umbraco API and&nbsp;therefore 100%&nbsp;<span class="hps">compatible</span>&nbsp;<span class="hps">with</span>&nbsp;all the great community created&nbsp;<span class="hps">packages</span>. Below you'll find ideas of how you could use different Umbraco modules to create a&nbsp;<em>unique Umbraco ecommerce</em>&nbsp;solution.</p> 
        <ul> 
         <li>XSLTSearch - Product search</li> 
         <li>CMSImport - Import products</li> 
         <li>Universal Media Picker - Product images from external datasources - Amazon, Flickr etc.</li> 
         <li>uTube - Video presentation of a product</li> 
         <li>ImageProcessor&nbsp;- Have one large product image and generate infinite image sizes&nbsp;on-the-fly</li> 
        </ul> 
        <p>It's only the imagination that sets limits on what should be possible with your next Umbraco ecommerce solution. See the complete list of&nbsp;<a href="http://our.umbraco.org/projects" target="_blank" title="Umbraco packages">packages for Umbraco</a>.</p>
