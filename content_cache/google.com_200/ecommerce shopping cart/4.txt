              <p>I know that this question was asked before. But I see the responses are quite dated (at least in comparison to the pace we move). </p> 
              <p>So I am asking same question again.</p> 
              <p>We are about to start a ground up project to build a multi-tenant platform which will support basic e-commerce (will extend later) and basic CMS for feeding the catalogues. As this is targeted to a huge focus market and expected hit on certain event would go up to 20K (concurrent), we are moving away from platform like magento and thinking about building our own. </p> 
              <p>As per today's date the only "silver bullet" is node.js (pun intended) - so we plan to use node in backend, html5/KO in front end and still debating between mongoDB and MySQL. Even beta or alpha will do so that it gives us a start.</p>
