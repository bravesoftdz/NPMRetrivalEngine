        </table> <h3>E-Commerce</h3> <p><font size="2">There are great advantages to offering your products for sale on the internet.&nbsp; Using an ecommerce package allows you to select the features you need for presenting your products and fulfilling orders.</font></p> <p><font size="2"><b>Shopping Cart</b><br> </font><img border="0" src="images/cart.gif" align="left" width="78" height="91"><font size="2">The standard for ecommerce websites is the shopping cart technology.&nbsp; This is a software program that allows your visitor to shop through your catalog of items, while placing some in a "cart".&nbsp; This virtual cart keeps track of the items, their options and prices until the shopper is ready to check out.&nbsp; The software then compiles an order form and asks the purchaser information about payment methods, shipping, etc.</font></p> <p><font size="2">If your online store is likely to sell multiple items to each shopper, then a shopping cart program is almost a requirement.&nbsp; The advantage is that it makes the purchase process painless for the customer.&nbsp; This in turn encourages more sales.</font></p> <p><font size="2"><b>Order Form Shopping</b><br> <img border="0" src="images/ssl.gif" align="right" alt="Secure Sockel Layers Available" width="46" height="49">An html method of ecommerce is to create an order form that is linked to the display pages for your products.&nbsp; This enables a purchaser to find the product they are interested in, and then complete the form to make the purchase.&nbsp; The advantage is lower development cost.&nbsp; The disadvantage is that it generally requires the purchaser to remember the description of the product and price so they can fill the order form out properly.</font></p> <p><font size="2"><b>JavaScript Cart</b><br> <img border="0" src="images/rims.jpg" align="left" width="200" height="144">Java Script is a computer language that is supported by all the newer browsers, and because of that it will work for the vast majority (although not all) of your visitors.&nbsp; A java script cart is an html web page with extra programming to do calculations and capture the purchasers personal information.&nbsp; For a small list of items, or ones that rarely change in price or description, the java script cart can be an effective tool.</font></p> <p><font size="2"><b>Affiliate Agreements</b><br> In order to attract, track and pay affiliates, the shopping cart software you use must be enabled to establish affiliates and then report activities on their behalf.&nbsp; This is important technology as more ecommerce sites are using affiliates to help them generate traffic.&nbsp; The program we use for this is called <b>Commission Cart</b> and is available for just a few hundred dollars.&nbsp; In addition to the purchase of a license with <b>ComCart</b> - there are installation and customization costs.&nbsp; Talk to us about your product to get an idea of what an affiliate program would cost to get set up.</font></p> <p><font size="2"><b>Deciding What's Best For You</b><br> Let us give you a recommendation for how to equip your ecommerce website.&nbsp;
