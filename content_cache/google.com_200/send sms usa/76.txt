    <h1 class="titleLarge">Your TI CC3220 can send SMS on demand</h1> 
    <hr> 
    <p>We'll show you how to get your CC3220 to send SMS with Twilio. You can set up your board to send text message alerts about the temperature at your house, the status of a package or anything that's important to you.</p> 
    <p>This code uses our <a href="/library/Library/Twilio/SMSMessages/SendSMS/" target="_blank">Twilio &gt; SMSMessages &gt; SendSMS</a> Choreo. </p>
    <h3 class="subtitle">Get Set Up</h3> 
    <p class="num-body"><span class="ord">1</span>Make sure you have a Temboo account. If you don't already have one, you can <a href="/signup" target="_blank">register for free</a>.</p> 
    <p class="num-body"><span class="ord">2</span>You'll need a free Texas Instruments account to use their online IDE, CCS Cloud. You can sign up <a href="https://my.ti.com" target="_blank">here</a>.</p> 
    <p class="num-body"><span class="ord">3</span>If you haven't already done so, prepare your board for making secure connections by following our <a href="/hardware/ti/setting-up-cc3220-for-ssl" target="_blank">Setting up CC3220 for SSL</a> tutorial.</p> 
    <p class="num-body"><span class="ord">4</span>You'll also need a Twilio account, which you can create for free <a href="https://www.twilio.com" target="_blank">here</a>.</p> 
    <p class="num-body"><span class="ord">5</span>When you register with Twilio, you'll be given a Twilio phone number, along with the <strong>Account SID</strong> and <strong>Auth Token</strong>. Make sure to use the Account SID and Auth Token from your <a href="https://www.twilio.com/console" target="_blank">Twilio Console Dashboard</a>, shown below, as opposed to the test credentials from the Dev Tools panel.</p> 
    <p class="num-body"><img src="/assets/ti-screen-twiliodashboard.png" alt="The Twilio Console Dashboard" pagespeed_url_hash="591898181" onload="pagespeed.CriticalImages.checkImageForCriticality(this);"></p> 
    <p class="num-body"><em>A Twilio Account SID and Auth Token in the Twilio Console Dashboard</em></p> 
    <p class="num-body"><span class="ord">6</span>When using a free Twilio account, you'll need to verify the phone number to which messages are being sent by going to <a href="https://www.twilio.com" target="_blank">Twilio</a> and following the instructions under the "Numbers &gt; Verified Caller IDs" tab.</p> 
    <h3 class="subtitle">Auto-Generate your CC3220 Code</h3> 
    <p class="num-body"><span class="ord">7</span>Go to the <a href="/library/Library/Twilio/SMSMessages/SendSMS/" target="_blank">Twilio &gt; SMSMessages &gt; SendSMS</a> Choreo in our Library.</p> 
    <p class="num-body"><span class="ord">8</span>Select <strong>Texas Instruments</strong> from the drop down menu, then choose your board. Make sure that you've added details about how your CC3220 is connected to the internet.</p> 
    <p class="num-body"><img src="/assets/ti-ccs-screen-language-selector.png" alt="Selecting the Texas Instruments CC3220" pagespeed_url_hash="857065431" onload="pagespeed.CriticalImages.checkImageForCriticality(this);"></p> 
    <p class="num-body"><span class="ord">9</span>Fill in the required Choreo inputs, then click <strong>Generate Code</strong> to test out the Choreo from our website and confirm that you can send an SMS to your own phone.</p> 
    <p class="num-body"><img src="/assets/ti-screen-twilio-test.png" alt="Example inputs to the Twilio Send SMS Choreo" pagespeed_url_hash="1467433676" onload="pagespeed.CriticalImages.checkImageForCriticality(this);"></p> 
    <p class="num-body"><em>Testing the SendSMS Choreo from our website</em></p> 
    <p class="num-body"><span class="ord">10</span>When you've confirmed that the Choreo runs successfully and you've received an SMS, scroll down to the <strong>CODE</strong> section. Click the "Send to CCS Cloud" button to the right of your generated code. Name your application and click <strong>Send</strong>. Click the link that appears to visit the CCS Cloud IDE in your browser.</p> 
    <p class="num-body"><img src="/assets/ti-send-to-ccs-cloud.gif" alt="Sending code from Temboo to CCS Cloud" pagespeed_url_hash="802783697" onload="pagespeed.CriticalImages.checkImageForCriticality(this);"></p> 
    <h3 class="subtitle">Run The Code</h3> 
    <p class="num-body"><span class="ord">11</span>Plug your CC3220 board into your computer via USB. Select its USB connection in CCS Cloud via the <strong>Target &gt; Connect COM port...</strong> menu.</p> 
    <p class="num-body"><span class="ord">12</span>Now run the code on your CC3220 from CCS Cloud to start sending SMS from your CC3220. Open the Serial Monitor in and you'll see that the text has been sent successfully. Text away!</p> 
    <p class="num-body"><img src="/assets/ccs-cloud-run-code.gif" alt="Running your code on your CC3220 board from CCS Cloud" pagespeed_url_hash="48917096" onload="pagespeed.CriticalImages.checkImageForCriticality(this);"></p> 
    <p><strong>Note</strong>: Twilio doesn't yet support sending SMS from numbers for all countries. Here's a <a href="https://www.twilio.com/help/faq/phone-numbers/which-countries-does-twilio-have-phone-numbers-in-and-what-are-their-capabilities" target="_blank">full list</a> of the countries they currently have SMS support for.</p>
