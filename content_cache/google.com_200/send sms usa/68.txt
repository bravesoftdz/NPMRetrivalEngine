             <article class="faq">
              <ul class="checklist"> 
               <li class="checklist-item">Make phone calls and surf at the lowest roaming charges in over 180 countries:<br>from CHF 0.14 / minute and CHF 0.10 / MB in any EU country plus Switzerland, Liechtenstein and Norway</li>

             <p class="paragraph">SWISS Mobile works with a <strong>UK phone number (+44)</strong>in any <strong>non-SIM-locked standard mobile phone</strong> and any tablet with a SIM card slot. The SIM card is available in the three general sizes standard-SIM, micro-SIM and nano-SIM.</p>

             <!--{groups:ALL}-->
             <h2 class="h2">Buy</h2>
             <!--{endgroups}-->
             <!--{groups:ALL}-->
             <p class="paragraph">The SWISS Mobile SIM card costs CHF 40.00 if you order online or CHF 29.00 if you make your purchase on-board SWISS flights from the SWISS Shop. Both prices include a starting credit of CHF 20.-. You can also pre-order your SWISS Mobile SIM card through our Duty Free Pre-Order Service and take delivery of it on your next flight.<br></p>

             <!--{groups:ALL}-->

             <!--{endgroups}-->
             <!--{groups:ALL}-->

             <!--{endgroups}-->
             <!--{groups:ALL}-->
             <p class="paragraph"><a class="link icon-after" href="https://swiss-mobile.swiss.com/myaccount/addsim?l=en" target="_blank">Activate SIM card</a></p>

             <!--{endgroups}-->
             <!--{groups:ALL}-->

             <p class="paragraph">Because your SWISS Mobile SIM card can be charged with several country profiles, your mobile phone will be recognised as a local mobile by networks in other countries. In countries where this is not yet possible, the SIM card will use call-back to reduce your roaming charges.As the user, you do not have to do anything - it all happens automatically.</p>
             <!--{endgroups}-->

For your own safety and as a control mechanism, set a limit for the amount that will automatically be charged to your credit card.</p>
