       <p class="roboto-light" style="font-size: 14px;">We offer free shipping on all U.S. orders, $5.95 flat rate to Canada and $6.95 flat rate to Australia free exchanges provided for both. Orders are usually processed and shipped within <strong>1-2 business days</strong> (M-F) of purchase. When your order ships, we will contact you with a shipping confirmation and tracking information. Tracking information is also accessible through your account, under <a href="/sales/order/history">My Orders</a>.</p>

       <p class="roboto-light" style="clear: both; padding: 15px 0; border-bottom: 1px solid #edf0f2; font-size: 20px;">Free Exchanges To The U.S, Canada &amp; Australia</p> 
       <p class="roboto-light" style="font-size: 14px;">Request your exchange and <strong>process it on the spot!</strong> You will immediately receive a voucher for the exchange order. Free tracking is included for all U.S. orders. We recommend Canadian shoppers purchase insurance for all shipments. Exchanged orders must be returned to our warehouse <strong>within 30 days</strong>, or pay for the exchange voucher in full. Final sale items may be exchanged for the same style, different size while stock lasts.</p> 
       <p class="roboto-light" style="padding: 15px 0; border-bottom: 1px solid #edf0f2; font-size: 20px;">Easy returns</p> 
       <p class="roboto-light" style="font-size: 14px;">Returns are easy and convenient! Free tracking is included for all addresses within the U.S. We recommend Canadian &amp; Australian shoppers purchase insurance for all shipments. Each returned set is subject to a $5.95 restocking fee. Final sale items are not eligible for returns.</p> 
       <div style="padding-left: 50px;"> 
        <p class="roboto-light" style="font-size: 14px;"><strong>1.</strong> Make sure to ship back your <strong>entire set</strong> in its new, unworn condition* <strong>within 30 days of delivery</strong>. Sets received 30 days after delivery, or incomplete sets will not be accepted.</p> 
        <p class="roboto-light" style="font-size: 14px;"><strong>2.</strong> Complete your exchange &amp; return form available <a href="/awrma/customer_rma/new/">Here</a> or under <a href="/awrma/customer_rma/new/">My Orders</a>. Australian customers please contact <span style="color: #993300;"><strong>Australia@adoreme.com</strong></span></p> 
        <p class="roboto-light" style="font-size: 14px;"><strong>3.</strong> Repack items in their <strong>original packaging</strong> and seal the box securely with tape. If your poly-mailer is not reusable, simply send your items in the Adore Me box.</p> 
        <p class="roboto-light" style="font-size: 14px;"><strong>4.</strong> Please <strong>print your pre-paid Return Label</strong> and affix to your original package. You must cover or remove any original shipping information. If not, your package might be lost or rejected by the warehouse.</p> 
        <p class="roboto-light" style="font-size: 14px;"><strong>5.</strong> Schedule a pickup, give the package to your mailman, drop it in your mailbox, at work or at a post office without waiting in line. Please do not return packages to self-serve drop boxes as this may result in delays. Allow 30 days for us to process your request upon receipt.</p> 
        <p class="roboto-light" style="font-size: 12px;"><strong><span style="color: #cb213d;">Note</span>:</strong> Again, <strong>Adore Me does not offer any tracking services on labels for exchanges or returns from Canada and Australia</strong>, and is not responsible for lost packages. Customers are welcome to purchase alternative shipping methods that allow for tracking or insurance on the package.</p> 
        <p class="roboto-light" style="font-size: 12px;"><em><span style="color: #cb213d;">*</span>&nbsp;<span>For sanitary reasons, we are unable to accept items where the garment tag</span><br><span>has been cut or removed, as well as panties and swimsuits where the</span><br><span>protective adhesive strip had been removed. Thus Items not in new or unworn</span><br><span>condition will be rejected during the sanitary inspection.</span></em></p>
