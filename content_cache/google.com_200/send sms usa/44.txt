      <h2>High Level Hardware API<span style="font-size: 0.75em">s</span></h2>
      <h3>Build your application in minutes.</h3>
      <p>Tessel 2 runs JavaScript and supports NPM (the Node package manager)— that's HTTP, Twitter, web server, color, and async right out of the box.</p>
      <h4 class="preserve-3d text--monospace push__top">
       <div class="instructional">
        Install
       </div><span class="fa fa-angle-right red"></span><span class="red js-npm-install-tessel cursor-blink disabled">npm install t2-cli -g</span></h4>
      <h4 class="preserve-3d text--monospace">
       <div class="instructional">
        Add Library
       </div><span class="fa fa-angle-right hide js-angle red"></span><span class="red js-npm-install cursor-blink disabled">npm install ambient-attx4</span> <span class="red js-npm-install-module cursor-blink disabled"></span>&nbsp;</h4>
      <h4 class="preserve-3d text--monospace push__bottom">
       <div class="instructional">
        Get Online

    <h2>FAQ</h2>
   </div>
   <div class="column large-10 large-centered">
    <ul id="faq">
     <li>
      <div class="question">
       What’s changing with Tessel 2?
      </div>
      <!-- #[span.question-arrow]-->
      <div id="faq0" class="content">
       New features on Tessel 2 include extremely reliable WiFi, an ethernet jack, two USB ports, and a system that runs real Node.js/io.js.
       <p>We’ve also added support for scaling up: board customization options, and services for embedding Tessel in a product.</p>
      </div></li>
     <li>
      <div class="question">
       When will my Tessel 2 ship?
      </div>
      <!-- #[span.question-arrow]-->
      <div id="faq1" class="content">
       Tessel 2 is currently shipping! You can order T2s for immediate delivery from 
       <a href="http://www.seeedstudio.com/depot/Tessel-m-153.html">Seeed</a>.
      </div></li>
     <li>
      <div class="question">
       What modules are available/compatible?
      </div>
      <!-- #[span.question-arrow]-->
      <div id="faq2" class="content">
       Check out our 
       <a href="/modules">modules page!</a>
      </div></li>
     <li>
      <div class="question">
       How is Tessel 2 different from other development boards?
      </div>
      <!-- #[span.question-arrow]-->
      <div id="faq3" class="content">
       Tessel 2 is designed for the fastest possible path to production. Plug and play modules and high-level APIs set it apart from most other development boards. Tessel doesn't expect you to set up and maintain a Linux system or track down pinout diagrams to wire up sensors (though the root shell and GPIO access are available should you desire them).
      </div></li>
     <li>
      <div class="question">
       Can I try out the Tessel platform?
      </div>
      <!-- #[span.question-arrow]-->
      <div id="faq4" class="content">
       Absolutely! You can find in-person communities hacking on Tessels on our 
       <a href="/community">Community</a> page.
      </div></li>
     <li>
      <div class="question">
       Can I use my own USB devices?
      </div>
      <!-- #[span.question-arrow]-->
      <div id="faq5" class="content">
       Yes. First-party modules will have high-level APIs and be tested to ensure driver support, but you can also add your own USB devices if the drivers are compatible and/or you dive a little deeper into the OpenWrt ecosystem.
      </div></li>
     <li>
      <div class="question">
       Is Tessel a start-up?
      </div>
      <!-- #[span.question-arrow]-->
      <div id="faq6" class="content">
       No– in fact, we're not a company at all. Originally, Tessel was produced by a start-up called Technical Machine, but it has since grown into an independent, all-volunteer organization called the 
       <a href="//github.com/tessel/project">Tessel Project</a>.
      </div></li>
     <li>
      <div class="question">
       I have more questions! How can I contact you?
      </div>
      <!-- #[span.question-arrow]-->
      <div id="faq7" class="content">
       For quick questions, we're 
       <a href="//twitter.com/tesselproject">@tesselproject</a> on Twitter. Longer questions are welcome on our 
       <a href="//tessel.io/forums">forums</a>. Questions about orders should be directed to Seeed Studio.
       <p></p>
      </div></li>
