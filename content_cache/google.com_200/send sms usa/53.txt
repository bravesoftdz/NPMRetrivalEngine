        <h4>Channel Islands, Isle of Man and Republic of Ireland charges</h4> 
        <p></p>
        <p>Please note, calls and texts to the Channel Islands, including Jersey and Guernsey and the Isle of Man are not included in your monthly allowance, and will be charged at International Zone 3 rates. </p>
        <p>Calls and texts to the Republic of Ireland are also not included in your standard monthly allowance, and will be charged at International Zone 1 (EU) rates. </p>
        <p>All calls are charged at the standard rate per minute, with a 1 minute minimum call charge.</p>
