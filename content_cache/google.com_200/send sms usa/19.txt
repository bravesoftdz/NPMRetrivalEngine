See <a href="/english/service/world/roaming/content/sms/index.html#smsAnnka01">International Settings</a> for information on how to disable the Reject SMS option.</li>

          <div class="bluept01"> 
           <h3 class="title">How to send International SMS messages</h3> 
          </div> 
         </div> 
         <!-- / --> 
         <p class="txt">?Display the Compose SMS message screen ?</p> 
         <p><img src="images/sms_pic_02.gif" alt="Enter the recipient's phone number (Hold down the &quot;0&quot; for over one second to display &quot;+&quot; + Country code + Mobile phone number of the recipient (if the recipient's mobile phone number begins with zero, exclude the zero when dialing.(Except for some countries and regions.) > Enter the text Up to 160 half-size alphanumeric characters *Up to 70 full-size characters > Send the message" width="648" height="217"></p> 
         <ul class="notice"> 
          <li><img src="/english/images/common/blet_notice.gif" alt="notice" class="icon" height="7" width="7">The procedure to display "+" depends on the handset. Please check the user's manual for details.</li> 
          <li><img src="/english/images/common/blet_notice.gif" alt="notice" class="icon" height="7" width="7">With some operators, such as those in the United States, China, South Korea, and the Philippines, the SMS Delivery Notice will be sent as soon as the message reaches the message center of the destination operator (charges will not apply).</li> 
         </ul>
