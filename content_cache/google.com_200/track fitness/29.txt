      <p class="title">Websites</p> 
      <p class="body">I'm a web and database programmer with particular experience in e-commerce, site administration and content management. In the course of my work, I can help with any of the following:</p> 
      <ul class="body"> 
       <li>Share my experience with online business models, feature lists and launch plans</li> 
       <li>Plot your site functionality in detail, answering all technical questions along the way</li> 
       <li>Build unique functionality from scratch to support your business ideas</li> 
       <li>Integrate open-source software as appropriate for common functionality</li> 
       <li>Connect your website to payment processing, communications, analytics or other services</li> 
       <li>Add a content management interface so you can edit website content yourself</li> 
       <li>Implement a graphic designer's vision for the look and feel of the site</li> 
       <li>Install your website at a capable and reliable hosting company</li> 
      </ul> 
      <p class="body">My preferred development technologies are:</p> 
      <ul class="body"> 
       <li> Linux, Apache, MySQL and PHP (LAMP) on the back end</li> 
       <li> HTML, CSS and standard JavaScript on the front end</li> 
       <li>My own specialized tools for <a href="http://www.arlomedia.com/software/contemplate/" target="_blank">web templating</a>, <a href="http://www.arlomedia.com/software/meditate/" target="_blank">content editing</a> and <a href="http://www.mgtsuite.com/" target="_blank">project management</a></li> 
      </ul>
