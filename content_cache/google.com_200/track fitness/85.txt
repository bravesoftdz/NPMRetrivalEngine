        <p>Medway Park offers an affordable way to stay in shape, look great and get active. There are various options to suit your budget. Go straight to the <a href="http://www.medwaypark.org.uk/activities_facilities/echoes_gym/membership_prices/">gym membership price list</a>, the <a href="http://www.medwaypark.org.uk/medwaypark_membership/types_prices/">full centre membership price list</a> or see the <a href="../medwaypark_membership/types_prices/">full price list page</a> to find out more.</p> 
        <p>If you're not sure you want to commit to a membership package or if you just want more information, <a href="../medway_park/contact/">send us an email</a>. </p>
