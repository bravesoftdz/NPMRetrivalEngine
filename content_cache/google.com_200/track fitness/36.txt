            <span property="dc:title" content="Campus Activity Routes" class="rdf-meta element-hidden"></span>

             <div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden">
              <div class="field-items">
               <div class="field-item even" property="content:encoded">
                <div class="accordion">
                 Routes
                </div> 
                <div class="accordion_content"> 
                 <p>The campus of the University of Kansas is one of the most beautiful campuses of any university. We encourage and invite you to enjoy this beauty while getting some exercise in the process. Listed below are a variety of paths for campus users to get some physical activity. You can also click on <a href="/sites/recreation.ku.edu/files/docs/Activity%20Routes%202014%202.pdf">Campus Activity Routes (PDF) </a>to gain access to all of the maps. Or, if you prefer one specific route, you may click on one of the links below for that route. Enjoy the scenery and the exercise!</p> 
                 <p><a href="/sites/recreation.ku.edu/files/docs/Baby%20Jay%20Route.pdf">Baby Jay Route (PDF)</a></p> 
                 <p><a href="/sites/recreation.ku.edu/files/docs/Daisy%20Hill%20Route.pdf">Daisy Hill Route&nbsp;(PDF)</a></p> 
                 <p><a href="/sites/recreation.ku.edu/files/docs/Hawk%20Route.pdf">*Hawk Route&nbsp;(PDF)</a></p> 
                 <p><a href="/sites/recreation.ku.edu/files/docs/Jayhawk%20Blvd%20Route.pdf">*Jayhawk Boulevard Route&nbsp;(PDF)</a></p> 
                 <p><a href="/sites/recreation.ku.edu/files/docs/Jayhawk%20Route.pdf">Jayhawk Route&nbsp;(PDF)</a></p> 
                 <p><a href="/sites/recreation.ku.edu/files/docs/Memorial%20Stadium%20Route.pdf">Memorial Stadium Route&nbsp;(PDF)</a></p> 
                 <p><a href="/sites/recreation.ku.edu/files/docs/Phog%20Route.pdf">*Phog Route&nbsp;(PDF)</a></p> 
                 <p><a href="/sites/recreation.ku.edu/files/docs/Rock%20Chalk%20Route.pdf">*Rock Chalk Route&nbsp;(PDF)</a></p> 
                 <p><a href="/sites/recreation.ku.edu/files/docs/Sunflower%20Route.pdf">Sunflower Route&nbsp;(PDF)</a></p> 
                 <p><a href="/sites/recreation.ku.edu/files/docs/West%20Campus%20Route.pdf">*West Campus Route&nbsp;(PDF)</a></p> 
                 <p><em>* Denotes routes that are more accessible than the others - they have very few inclines, if any, and are more level routes.</em></p> 
                 <p>The following students contributed to the design of these routes:</p>

Now, get up and get moving while enjoying KUs scenic campus!</p> 
                 <p>* Wheeling is defined as people using wheelchairs, scooters, strollers, and/or bicycles. Please no skateboards.</p>
