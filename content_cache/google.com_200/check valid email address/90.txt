       <p class="post-description">As a owner or web master you wouldn't really want someone anonymously utilizing your website or campaign benefits.        <p><img src="http://www.idiotinside.com/static/uploads/2015/07/temporary-email-address-validator.png"></p> 
       <p>There are lot of temporary email services which helps to use anonymous email addresses. When you are registering in a new website you may not provide your real email address because you did not have confidence on the website is going to use your email address. So as a user temporary email addresses are good for you.</p> 
       <h2 id="why-temporary-disposable-email-address-validator">Why Temporary / Disposable Email Address Validator?</h2> 
       <p>As a owner or web master you wouldn’t really want someone anonymously utilizing your website or campaign benefits. So You really want to prevent using temporary email addresses on website. So this plugin was created in an attempt to make a collection of disposable email addresses and validate signups against with the list of temporary email providers.</p>
