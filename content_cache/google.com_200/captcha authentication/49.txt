     <h2 class="layout-post__title"> Our top 10 favorite WordPress plugins </h2> 
     <p class="layout-post__meta"> May 18, 2016 by <a href="https://getflywheel.com/layout/author/morgan">Morgan Ryan</a> </p> 
     <p>Sifting through the WordPress Plugin Directory is a bit like standing in a candy store – they all look so exciting to try!&nbsp;And these days, there’s a plugin for just about everything: security enhancements, performance boosts, SEO optimization, and the list goes on and on.</p> 
     <p>Ultimately, the plugins you decide to install are completely up to you and your site’s needs.&nbsp;But to help you start sorting through the masses, we’ve compiled a list of our top 10 plugins. We’ve found all of these&nbsp;to be super simple to use, immensely useful when building sites, and just downright reliable.</p> 
     <p>So without further ado, here are our top 10 favorite WordPress plugins right now!</p> 
     <h2 class="p1"><span class="s1"><a href="https://wordpress.org/plugins/advanced-custom-fields/" target="_blank">Advanced Custom Fields</a>&nbsp;</span></h2> 
     <p><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-17032" src="http://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-advanced-custom-fields.jpg" alt="top-wordpress-plugins-advanced-custom-fields" width="710" height="228" srcset="https://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-advanced-custom-fields.jpg 710w, https://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-advanced-custom-fields-300x96.jpg 300w, https://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-advanced-custom-fields-157x50.jpg 157w, https://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-advanced-custom-fields-225x72.jpg 225w, https://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-advanced-custom-fields-296x95.jpg 296w, https://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-advanced-custom-fields-592x190.jpg 592w, https://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-advanced-custom-fields-171x55.jpg 171w, https://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-advanced-custom-fields-343x110.jpg 343w, https://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-advanced-custom-fields-276x89.jpg 276w, https://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-advanced-custom-fields-552x177.jpg 552w" sizes="(max-width: 710px) 100vw, 710px"></p> 
     <div class="page" title="Page 2"> 
      <div class="layoutArea"> 
       <div class="column"> 
        <blockquote>
         <p><em><strong>“Advanced Custom Fields is the perfect solution for any WordPress website which needs more flexible data like other Content Management Systems.”</strong></em></p>
        </blockquote> 
        <p>When you edit a post or page in WordPress, you’ll notice that out of the box, there aren’t a whole lot of options. For many sites, that’ll get the job done. If you’re building something a little more robust, however, you might want to add a little more functionality&nbsp;and a few more options. That’s where the <a href="https://wordpress.org/plugins/advanced-custom-fields/" target="_blank">Advanced Custom Fields</a> plugin comes in. It gives you access to lots of different data types and even allows you to create your own.</p> 
        <p>Especially if you’re developing sites for clients, this plugin is a great solution for keeping things organized and making pages and posts easy to edit.</p> 
        <h2 class="p2"><span class="s2"><a href="https://wordpress.org/plugins/bwp-minify/" target="_blank">Better WordPress Minify</a></span></h2> 
        <blockquote>
         <p><em><strong>“Allows you to combine and minify your CSS and JS files to improve page load time.”</strong></em></p>
        </blockquote> 
        <p>Looking for a little performance boost? When building sites, it’s important to try to serve the fewest amount of assets possible, compressed as much as you can, in order to make your site as fast as possible. <a href="https://wordpress.org/plugins/bwp-minify/" target="_blank">Better WordPress Minify</a> will help you&nbsp;by combining and minifying your CSS and JavaScript files. Plus, it’s got some great documentation and a fantastic list of <a href="http://betterwp.net/wordpress-minify-javascript-css/" target="_blank">best practices</a> when it comes to minification.</p> 
        <p>It’s a super useful plugin if you’re looking to speed up your site and increase performance for your users.</p> 
       </div> 
      </div> 
     </div> 
     <h2 class="p1"><a href="https://wordpress.org/plugins/captcha/" target="_blank"><span class="s1">Captcha by BestWebSoft</span></a></h2> 
     <p><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-17041" src="http://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-captcha-by-bestwebsoft.jpg" alt="top-wordpress-plugins-captcha-by-bestwebsoft" width="710" height="227" srcset="https://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-captcha-by-bestwebsoft.jpg 710w, https://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-captcha-by-bestwebsoft-300x96.jpg 300w, https://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-captcha-by-bestwebsoft-157x50.jpg 157w, https://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-captcha-by-bestwebsoft-225x72.jpg 225w, https://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-captcha-by-bestwebsoft-297x95.jpg 297w, https://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-captcha-by-bestwebsoft-594x190.jpg 594w, https://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-captcha-by-bestwebsoft-172x55.jpg 172w, https://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-captcha-by-bestwebsoft-344x110.jpg 344w, https://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-captcha-by-bestwebsoft-276x88.jpg 276w, https://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-captcha-by-bestwebsoft-552x176.jpg 552w" sizes="(max-width: 710px) 100vw, 710px"></p> 
     <blockquote>
      <p><em><strong>“It protects your website from spammers by means of math logic, easily understandable by human beings.”</strong></em></p>
     </blockquote> 
     <p>It’s pretty disappointing (and annoying) to think you’ve received an email from a new client only to find it’s some lame&nbsp;spam email. <a href="https://wordpress.org/plugins/captcha/" target="_blank">Captcha by BestWebSoft</a>&nbsp;will help you eliminate&nbsp;that nonsense by adding a simple Captcha form to your site. It uses basic&nbsp;math logic (addition, subtraction, or multiplication) to stop spammers while remaining easily understood by your human visitors.</p> 
     <p>If you’re receiving a bunch of bogus spam emails or nonsense comments on your site, this plugin is a quick and efficient way to clean up the clutter.</p> 
     <h2 class="p1"><a href="https://wordpress.org/plugins/wpclef/" target="_blank"><span class="s1">Clef Two-Factor Authentication</span></a></h2> 
     <p><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-17042" src="http://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-clef-two-factor-authentication.jpg" alt="top-wordpress-plugins-clef-two-factor-authentication" width="710" height="228" srcset="https://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-clef-two-factor-authentication.jpg 710w, https://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-clef-two-factor-authentication-300x96.jpg 300w, https://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-clef-two-factor-authentication-157x50.jpg 157w, https://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-clef-two-factor-authentication-225x72.jpg 225w, https://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-clef-two-factor-authentication-296x95.jpg 296w, https://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-clef-two-factor-authentication-592x190.jpg 592w, https://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-clef-two-factor-authentication-171x55.jpg 171w, https://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-clef-two-factor-authentication-343x110.jpg 343w, https://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-clef-two-factor-authentication-276x89.jpg 276w, https://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-clef-two-factor-authentication-552x177.jpg 552w" sizes="(max-width: 710px) 100vw, 710px"></p> 
     <blockquote>
      <p><em><strong>“Modern two-factor that people love to use: strong authentication without passwords or tokens; single sign on/off; magical user experience.”</strong></em></p>
     </blockquote> 
     <p>For those of you concerned about security, you probably already know that two-factor authentication is a great process to have in place. <a href="https://wordpress.org/plugins/wpclef/" target="_blank">Clef Two-Factor Authentication</a> takes that one step further by removing passwords – everything is done by using the super nifty <a href="https://getclef.com/tutorial/" target="_blank">Clef wave</a>.&nbsp;(Seriously, go try it out. It’s awesome!)</p> 
     <p>If you’re looking to boost security on your site (or just want to use a really awesome piece of technology), this plugin is definitely a must-install!</p> 
     <h2><a href="https://wordpress.org/plugins/jetpack/" target="_blank">Jetpac</a><a href="https://wordpress.org/plugins/jetpack/">k&nbsp;by WordPress.com</a></h2> 
     <p><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-17043" src="http://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-jetpack-by-wordpress.jpg" alt="top-wordpress-plugins-jetpack-by-wordpress" width="710" height="227" srcset="https://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-jetpack-by-wordpress.jpg 710w, https://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-jetpack-by-wordpress-300x96.jpg 300w, https://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-jetpack-by-wordpress-157x50.jpg 157w, https://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-jetpack-by-wordpress-225x72.jpg 225w, https://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-jetpack-by-wordpress-297x95.jpg 297w, https://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-jetpack-by-wordpress-594x190.jpg 594w, https://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-jetpack-by-wordpress-172x55.jpg 172w, https://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-jetpack-by-wordpress-344x110.jpg 344w, https://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-jetpack-by-wordpress-276x88.jpg 276w, https://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-jetpack-by-wordpress-552x176.jpg 552w" sizes="(max-width: 710px) 100vw, 710px"></p> 
     <blockquote>
      <p><em><strong>“Increase your traffic, view your stats, speed up your site, and protect yourself from hackers with Jetpack.”</strong></em></p>
     </blockquote> 
     <p>If you’re familiar with WordPress, you’ve probably heard of <a href="https://wordpress.org/plugins/jetpack/" target="_blank">Jetpack</a> already, and for good reasons. All around, it’s just a good, solid plugin with some great, basic functionality. With lots of features, it comes packed with options to optimize each and every WordPress site. And sometimes, it’s just nice to have all of those features in one place, instead of navigating several plugins.</p> 
     <p>If you want to add lots of functionality to your site with just one all-inclusive plugin, Jetpack is definitely the way to go.</p> 
     <h2 class="p1"><a href="https://wordpress.org/plugins/ninja-forms/" target="_blank"><span class="s1">Ninja Forms</span></a></h2> 
     <p><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-17044" src="http://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-ninja-forms.jpg" alt="top-wordpress-plugins-ninja-forms" width="710" height="228" srcset="https://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-ninja-forms.jpg 710w, https://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-ninja-forms-300x96.jpg 300w, https://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-ninja-forms-157x50.jpg 157w, https://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-ninja-forms-225x72.jpg 225w, https://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-ninja-forms-296x95.jpg 296w, https://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-ninja-forms-592x190.jpg 592w, https://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-ninja-forms-171x55.jpg 171w, https://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-ninja-forms-343x110.jpg 343w, https://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-ninja-forms-276x89.jpg 276w, https://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-ninja-forms-552x177.jpg 552w" sizes="(max-width: 710px) 100vw, 710px"></p> 
     <blockquote>
      <p><em><strong>“Ninja Forms is the easiest way to build any form you need for your WordPress website.&nbsp;Create the form you want, when you want with a simple drag and drop interface provided by the very powerful Ninja Forms framework.”</strong></em></p>
     </blockquote> 
     <p>If you’re collecting information from your users (which you should be!) forms are one of the most important&nbsp;aspects of your site. With a simple drag and drop solution, <a href="https://wordpress.org/plugins/ninja-forms/" target="_blank">Ninja Forms</a> is super easy to use and offers lots of great customizations.&nbsp;Plus, it offers several advanced options with a library of add-ons.</p> 
     <p>Ninja Forms is sure to help you increase conversions&nbsp;while simplifying form creation.</p> 
     <h2><a href="https://wordpress.org/plugins/p3-profiler/" target="_blank">P3 (Plugin Performance Profiler)</a></h2> 
     <p><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-17046" src="http://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-p3.jpg" alt="top-wordpress-plugins-p3" width="710" height="228" srcset="https://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-p3.jpg 710w, https://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-p3-300x96.jpg 300w, https://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-p3-157x50.jpg 157w, https://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-p3-225x72.jpg 225w, https://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-p3-296x95.jpg 296w, https://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-p3-592x190.jpg 592w, https://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-p3-171x55.jpg 171w, https://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-p3-343x110.jpg 343w, https://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-p3-276x89.jpg 276w, https://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-p3-552x177.jpg 552w" sizes="(max-width: 710px) 100vw, 710px"></p> 
     <blockquote>
      <p><em><strong>“See which plugins are slowing down your site. This plugin creates a performance report for your site.”</strong></em></p>
     </blockquote> 
     <p>Sometimes plugins do nothing but wonders for your site. Other times, they can slow things down and become a real pain. Curious if any of your plugins are causing problems? P3 will create a performance report for your site and help you discover any pesky plugins that are slowing down performance.</p> 
     <p>Especially if you have lots of plugins installed,&nbsp;P3 is a great auditing tool to make sure none of them are causing more problems than they’re worth.</p> 
     <h2><a href="https://wordpress.org/plugins/tiny-compress-images/" target="_blank">TinyPNG for WordPress</a></h2> 
     <p><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-17047" src="http://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-compress-jpeg-png-images.jpg" alt="top-wordpress-plugins-compress-jpeg-png-images" width="710" height="228" srcset="https://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-compress-jpeg-png-images.jpg 710w, https://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-compress-jpeg-png-images-300x96.jpg 300w, https://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-compress-jpeg-png-images-157x50.jpg 157w, https://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-compress-jpeg-png-images-225x72.jpg 225w, https://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-compress-jpeg-png-images-296x95.jpg 296w, https://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-compress-jpeg-png-images-592x190.jpg 592w, https://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-compress-jpeg-png-images-171x55.jpg 171w, https://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-compress-jpeg-png-images-343x110.jpg 343w, https://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-compress-jpeg-png-images-276x89.jpg 276w, https://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-compress-jpeg-png-images-552x177.jpg 552w" sizes="(max-width: 710px) 100vw, 710px"></p> 
     <blockquote>
      <p><em><strong>“Speed up your website. Optimize your JPEG and PNG images automatically with TinyPNG.”</strong></em></p>
     </blockquote> 
     <p>If your site includes lots of visual content, it’s critical that you optimize and compress images for the web. One problem with WordPress, however, is that even if you optimize the original image that you upload, when WordPress generates new image sizes from it, those won’t be compressed. <a href="https://wordpress.org/plugins/tiny-compress-images/" target="_blank">TinyPNG for WordPress</a> will take care of that for you, and even help you optimize images that you’ve already uploaded.</p> 
     <p>If you have a site with lots of images and things aren’t running quite as fast as you’d like, TinyPNG for WordPress is a fantastic solution.</p> 
     <h2 class="p1"><a href="https://wordpress.org/plugins/woocommerce/" target="_blank"><span class="s1">WooCommerce</span></a></h2> 
     <p><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-17049" src="http://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-woocommerce.jpg" alt="top-wordpress-plugins-woocommerce" width="710" height="229" srcset="https://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-woocommerce.jpg 710w, https://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-woocommerce-300x97.jpg 300w, https://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-woocommerce-157x51.jpg 157w, https://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-woocommerce-225x73.jpg 225w, https://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-woocommerce-295x95.jpg 295w, https://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-woocommerce-589x190.jpg 589w, https://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-woocommerce-171x55.jpg 171w, https://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-woocommerce-341x110.jpg 341w, https://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-woocommerce-276x89.jpg 276w, https://getflywheel.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/top-wordpress-plugins-woocommerce-552x178.jpg 552w" sizes="(max-width: 710px) 100vw, 710px"></p> 
     <blockquote>
      <p><em><strong>“WooCommerce is a powerful, extendable eCommerce plugin that helps you sell anything. Beautifully.”</strong></em></p>
     </blockquote> 
     <p>If you’re building an eCommerce site, <a href="https://wordpress.org/plugins/woocommerce/" target="_blank">WooCommerce</a> is definitely the way to go. With lots of options and customizations, it’s incredibly easy to build the shop of your dreams while&nbsp;also managing all of the logistics, right from one plugin. And if you’re looking for more functionality than just the free plugin, there are a plethora of premium extensions to add on.</p> 
     <p>For anyone looking to build an eCommerce site, WooCommerce is the plugin for you!</p>

Yoast SEO is here to do just that – help you optimize every post and page for maximum exposure. While you’re editing content, this plugin&nbsp;will help you preview how it’ll display in search engine results and also make suggestions to increase rankings.</p>
