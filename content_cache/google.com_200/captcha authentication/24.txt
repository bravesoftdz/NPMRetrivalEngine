         <h2>Creating Facebook App:</h2> 
         <p>Very first thing you going to need is <strong>AppID&nbsp;</strong>and <strong>AppSecret&nbsp;</strong>from Facebook App. Please go to <a title="Create facebook app" href="https://developers.facebook.com" target="_blank">Facebook Developers</a>&nbsp;and create your app.</p> 
         <p>Choose <strong>WWW&nbsp;</strong>as platform when it ask. Give the proper name and you can leave namespace field blank.</p> 
         <p><img class="aligncenter wp-image-534" src="https://i2.wp.com/104.131.231.142/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/app-2.png?resize=600%2C335" alt="app 2" srcset="https://i0.wp.com/codeforgeek.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/app-2.png?w=715&amp;ssl=1 715w, https://i0.wp.com/codeforgeek.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/app-2.png?resize=300%2C167&amp;ssl=1 300w" sizes="(max-width: 600px) 100vw, 600px" data-recalc-dims="1"></p> 
         <p>&nbsp;</p> 
         <p>It will ask for Captcha, enter it carefully and once done it will redirect you to app page. By authenticating your facebook account again you can view the <strong>App secret</strong>. Now go to <strong>Setting </strong>and add the Web as a platform.</p> 
         <p>If you want to test the app in <strong>localhost&nbsp;</strong>environment then add <strong>localhost:3000&nbsp;</strong>as an site address. We are done with the Facebook App creation.</p>
