           <h1> Metabolic Nutrition </h1> 
           <div class="lead">
             The BC Metabolic Nutrition Program provides specialized metabolic formulas and low protein foods to children and adults with inborn errors of metabolism. 
           </div> 
           <div class="tab-nav"> 
            <ul class="nav justify"> 
            </ul> 
           </div>

               About 
             </div> 
             <div id="ctl00_PlaceHolderMain_SubPlaceholder_ctl03_label" style="display:none">

             </div>
             <div id="ctl00_PlaceHolderMain_SubPlaceholder_ctl03__ControlWrapper_RichHtmlField" class="ms-rtestate-field" style="display:inline" aria-labelledby="ctl00_PlaceHolderMain_SubPlaceholder_ctl03_label">
              <p class="phsa-rteElement-Paragraph">We&nbsp;provide&nbsp;the metabolic formulas and low protein foods you need&nbsp;to help manage your inborn error of metabolism or metabolic condition.</p>
              <h2 class="phsa-rteElement-Header2">Metabolic formulas</h2>
              <p class="phsa-rteElement-Paragraph">Your physician and dietitian will let us know what specialty formula to give you. The formulas you need to manage your Inborn Error of Metabolism continue to be provided to BC residents free of charge.</p>
              <p class="phsa-rteElement-Paragraph">Metabolic formula and amino acids are provided to you by the BC Metabolic Nutrition Program (BCMNP). Phone or email us when you need to order more formula or amino acids. The&nbsp;formula&nbsp;you have been prescribed is specially ordered for you. We need 4 weeks' notice&nbsp;to fill most formula orders.</p>
              <h2 class="phsa-rteElement-Header2">Low protein foods</h2>
              <p class="phsa-rteElement-Paragraph">Your dietitian will talk to you if you need these special foods. </p>
              <p class="phsa-rteElement-Paragraph">BCMNP&nbsp;is partnered with National Food Distribution Centre to provide low protein foods. All the low protein foods you wish to purchase with your subsidy now come directly to you from our approved vendor, National Food Distribution Centre. This includes a wide variety of cold ship and shelf stable low protein foods. </p>
              <p class="phsa-rteElement-Paragraph">When&nbsp;you are ready to place your next food order phone National Food. The phone number is <strong>1-888-606-6676 ext. 225</strong>.&nbsp; You will speak directly with&nbsp;Lyne. The order desk is open from 6am - 9am and 10am - 2pm (PST). </p>
              <p class="phsa-rteElement-Paragraph">The order will be delivered right to your home. Dry or shelf stable foods should be received within one week. &nbsp;Cold ship or frozen foods are delivered by freezer truck from Montreal and then courier to your door,&nbsp;you should receive cold foods within 4 weeks from when you placed the order.</p>
              <p class="phsa-rteElement-Paragraph">The delivery method of cold ship foods has recently changed. <a href="/Nutrition-site/Documents/NFDC%20letter%20to%20BC%20patients%20Oct%202016.pdf">More information from National Food Distribution Centre can be found by clicking here</a>. &nbsp;</p>
              <p class="phsa-rteElement-Paragraph"><a href="/Nutrition-site/Documents/Low%20Protein%20Food%20List%20from%20NFDC%202017.06.pdf">A&nbsp;list of available foods can be found by clicking here.</a>&nbsp;The list of available foods will change as new products become available and other products are discontinued by the manufacturer. If you have questions about which foods are available, phone National Food and Josee or Lyne.&nbsp; They have the most up to date information on which foods are available.</p>
              <p class="phsa-rteElement-Paragraph"><a href="/Nutrition-site/Documents/BCMNP%20NFDC%20Low%20Protein%20Food%20Transition%20Info%20Sheet%202015%2009%2004%20FINAL.pdf">More information about&nbsp;National Food&nbsp;can be found here.</a>&nbsp;</p>
              <p class="phsa-rteElement-Paragraph">To provide this service, we will collect some personal information including your name and address and provide it to National Food Distribution Centre so they can create a personal profile and arrange for delivery. </p>
              <p class="phsa-rteElement-Paragraph">We collect your personal information as authorized under section 26(c) of the <em>BC Freedom of Information and Protection of Privacy Act ("FIPPA")</em> to provide metabolic formulas and low protein foods to you. We collect your name, address, telephone number and personal health number. If you have questions or concerns about how your information is collected or used, you may contact:</p>
              <p class="phsa-rteElement-Paragraph">Heather Lovelace, Professional Practice Leader for Nutrition Services (Program Administrator) </p>
              <p class="phsa-rteElement-Paragraph">BC Children's Hospital 4500 Oak Street, Vancouver BC, V6H 3N1<br>Phone: 604-875-2805&nbsp;</p>
              <h2 class="phsa-rteElement-Header2"></h2>
              <p class="phsa-rteElement-Paragraph"><span style="line-height:24px;">We&nbsp;</span><span style="line-height:24px;">are&nbsp;</span><span style="line-height:24px;">dedicated to meeting the&nbsp;</span><span style="line-height:24px;">needs&nbsp;of&nbsp;patients&nbsp;and&nbsp;families in the BC metabolic community.</span><span style="line-height:24px;">&nbsp;</span>Providing the best care to our patients and families remains our top priority. We want to hear from you.</p>
