       <p><strong><a title="Base%20Root%20%BB%20Abertay%20Dundee%20%BB%20Home%20%BB%20Courses%20%BB%20Undergraduate%20courses%20%BB%20Food%20%26%20Consumer%20Science" href="/studying/undergraduate/bsc-food-and-consumer-science/">BSc (Hons) Food &amp; Consumer Science</a> </strong></p> 
       <p><strong>BSc (Hons) Food, Nutrition &amp; Health <br><br></strong></p> 
       <p><strong>Why choose this course?<br></strong></p> 
       <ul> 
        <li>The two-year common core programme allows students to transfer between our degrees at the end of Year 2, in line with career aspirations.</li> 
        <li>Work placement in Year 3 provides opportunity to practice skills and test careers.</li> 
        <li>It is vocationally relevant, with emphasis throughout on development of practical skills in labs and kitchens as well as academic learning.</li> 
        <li>The programme content is influenced by industry contact and close working with&nbsp;<a href="https://www.foodinnovation.abertay.ac.uk/" target="_blank">Food Innovation @ Abertay</a>.</li> 
        <li>All programmes meet General Teaching Council requirements as a pre-requisite degree for Home Economics teaching.</li> 
       </ul> 
       <p>If food is your passion, be it nutrition, health or consumer science, then these are the programmes for you. &nbsp;The programmes are aimed to secondary school leavers, entrants from further education colleges and mature students who wish to pursue a career in a food related sector, or Home Economics teaching. &nbsp;</p> 
       <p>Careers range from roles in the food industry - the fastest growing manufacturing sector in the UK - such as food technologists, quality control and quality assurance managers and hygiene offers. &nbsp;Home economics teachers, nutritionists, community food workers, consumer advice officers and health promotion workers are other areas graduates can pursue careers.<br><br>Your graduate CV will be enhanced by your work experience placement and an honours project linked to your area of interest and your chosen career path. &nbsp;Vocationally relevant, you will develop practical skills in laboratories and kitchens, backed up by academic learning. &nbsp;You will be trained by experts in their field, with hands on experience in industry-linked projects in association with FoodInnovation@Abertay (FIA). &nbsp;FIA is a knowledge transfer platform based at Abertay, providing networking between students and the food industry.<br><br>On completion of this course, there is the potential to progress to the postgraduate programme, <a href="https://www.abertay.ac.uk/studying/postgraduate/msc-food-and-drink-innovation/" target="_blank" title="Base Root  Abertay LIVE  Home  Study Here  Studying  Postgraduate courses  Food &amp; Drink Innovation | Links to: Base Root  Abertay LIVE  Home  Study Here  Studying  Postgraduate courses  Food and Drink Innovation">MSc Food &amp; Drink Innovation</a>,&nbsp;which aims to train individuals for careers in the food and drink industry.</p>

     <div id="accordion-2" class="accordion-section-content"> 
      <p><strong>What you study</strong></p> 
      <p><strong></strong>You will learn the fundamentals of food science and nutrition, and develop practical professional food handling skills. &nbsp;You will learn about the nutritional requirements of the individual through the lifespan, and explore the role of food in public nutrition and health. &nbsp;You will understand what drives consumer food choices, and how to develop innovative healthy food products, ensuring they are safe for the consumer to eat.</p> 
      <p>For more information on the course content, download the&nbsp;<a href="/media/Programme information - BSc with Honours in Food, Nutrition and Health.pdf" title="Programme information - BSc with Honours in Food, Nutrition and Health" target="_blank">Programme information - BSc with Honours in Food, Nutrition and Health</a></p> 
      <p><strong>How you learn and are assessed</strong></p> 
      <p>A variety of teaching methods from traditional lectures to tutorials and case studies, drawing examples from industry linked projects, through collaboration with FoodInnovation@Abertay. The students have an opportunity to gain real life experience through the 10 week-long placement at Year 3, giving them the opportunity to put into practice their academic learning.</p> 
      <p>You will be assessed by coursework, class tests, online assessments, exam presentations, vivas and projects.&nbsp;</p>

      <p><strong></strong><strong>YEAR 1:<br>Higher</strong>&nbsp;BBBB;&nbsp;<strong>A Level</strong>&nbsp;CCC;&nbsp;<strong>Irish Highers</strong>&nbsp;H3H3H3H3;&nbsp;<strong>BTEC Extended Diploma&nbsp;</strong>MMM;&nbsp;<strong>International Baccalaureate&nbsp;</strong>28 Points<br><strong>Essential subjects</strong>: None?<br><strong>AHEAD</strong>&nbsp;- successful completion of the relevant stream of our&nbsp;<a target="_blank" href="/studying/undergraduate/access-to-higher-education-at-abertay-dundee/">AHEAD</a>&nbsp;programme<br><strong>SWAP&nbsp;</strong>- our&nbsp;<a href="/studying/fromcollege/entryfromcollege/">Coming from College</a>&nbsp;pages list approved SWAP courses<br><strong><br>YEAR 2:<br></strong><strong>Advanced Higher:&nbsp;</strong>BBB (to include Health and Food Technology or Home Economics and Biology or Chemistry)<br><strong>A Level:&nbsp;</strong>BBB (to include Home Economics or Food Studies and Biology or Chemistry)<br><strong>BTEC Extended Diploma</strong>&nbsp;- DDD Hospitality<br><strong>HNC/HND:</strong>&nbsp;our&nbsp;<a href="/studying/fromcollege/entryfromcollege/">Coming from College</a>&nbsp;pages list approved HNC/HND courses<br><br><strong>YEAR 3:<br></strong><strong>HND:</strong>&nbsp;our&nbsp;<a href="/studying/fromcollege/entryfromcollege/">Coming from College</a>&nbsp;pages list approved HND courses</p> 
      <p><strong>Not sure if you are eligible for entry?</strong></p> 
      <p>If you have the potential and motivation to study at university, regardless of your background or personal circumstances,&nbsp;<strong>we welcome your application</strong>.</p> 
      <p>We know that some people have faced extra&nbsp;challenges&nbsp;before applying to university and that is why we consider the background in which your academic grades have been achieved when making an offer.&nbsp;</p> 
      <p>If you expect to receive passes in&nbsp;<strong>3 Scottish Highers (grades A-C)&nbsp;</strong>and have:</p> 
      <ul> 
       <li>been in care, or</li> 
       <li>participated in a targeted aspiration-raising programme such as LIFT OFF, LEAPS, FOCUS West, or Aspire North</li> 
       <li>no family background of going to university, or</li> 
       <li>live in an area, or attend a school, where not many people go to university.</li> 
      </ul> 
      <p>We encourage you to submit an application.</p> 
      <p><strong>International Applicants<br></strong>You must provide certified proof of your English language level (minimum requirements are listed&nbsp;<a href="/studying/informationforinternationalstudents/english-requirements/">here</a>). &nbsp;Our British Council accredited&nbsp;<a href="/studying/informationforinternationalstudents/english-language-courses/iefc/">International English Foundation Course</a>&nbsp;can help you reach that level or, if you would like further develop your academic English,&nbsp;<a href="/studying/informationforinternationalstudents/english-language-courses/presessional-english/">Intensive English for Academic Purposes</a>&nbsp;can help you achieve your full potential.</p> 
      <p><em>EU applicants: &nbsp;</em>Entry requirements can be found on our&nbsp;<a href="/studying/informationforinternationalstudents/yourcountry/europe/">Europe pages</a>. &nbsp;<em>Overseas (non-EU) applicants: &nbsp;</em>We're currently developing your country pages, so please&nbsp;<a href="mailto:j.grant@abertay.ac.uk">email us</a>&nbsp;details of your entry requirements.</p> 
      <p><strong>Alternative Entrance Requirements<br></strong>Please&nbsp;<a href="mailto:admissions@abertay.ac.uk" target="_blank">email us</a>&nbsp;if you have other qualifications/experience not listed here.</p>
