     <p class="package-description-redundant">Third-party authentication plugin for <a href="https://github.com/hapijs/hapi">hapi</a>.</p> 
     <p>Lead Maintainer: <a href="https://github.com/ldesplat">Lois Desplat</a></p> 
     <p><a href="http://travis-ci.org/hapijs/bell"><img src="https://secure.travis-ci.org/hapijs/bell.png" alt="Build Status"></a></p> 
     <p><strong>bell</strong> ships with built-in support for authentication using <code>Facebook</code>, <code>GitHub</code>, <code>Google</code>, <code>Google Plus</code>, <code>Instagram</code>, <code>LinkedIn</code>, <code>Slack</code>, <code>Stripe</code>, <code>Twitter</code>, <code>Yahoo</code>, <code>Foursquare</code>, <code>VK</code>, <code>ArcGIS Online</code>, <code>Windows Live</code>, <code>Nest</code>, <code>Phabricator</code>, <code>BitBucket</code>, <code>Dropbox</code>, <code>Reddit</code>, <code>Tumblr</code>, <code>Twitch</code>, <code>Mixer</code>, <code>Salesforce</code>, <code>Pinterest</code>, <code>Discord</code>, <code>DigitalOcean</code>, and <code>Okta</code>. It also supports any compliant <code>OAuth 1.0a</code> and <code>OAuth 2.0</code> based login services with a simple configuration object.</p>
