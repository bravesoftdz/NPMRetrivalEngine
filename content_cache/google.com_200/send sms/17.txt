                  <p style="text-align: center;"><strong>NodeJs Sms Verification</strong></p> 
                  <ol> 
                   <li>We will Store user's information along with user's mobile number.</li> 
                   <li>Whenever a user tries to verify her/his mobile number we will initiate a request to SMS Gateway to send an SMS with randomly generated OTP.</li> 
                   <li>SMS Gateway will deliver the SMS with OTP to user's mobile number.</li> 
                   <li>Than OTP will be entered by user for verification and after verification, we will activate user's mobile number</li> 
                  </ol>
