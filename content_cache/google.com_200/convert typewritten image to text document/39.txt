        <p>Scanned PDF and documents containing embedded images can be converted to searchable and text selectable PDF documents through Optical Character Recognition.</p>

        <p>Documents can be digitally signed using x509 certificates such as .pfx files with private keys.</p>

        <p><a href="http://mvc.syncfusion.com/demos/web/pdf/barcode">View Demo</a></p>

        <p>Add, edit, and remove interactive elements such as attachments, annotations, actions, and bookmarks.</p> 
        <p><a href="http://mvc.syncfusion.com/demos/web/pdf/interactivefeatures">View Demo</a></p>

        <h3 id="extract-images-and-text-from-pdf">Extract Images and Text from PDF</h3> 
        <p>Text and embedded images in a document can be extracted and saved to disk. You can also preserve the layout of a PDF during text extraction.</p>
