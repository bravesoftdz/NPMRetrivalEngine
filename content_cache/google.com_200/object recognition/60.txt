        <article class="article-text-block"> 
         <h3 class="project-attribution">by <span class="project-attribution-title">Jim Spadaccini</span>, founder &amp; creative director</h3> 
         <div class="list-item"> 
          <p></p>
          <p>Our <a href="http://tangibleengine.com/" target="_blank" rel="nofollow" title="This link will take you to an external web site. We are not responsible for their content." class="external-link no-image">Tangible Engine</a> software package allows for the recognition of 3D printed conductive objects on the surface of our <a href="/products/touch-tables/">multitouch tables</a>. Tangible Engine is compatible with our touch table models that are integrated with 43?, 49?, 55? and 65? 4K Ultra HD displays. What makes our object recognition system unique is that it works with the 3M projected-capacitive touch technology featured in our products. Therefore, unlike other touch systems, it is non-optical which means that it doesn’t require the use cameras or other optical sensors.</p> 
          <p>And now, Tangible Engine supports object recognition on multiple displays! We’ve been testing Tangible Engine on a four 55? <a href="/products/touch-tables/platform/">Platform multitouch table</a> configuration as seen in the video below.</p> 
          <style>.videoWrapper{position:relative;padding-bottom:56.25%;padding-top:25px;height:0;}.videoWrapper iframe{position:absolute;top:0;left:0;width:100%;height:100%;}</style> 
          <div class="row videoWrapper">
           <iframe src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/zp1S0D8zD9Y?rel=0" width="853" height="480" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe>
          </div> 
          <p>Tangible Engine is a proprietary software system that works with Ideum touch tables and displays. It is available for purchase with our products or can be acquired for free, at the time of sale, with a multiple table unit purchase. <a href="/contact/">Contact our sales group</a> for more information or visit the <a href="http://tangibleengine.com/" target="_blank" rel="nofollow" title="This link will take you to an external web site. We are not responsible for their content." class="external-link no-image">Tangible Engine</a> website to learn more. </p>
