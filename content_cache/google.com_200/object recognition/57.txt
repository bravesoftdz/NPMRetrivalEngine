           <h1>Sensor Packages</h1> 
           <div class="sectionH2 cf" id="h"> 
            <h2>Detecting potential collisions</h2> 
            <p>Toyota Safety Sense is available in two packages, both of which integrate numerous sensors to detect for potential collisions ahead of the vehicle.</p> 
            <br class="clear"> 
            <div class="sectionH3 cf"> 
             <h3 class="mb5">Laser radar</h3> 
             <ul class="ulist mt0"> 
              <li class="mt5">Can make precise distance measurements, even at night or at short distances; effective for detecting vehicles ahead.</li> 
             </ul> 
             <h3 class="mb5">Millimeter-wave radar</h3> 
             <ul class="ulist mt0"> 
              <li class="mt5">Particularly effective when detecting vehicles relatively far ahead</li> 
              <li class="mt0">Not easily impacted by adverse weather (e.g. rain, fog or falling snow)</li> 
              <li class="mt0">Pedestrian detection also possible (works in combination with single lens camera)</li> 
             </ul> 
             <h3 class="mb5">Single-lens camera</h3> 
             <ul class="ulist mt0 mb30"> 
              <li class="mt5">Can detect objects and recognize shapes and white or yellow lane markings.</li> 
             </ul> 
             <div class="mb0 pt30 bdt_gr"> 
              <h2>Sensor combinations offered in Toyota Safety Sense packages</h2> 
             </div> 
             <h3 class="mb5">Single-lens camera and laser radar</h3> 
             <p class="mb5">Combined, a single lens camera and laser radar device ensure exceptionally high performance and reliability.</p> 
             <p class="mb10"><img src="/innovation/safety_technology/safety_technology/technology_file/pre-collision/images/sft_17_01.jpg" alt="" width="700" height="180"></p> 
             <!-- /sectionH3 --> 
            </div> 
            <div class="floLeft w340 mb30"> 
             <img src="/innovation/safety_technology/safety_technology/technology_file/pre-collision/images/sft_17_02.jpg" alt="" width="340" height="250">
            </div> 
            <div class="sectionH3 cf"> 
             <div class="floRight w340"> 
              <h3>System and features</h3> 
              <h4 class="mb5">[Laser radar]</h4> 
              <ul class="ulist mt0"> 
               <li class="mt5">Can make precise distance measurements, even at night or at short distances; effective for detecting vehicles ahead</li> 
              </ul> 
              <h4 class="mb5">[Single-lens camera]</h4> 
              <ul class="ulist mt0"> 
               <li class="mt5">Can detect objects and recognize shapes and white or yellow lane markings.</li> 
              </ul> 
             </div> 
             <!-- /sectionH3 --> 
            </div> 
            <br class="clear"> 
            <div class="sectionH3 cf"> 
             <h3 class="mb20">Single-lens camera and millimeter-wave radar</h3> 
             <p class="mb5">Combined, a single-lens camera and millimeter-wave radar also detect potential collisions with pedestrians. Additionally, millimeter-wave radar enables radar cruise control.</p> 
             <p class="mb10"><img src="/innovation/safety_technology/safety_technology/technology_file/pre-collision/images/sft_17_03.jpg" alt="" width="700" height="180"></p> 
             <!-- /sectionH3 --> 
            </div> 
            <div class="floLeft w340 mb5"> 
             <img src="/innovation/safety_technology/safety_technology/technology_file/pre-collision/images/sft_17_04.jpg" alt="" width="340" height="250">
            </div> 
            <div class="sectionH3 cf"> 
             <div class="floRight w340"> 
              <h3>System and features</h3> 
              <h4 class="mb5">[Millimeter-wave radar]</h4> 
              <ul class="ulist mt0"> 
               <li class="mt5">Particularly effective when detecting vehicles relatively far ahead</li> 
               <li class="mt0">Not easily impacted by adverse weather (e.g. rain, fog or falling snow)</li> 
               <li class="mt0">Pedestrian detection also possible (works in combination with single-lens camera)</li>  
              </ul> 
              <h4 class="mb5">[Single-lens camera]</h4> 
              <ul class="ulist mt0"> 
               <li class="mt5">Can detect objects and recognize shapes and white or yellow lane markings.</li> 
              </ul> 
             </div>
