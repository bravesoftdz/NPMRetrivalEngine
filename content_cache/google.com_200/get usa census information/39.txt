             <div class="post-text" itemprop="text"> 
              <p>If you wish to use <a href="https://github.com/vogdb/SelectLayersControl" rel="nofollow">Leaflet.selectLayers plugin</a>, you need to download the <a href="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/vogdb/SelectLayersControl/master/dist/leaflet.select-layers.min.js" rel="nofollow">corresponding JavaScript file</a>, and add it through a script tag to your page in order for the JS engine to know what <code>L.control.selectLayers</code> is. As said by IvanSanchez, it is not part of the core Leaflet API.</p> 
              <p>Assuming you have placed the JS file in your <code>scripts</code> subfolder, like the other plugins you use:</p> 
              <pre class="lang-html prettyprint-override"><code>&lt;script src="scripts/leaflet.select-layers.min.js"&gt;&lt;/script&gt;
</code></pre> 
              <p>Not sure why you instantiate <code>L.control.layers</code> then try to instantiate <code>L.control.selectLayers</code>…</p>
