        <h1 class="medium">What call packages are available with BT Business Call Essentials? </h1> 
       </header> 
      </div> 
     </div>

     <article class="article" role="article" itemscope itemtype="http://schema.org/Article"> 
      <!-- Module: Article content --> 
      <p>We've got two call packages, both of which cost £10 a month for each line.</p> 
      <p><b>BT Business Call Essentials UK Unlimited</b> includes:</p> 
      <ul> 
       <li>Unlimited calls to UK numbers starting with 01, 02, 03, and 05 (where charged at G21 rates), and calls from Northern Ireland to landlines in the Republic of Ireland</li> 
       <li>Unlimited calls to UK mainland-located mobiles (excluding calls to FM bands 7, 9, 10, 11, 13, 15, and 17 and all FW bands)</li> 
      </ul> 
      <p><b>BT Business Call Essentials IDD</b> includes:</p> 
      <ul> 
       <li>2500 fixed IDD minutes (excludes IDD mobiles and some IDD countries)</li> 
      </ul> 
      <p>We'll charge the normal BT Call Essentials rates for calls not included within your call package.</p> 
      <p>Information on all call charges can be found in the <b><a href="http://www.bt.com/pricing/current/Cust_Opts_Bus_boo/FrameworkImpl1336268.htm#FrameworkImpl1336268">BT Price List</a></b>.</p>
