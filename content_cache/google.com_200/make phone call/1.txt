           <p>When handling phone calls with <a href="https://www.twilio.com/docs/api/twiml" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'https://www.twilio.com/docs/api/twiml', 'TwiML');">TwiML</a>, you have many options for controlling the caller’s experience. You can <a href="https://www.twilio.com/docs/api/twiml/say" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'https://www.twilio.com/docs/api/twiml/say', '<Say>');">&lt;Say&gt;</a>&nbsp;something in a robot voice, <a href="https://www.twilio.com/docs/api/twiml/dial" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'https://www.twilio.com/docs/api/twiml/dial', '<Dial>');">&lt;Dial&gt;</a>&nbsp;other numbers into the call and even <a href="https://www.twilio.com/docs/api/twiml/record" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'https://www.twilio.com/docs/api/twiml/record', '<Record>');">&lt;Record&gt;</a>&nbsp;the call. My personal favorite is the ability to play audio files over the phone using the <a href="https://www.twilio.com/docs/api/twiml/play" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'https://www.twilio.com/docs/api/twiml/play', '<Play>');">&lt;Play&gt;</a>&nbsp;TwiML verb.&nbsp;You can even create your own version of <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/They_Might_Be_Giants#Dial-A-Song_.281985.E2.80.932008.29" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/They_Might_Be_Giants#Dial-A-Song_.281985.E2.80.932008.29', 'Dial-A-Song');">Dial-A-Song</a>, a hotline run by the band <a href="https://www.twilio.com/blog/2015/02/they-might-be-giants-perform-at-signal.html" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'https://www.twilio.com/blog/2015/02/they-might-be-giants-perform-at-signal.html', 'They Might Be Giants');">They Might Be Giants</a>&nbsp;that allows you to listen to their music if you call the number (844) 387-6962.</p>
