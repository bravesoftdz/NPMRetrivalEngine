           <p>The HTTP stands for <strong>HyperText Transfer Protocol</strong>. HyperText is text with links in it (like <a href="http://www.w3.org/">this</a>!) and a transfer protocol is a fancy way of saying "rules for getting something from one place to another." In this case, the rules are for transferring web pages to your browser.</p>
