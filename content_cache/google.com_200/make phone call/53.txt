         <p>Star codes allow you to access additional features on your phone. Value-added features and star codes are available to Shaw Phone customers. The exact availability of these features depends on which Shaw Phone package you are currently subscribed to. To see which features come with your phone package, please visit <a class="jive-link-external-small" href="https://community.shaw.ca/external-link.jspa?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.shaw.ca%2Fhome-phone%2F" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">Shaw Home Phone Plans</a>.</p>
         <p style="min-height: 8pt; padding: 0px;">&nbsp;</p>

             <td colspan="2" style="background-color: #ffffff; height: 122px; padding: 20px; vertical-align: top; width: 96.0078%;"><p>Here is some additional information on how to enable/disable Call Forwarding.</p><p style="min-height: 8pt; padding: 0px;">&nbsp;</p><p><strong>How to activate Call Forwarding</strong><br> <br>To Activate Call Forwarding:</p>
              <ol>
               <li>Wait for dial tone</li>
               <li>Dial *72 and you will hear a stuttered tone</li>
               <li>Once the dial tone returns to normal, dial the number to which you would like to forward your calls. When the number rings, you have two options:
                <ul>
                 <li>Answer the call to the selected number to confirm the forward (e.g. cell phones)</li>
                 <li>If the line is busy, or there is no answer, hang up and repeat the above steps<br><br></li>
                </ul></li>
              </ol><p><strong>To disable Call Forwarding</strong>:</p>
              <ol>
               <li>Wait for dial tone</li>
               <li>dial *73</li>
