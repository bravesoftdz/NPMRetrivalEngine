            <p>Wake up to the calls of seagulls, splash in the warm surf, and swim in our crystal-clear pool—at Crowne Plaza Melbourne Oceanfront, you can do it all. Our inviting Melbourne beach hotel accommodations close to Disney World feature plush, comfortable guestrooms and suites, perfect for families, group travel, business trips, and romantic getaways. Indulge in our amenities, including the Crowne Plaza Sleep Advantage program, in-room microwaves and refrigerators, plush linens, and a complimentary shuttle to Melbourne International Airport (MLB).</p> 
            <p>Our Melbourne, Florida beach hotel is located in close proximity to Space Coast attractions including Patrick Air Force Base, Port Canaveral, and the Kennedy Space Center. We are also just an hour from Orlando, home to such major attractions as Walt Disney World, SeaWorld, and Universal Studios. Spend one day of your vacation strolling down the Florida seashore or surfing Cocoa Beach, and spend the next on the area’s most thrilling rides and sweetest theme parks—the possibilities are endless.</p> 
            <p>Events and meetings are made memorable at our hotel, boasting 10,000 square feet of indoor event space and 6,000 square feet of outdoor deck space that can accommodate up to 500 people. Plan an alluring beach wedding overlooking the Atlantic for 300, or organize a board meeting for 12. The master event planners at Crowne Plaza Melbourne Oceanfront can do it all.</p>
