       <p class="content-subtitle">A fun filled family adventure package</p> 
       <h1 class="content-title">2017 Anglesea Adventure Winter Package</h1> 
       <div class="content-content container container-thin"> 
        <div class="typography typography-contentblock"> 
         <p><img class="leftAlone" title="" src="/assets-anglesea/Uploads/Anglesea-Beach-Great-Ocean-Road-Victoria-Australia-2a.jpg" alt="Anglesea Beach Great Ocean Road Victoria Australia 2a" width="223" height="333"></p> 
         <h3>From learning to surf, grabbing a canoe and exploring our river, to hitting a little white ball around our new mini-golf course or spending a day trampolining. And we know that doing things together as a family is important, so we have a self guided bike tour organised for you all, with no need to bring your bikes. Yep, that's right, we provide them. For $300 the package includes exclusive discounts to all these and more great Anglesea winter activities.&nbsp;</h3> 
         <h3><a href="/assets-anglesea/Uploads/2017-Anglesea-Adventure-Package-Flyer3.pdf" target="_blank">Click here for full details of the adventure package</a></h3> 
         <h3><a title="Buy Now" href="http://www.greatoceanroadtourism.org.au/product/anglesea-adventure-winter-package/" target="_blank">BUY NOW</a></h3>

         <div class="attributes-intro-inner typography container"> 
          <p class="attributes-desc" style="text-align: center;"><strong>I AM ANGLESEA</strong></p> 
          <p class="attributes-desc attributes-subdesc" style="text-align: left;">With salty hair, sandy toes and wind against your cheeks slide down sand dunes, explore the forests, swim in the sea, float on the river, breathe the wild air, gaze at the stars…and enjoy the natural world.</p> 
          <p class="attributes-desc attributes-subdesc" style="text-align: left;">Where the river meets the ocean, the seaside town of Anglesea boasts towering coastal cliffs and sprawling white beaches along the Great Ocean Road and is nestled between the nearby coastal towns of Torquay and Aireys Inlet.</p> 
          <p><iframe src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/4dAp8HP1MVM?rel=0" width="854" height="480" frameborder="0"></iframe></p> 
         </div> 
        </div> 
       </div> 
       <div class="container"> 
        <div class="row clearfix odd"> 
         <div class="col-main"> 
          <div class="image-holder"> 
           <a href="/family-fun/"> <img src="/assets-anglesea/Uploads/_resampled/ScaleWidthWyI4NDkiXQ-ANGLESEA-GREAT-OCEAN-ROAD-VICTORIA-AUSTRALIA.jpg" alt="Family fun" class="resize"> <span class="title-overlay "> <span class="vc"> <span class="vc-inner"> <h3 class="col-main-title">Family fun</h3> </span> </span> </span> </a> 
          </div> 
          <div class="typography"> 
           <p></p>
           <p>Nature provides an extensive playground of sheltered beaches where kids can make sand castles, swim and run expending excess energy and making joyous laughter and happy memories. When the beach weather turns head to the shipwreck playground and picnic areas at Coogarah Park set on the riverbank just a couple of minutes from the centre of town. There are also walking tracks and a skate park in the grounds.</p>
           <p></p> 
          </div> 
         </div> 
         <div class="col-sub"> 
          <div class="inner-attribute-padding"> 
           <div class="image-holder"> 
            <a href="/mountain-biking/"> <img src="/assets-anglesea/Uploads/_resampled/FillWyIyNjciLCIyNTgiXQ-Anglesea-Mountain-biking-16.jpg" alt="Anglesea Mountain biking 16" class="resize"> </a> 
           </div> 
           <h4 class="col-sub-title"> <a href="/mountain-biking/"> Mountain Biking </a> </h4> 
           <div class="typography"> 
            <p>Check out the trails, with ample space to run amok and throw mud on your tyres. Pass through eucalypt forests and dense fern gullies and discover a new feature every time you visit with its purpose-built XC and downhill trails, with obstacles including log rides and boardwalks. Perfect your jumps at the Anglesea Bike Park Mountain Cross Track, Victoria's first professionally designed loop and jumps track. </p> 
           </div> 
          </div> 
         </div> 
        </div> 
        <div class="row clearfix even"> 
         <div class="col-main"> 
          <div class="image-holder"> 
           <a href="/surf-coast-walk/"> <img src="/assets-anglesea/Uploads/attractions-main.jpg" alt="Surf Coast Walk" class="resize"> <span class="title-overlay "> <span class="vc"> <span class="vc-inner"> <h3 class="col-main-title">Surf Coast Walk</h3> </span> </span> </span> </a> 
          </div> 
          <div class="typography"> 
           <p></p>
           <p>Disconnect from the ordinary and reconnect with the extraordinary. Whether you're a nature lover, or simply love being outdoors…whether you take an hour or a full day, the Surf Coast Walk puts a stunning and unique coastal environment within easy reach. Feel the dirt and sand beneath your feet, put one foot in front of the other, take a breath, pause and look around.</p>
           <p></p> 
          </div> 
         </div> 
         <div class="col-sub"> 
          <div class="inner-attribute-padding"> 
           <div class="image-holder"> 
            <a href="/beaches/"> <img src="/assets-anglesea/Uploads/_resampled/FillWyIyNjciLCIyNTgiXQ-ANGLESEA-MAIN-BEACH-GREAT-OCEAN-ROAD-VICTOTRIA-AUSTRALIA.jpg" alt="ANGLESEA MAIN BEACH GREAT OCEAN ROAD VICTOTRIA AUSTRALIA" class="resize"> </a> 
           </div> 
           <h4 class="col-sub-title"> <a href="/beaches/"> Beaches </a> </h4> 
           <div class="typography"> 
            <p></p>
            <p>Fall under the spell of the wild ocean. Play in the sun on Anglesea's main beach, a wide sandy expanse beside the Anglesea River, which is perfect for swimming, surfing, or simply relaxing. On holiday with the kids? Paddle in protected waters at nearby Point Roadknight beach. Learn from the pros with lessons in surfing, sailing and windsurfing at one of the local beaches.</p>
