       <h2>QuickStart</h2> 
       <h3>Step 1: Include FusionChart libraries</h3> 
       <p>In your HTML, include <code>fusioncharts.js</code> and <code>themes/fusioncharts.theme.ocean.js</code> after all other scripts:</p> 
       <pre class="language-markup"><code>&lt;script type="text/javascript" src="/path/to/fusioncharts.js"&gt;&lt;/script&gt;
&lt;script type="text/javascript" src="/path/to/themes/fusioncharts.theme.ocean.js"&gt;&lt;/script&gt;</code></pre> 
       <h3>Step 2: Include FusionCharts PHP wrapper in your page</h3> 
       <p>In the page, include <code>fusioncharts.php</code> wrapper. </p> 
       <pre class="language-php"><code>include("includes/fusioncharts.php");</code></pre> 
       <h3>Step 3: Render the chart by providing required data in constructor parameter</h3> 
       <p>In your code, find the section where you wish to add the chart and provide data to the FusionCharts constructor.</p> 
       <pre class="language-php"><code>// Syntax for the constructor - new FusionCharts("type of chart", "unique chart id", "width of chart", "height of chart", "div id to render the chart", "type of data", "actual data")
	$columnChart = new FusionCharts("column2d", "ex1" , 600, 400, "chart-1", "json", '{  
		   "chart":{  
			  "caption":"Harry's SuperMart",
			  "subCaption":"Top 5 stores in last month by revenue",
			  "numberPrefix":"$",
			  "theme":"ocean"
		   },
		   "data":[  
			  {  
				 "label":"Bakersfield Central",
				 "value":"880000"
			  },
			  {  
				 "label":"Garden Groove harbour",
				 "value":"730000"
			  },
			  {  
				 "label":"Los Angeles Topanga",
				 "value":"590000"
			  },
			  {  
				 "label":"Compton-Rancho Dom",
				 "value":"520000"
			  },
			  {  
				 "label":"Daly City Serramonte",
				 "value":"330000"
			  }
		   ]
		}');</code></pre> 
       <h3>Step 4: Render FusionCharts</h3> 
       <p>In the previous code, we are populating the FusionCharts constructor with data and chart properties.</p> 
       <p>It's time to render it.</p> 
       <pre class="language-php"><code>// Render the chart
$columnChart-&gt;render();</code></pre> 
       <p>And your chart should display when you load the page. </p>
