Maximum entropy classifiers are commonly..." target="_blank"> 12. </a> 3.Maximum Entropy? Maximum entropy classifiers are commonly used as alternatives to naiveBayes classifiers because they do not assume statistical independence of therandom variables (commonly known as features) that serve as predictors.?
