         <h1>Convert an image from JPG to PNG</h1> 
         <p>Changing the format of uploaded images is easily done using the <a href="/services/image-manipulation/image-resize/">/image/resize</a> <dfn>Robot</dfn>. You just need to supply the <code>format</code> parameter and set it to the appropriate value. Please notice the <code>[mime] =&gt; image/png</code> in the result JSON after having uploaded some images.</p> 
         <div class="referer-alert alert alert-block alert-danger"> 
          <strong>Warning</strong> It seems your browser does not send the referer, which we need to stop people from (ab)using our demos in other websites. If you want to use the demos, please allow your browser to send its referer to us. Adding us to the whitelist of blockers usually helps. 
         </div> 
         <h2>This demo uses:</h2> 
         <ul> 
          <li> Our <a href="/services/image-manipulation/image-resize/"> <img width="32" height="32" src="https://transloadit.edgly.net/assets/images/robots/120x120/image-resize.jpg" title="Our /image/resize robot"> /image/resize</a> <dfn>Robot</dfn>, which resizes, crops, changes colorization, rotation, and applies text and watermarks to images  </li> 
         </ul> 
         <p> <br> This demo uses <code>:original</code>, a magic word indicating uploads that were received by Transloadit. </p>
