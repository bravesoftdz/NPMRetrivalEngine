       <div class="jive-rendered-content">
        <p>Use these steps to use text to speech to have messages read out loud on the Samsung Galaxy S7 edge.</p>
        <p style="min-height: 8pt; padding: 0px;">&nbsp;</p>
        <div class="jiveBorder" style="border: 1px solid #9b9b9b; padding: 5px; width: 98%;">
         <p><strong>On this page:</strong></p>
         <ul>
          <li><a class="jive-link-anchor-small" href="#steps1">Set up</a></li>
          <li><a class="jive-link-anchor-small" href="#steps2">TalkBack settings</a></li>
          <li><a class="jive-link-anchor-small" href="#steps3">Text to speech settings</a></li>
          <li><a class="jive-link-anchor-small" href="#steps5">Turn on TalkBack</a></li>
         </ul>
        </div>
        <p style="min-height: 8pt; padding: 0px;">&nbsp;</p>
        <p style="min-height: 8pt; padding: 0px;">&nbsp;</p>
        <h3><a name="steps1" style="display: block; height: 75px; margin-top: -75px; visibility: hidden;"></a>Set up</h3>
        <p>If you are using Text to speech in English, this language is already installed and available to use.</p>
        <p style="min-height: 8pt; padding: 0px;">&nbsp;</p>
        <h4>Android 6.0 Marshmallow</h4>
        <ol>
         <li>From any Home screen, tap the<strong><strong> Apps</strong> icon</strong>.</li>
         <li>Tap <strong>Settings</strong>.</li>
         <li>Tap <strong>Language &amp; input</strong>.</li>
         <li>Under 'Speech,' tap <strong>Text-to-speech options</strong>.</li>
         <li>Select desired TTS engine:
          <ul>
           <li><strong>Samsung text-to-speech engine</strong></li>
           <li><strong>Google Text-to-speech Engine</strong></li>
          </ul></li>
         <li>Next to the desired search engine, tap the <strong>Settings </strong>icon.</li>
         <li>Tap <strong>Install voice data</strong>.
          <ul>
           <li>For the Samsung text-to-speech engine, the desired language may already be downloaded.</li>
           <li>However, you have the option of downloading a higher quality version.</li>
          </ul></li>
         <li>Next to the desired language, tap <strong>Download </strong>or the <strong>Download </strong>icon.</li>
         <li>Wait as the language downloads. It may take a few minutes.</li>
         <li>Tap the <strong>Back </strong>key.</li>
         <li>Tap <strong>Language </strong>to select the language.</li>
        </ol>
        <p style="min-height: 8pt; padding: 0px;">&nbsp;</p>
        <h4>Android 7.0 Nougat</h4>
        <ol>
         <li>From any Home screen, tap the <strong>Apps icon.</strong></li>
         <li>Tap <strong>Settings</strong>.</li>
         <li>Tap <strong>General management</strong>.</li>
         <li>Tap <strong>Language &amp; input</strong>.</li>
         <li>Under 'Speech,' tap <strong>Text-to-speech options</strong>.</li>
         <li>Select desired TTS engine:
          <ul>
           <li><strong>Samsung text-to-speech engine</strong></li>
           <li><strong>Google Text-to-speech Engine</strong></li>
          </ul></li>
         <li>Next to the desired search engine, tap the <strong>Settings </strong>icon.</li>
         <li>Tap <strong>Install voice data</strong>.
          <ul>
           <li>For the Samsung text-to-speech engine, the desired language may already be downloaded.</li>
           <li>However, you have the option of downloading a higher quality version.</li>
          </ul></li>
         <li>Next to the desired language, tap <strong>Download </strong>or the <strong>Download </strong>icon.</li>
         <li>Wait as the language downloads. It may take a few minutes.</li>
         <li>Tap the <strong>Back </strong>key.</li>
         <li>Tap <strong>Language </strong>to select the language.</li>
        </ol>
        <p style="min-height: 8pt; padding: 0px;">&nbsp;</p>
        <h3><a name="steps2" style="display: block; height: 75px; margin-top: -75px; visibility: hidden;"></a>TalkBack settings</h3>
        <ol>
         <li>From any Home screen, tap the<strong><strong> Apps</strong> icon</strong>.</li>
         <li>Tap <strong>Settings</strong>.</li>
         <li>Tap <strong>Accessibility</strong>.</li>
         <li>Under 'More settings,' tap <strong>Direct access</strong>.</li>
         <li>Slide the Direct access <strong>Switch </strong>right to the ON position.
          <ul>
           <li>This enables your Accessibility settings.</li>
           <li>You can also enable your Accessibility settings by pressing the Home key 3 times in quick succession.</li>
          </ul></li>
         <li>Tap <strong>Galaxy </strong><strong>TalkBack </strong>to enable the feature.</li>
         <li>Tap <strong>Settings</strong>.</li>
         <li>Tap to adjust the desired settings.</li>
        </ol>
        <p style="min-height: 8pt; padding: 0px;">&nbsp;</p>
        <h3><a name="steps3" style="display: block; height: 75px; margin-top: -75px; visibility: hidden;"></a>Text to speech settings</h3>
        <h4>Android 6.0 Marshmallow</h4>
        <ol>
         <li>From any Home screen, tap the <strong> Apps</strong> icon.</li>
         <li>Tap <strong>Settings</strong>.</li>
         <li>Tap <strong>Language &amp; input</strong>.</li>
         <li>Under 'Speech,' tap <strong>Text-to-speech options</strong>.</li>
         <li>Tap <strong>Speech rate </strong>and then adjust how fast the text will be spoken.</li>
         <li>Tap the <strong>Settings </strong>icon next to the desired TTS engine (Samsung or Google).</li>
         <li>To tap the Settings icon, the TTS engine must be selected.</li>
         <li>Tap <strong>Language </strong>to adjust the spoken language.</li>
         <li>Tap adjust any other desired settings.</li>
        </ol>
        <p style="min-height: 8pt; padding: 0px;">&nbsp;</p>
        <h4>Android 7.0 Nougat</h4>
        <ol>
         <li>From any Home screen, tap the <strong>Apps icon.</strong></li>
         <li>Tap <strong>Settings</strong>.</li>
         <li>Tap <strong>General management</strong>.</li>
         <li>Tap <strong>Language &amp; input</strong>.</li>
         <li>Under 'Speech,' tap <strong>Text-to-speech options</strong>.</li>
         <li>Tap <strong>Speech rate </strong>and then adjust how fast the text will be spoken.</li>
         <li>Tap the <strong>Settings </strong>icon next to the desired TTS engine (Samsung or Google).</li>
         <li>To tap the Settings icon, the TTS engine must be selected.</li>
         <li>Tap <strong>Language </strong>to adjust the spoken language.</li>
         <li>Tap adjust any other desired settings.</li>
        </ol>
        <p style="min-height: 8pt; padding: 0px;">&nbsp;</p>
        <h3><a name="steps5" style="display: block; height: 75px; margin-top: -75px; visibility: hidden;"></a>Turn on TalkBack</h3>
        <ol>
         <li>From any Home screen, tap the<strong><strong> Apps</strong> icon</strong>.</li>
         <li>Tap <strong>Settings</strong>.</li>
         <li>Tap <strong>Accessibility</strong>.</li>
         <li>Under 'More settings,' tap <strong>Direct access</strong>.</li>
         <li>Slide the Direct access <strong>Switch </strong>right to the ON position.
          <ul>
           <li>This enables your Accessibility settings.</li>
           <li>You can also enable your Accessibility settings by pressing the Home key 3 times in quick succession.</li>
          </ul></li>
         <li>Tap <strong>Galaxy </strong><strong>TalkBack </strong>to enable the feature.</li>
        </ol>
       </div>
