     <div class="entry-content"> 
      <p>Hey everyone – a little trick here today with the HTML5 audio element and the Google translate API.</p> 
      <p>Turns out you can use <a href="https://translate.google.com/translate_tts?ie=UTF-8&amp;q=hi%20this%20is%20wes&amp;tl=en" target="_blank">this query string</a> to dynamically create an audio element. We can then encode our text, pop that url into an audio element and play it with a few lines of JavaScript.</p> 
      <p>It’s worth noting that this isn’t the real way to do text to speech, but its a very simple, cross browser solution that can help you out in a pinch.</p> 
      <p>Here is a quick video detailing how it works – I’d love to see what you all do with this.</p>
