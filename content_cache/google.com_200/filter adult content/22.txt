    <div class="wrapper-80"> 
     <h2>Why do I need 1Blocker?</h2> 
     <div id="reasons"> 
      <div class="pure-g"> 
       <div class="pure-u-1 pure-u-sm-1-2"> 
        <div class="reason-icon">
         <img src="./img/adblock.svg">
        </div> 
        <h3>Block annoying ads</h3> 
        <p>Ads have become a <a target="_blank" href="https://www.theverge.com/2017/1/18/14304276/walt-mossberg-online-ads-bad-business">total disaster</a> on websites by ruining the browsing experience. <a target="_blank" href="https://research.hubspot.com/reports/why-people-block-ads-and-what-it-means-for-marketers-and-advertisers#LINK1">No one likes</a> to see ads that pop over the entire screen.</p> 
       </div> 
       <div class="pure-u-1 pure-u-sm-1-2"> 
        <div class="reason-icon">
         <img src="./img/stopwatch.svg">
        </div> 
        <h3>Save battery, data and time</h3> 
        <p>Independent tests by <a target="_blank" href="http://thewirecutter.com/blog/what-you-should-and-shouldnt-do-to-extend-your-phones-battery-life/#use-an-ad-blocker">Wirecutter</a>, <a target="_blank" href="https://www.nytimes.com/2015/10/01/technology/personaltech/ad-blockers-mobile-iphone-browsers.html">NY Times</a>, and <a target="_blank" href="https://brooksreview.net/2015/09/content-blocker-test/">BrooksReview</a> confirm that 1Blocker cuts data loaded by sites on average by <a target="_blank" href="https://twitter.com/DanFrakes/status/672871313292136448">over 50%</a>.</p> 
       </div> 
       <div class="pure-u-1 pure-u-sm-1-2"> 
        <div class="reason-icon">
         <img src="./img/shield.svg">
        </div> 
        <h3>Protect yourself online</h3> 
        <p>Many corporations <a target="_blank" href="https://myshadow.org/browser-tracking">track your behavior</a> and what sites you visit. Online <a target="_blank" href="https://www.riskiq.com/press-release/260-percent-spike-in-malicious-advertisements-2015/">ads are not safe</a> and may <a target="_blank" href="https://www.engadget.com/2016/01/08/you-say-advertising-i-say-block-that-malware/">serve you malware</a> even on major sites like Forbes and Yahoo.</p> 
       </div> 
       <div class="pure-u-1 pure-u-sm-1-2"> 
        <div class="reason-icon">
         <img src="./img/performance.svg">
        </div> 
        <h3>Improve your productivity</h3> 
        <p>Besides ads, webpages often contain distracting elements (like “You Might Also Like” and subscription boxes) that <a target="_blank" href="https://backstage.1blocker.com/how-to-hide-annoying-page-elements-17d0f20699ca#ae89">disturb your work flow</a> and productivity.</p> 
       </div> 
      </div>

     <h2>Why can’t I get all 1Blocker features for free?</h2> 
     <div id="letter"> 
      <p> A free app is an illusion. It is either going to stop being developed or get acquired by another company and shut down. Often free apps and services are using a shady way of making money which is bad for users. </p> 
      <blockquote class="twitter-tweet" data-dnt="true" data-lang="en">
       <p lang="en" dir="ltr">Pay for the apps you use, because it avoids companies having to resort to business models that are not in your favor like selling your data <a href="https://t.co/QVz5EheyN5">https://t.co/QVz5EheyN5</a></p>— Pieter Levels (@levelsio) 
       <a href="https://twitter.com/levelsio/status/856210395668766720">April 23, 2017</a>
      </blockquote> 
      <p> “If you are using a free product, you are the product being sold.” </p> 
      <p> AdBlock Plus makes money from <a target="_blank" href="https://www.theverge.com/2016/9/13/12890050/adblock-plus-now-sells-ads">selling ads</a> and <a target="_blank" href="http://www.businessinsider.com/how-adblock-plus-makes-money-2015-12">whitelisting advertisers</a> who pay them. Ghostery <a target="_blank" href="http://www.businessinsider.com/evidon-sells-ghostery-data-to-advertisers-2013-6">sells data to the ad industry</a> and recently <a target="_blank" href="https://brooksreview.net/2017/04/ghostery-is-acquired-by-cliqz/">was acquired</a> by a browser company which tracks users to show offers. These are just <a target="_blank" href="https://www.theverge.com/2015/9/24/9393941/clear-ios-ad-blocker-offering-paid-whitelist">publicly admitted examples</a>, a tip of the iceberg. </p> 
      <p> <strong>The only way we make money is through sales of 1Blocker on the App Store.</strong> </p> 
      <p> <a target="_blank" href="https://marco.org/2011/12/06/dont-be-a-free-user">Don't be a free user</a>. If you want 1Blocker to survive, please support us. It is the only way we can continue working on the app. </p>
