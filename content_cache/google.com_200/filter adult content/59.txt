          <h2>Why Contact Form 7?</h2>

We love Contact Form 7 because it is free, easy to use, and reliable. Here are some more reasons why we like Contact Form 7:</p>

           <li>It is free!  Though there are many contact form plug-ins out there, Contact Form 7 is free and very customizable.</li> 
           <li>Helpful Docs  The Contact Form 7 website has an awesome <a href="https://contactform7.com/docs/" target="_blank">Docs</a> page with helpful hints and tutorials.</li> 
           <li>Spam Filtering  Contact Form 7 <a href="https://contactform7.com/spam-filtering-with-akismet/" target="_blank">teams up with Akismet</a>, a plug-in that helps to filter spam in order to help protect your site.</li> 
           <li>Pre-Made Contact Forms  Contact Form 7 gives you the option of <a href="https://winningwp.com/contact-form-7/" target="_blank">automatically adding contact forms</a> to your websites.</li> 
           <li>Custom Forms&nbsp; Though having pre-made contact forms is very convenient, you can also customize your Contact Form 7. You can use HTML to do this.</li>

          <h2>Contact Form 7 Programming Languages</h2> 
          <p>The best way to style Contact Form 7 is by using CSS.&nbsp;However, Contact Form 7 warns not to add CSS to the plug-in or theme itself <a href="https://contactform7.com/styling-contact-form/" target="_blank">because it will be wiped when it is updated</a>. Instead, they recommend to create a child theme in order to protect your customizations. Our developers are able to use both CSS and HTML in order to customize Contact Form 7. We can set up and configure the child theme for you. We are to use PHP and JavaScript to flesh out other necessary elements of your WordPress site.</p>
