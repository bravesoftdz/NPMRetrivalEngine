        <h1>Shaw Gateway - How to remove channels from the Guide</h1>

        <div class="jive-rendered-content">
         <p style="color: #363636; background-color: #ffffff; border: 0px; padding: 0px;"><span style="border: 0px; font-weight: inherit; font-size: 12pt; padding: 0px;">On your Gateway system, you have the option to remove channels from the channel listings in your interactive guide. This can be done to remove channels you are not subscribed to, or if you want to hide certain channels from appearing in the guide, such as those that include mature content. Learn how to remove channels from the guide with the instructions below which apply to both the <a class="jive-link-wiki-small" data-containerid="2062" data-containertype="14" data-objectid="1265" data-objecttype="102" href="https://community.shaw.ca/docs/DOC-1265">Gateway</a> and <a class="jive-link-wiki-small" data-containerid="2062" data-containertype="14" data-objectid="5559" data-objecttype="102" href="https://community.shaw.ca/docs/DOC-5559">Gateway HDPVR</a> hardware.</span></p>
         <p style="min-height: 8pt; padding: 0px;">&nbsp;</p>
         <p style="min-height: 8pt; padding: 0px;">&nbsp;</p>
         <div class="j-rte-table">
          <table style="border: 1px solid #c6c6c6; border-width: 1px; border-color: #dedede;" width="100%">
           <thead style="border: 0px;">
            <tr style="background-color: #efefef; height: 38px;">
             <th style="background-color: #0082bb; height: 38px; padding: 10px; vertical-align: middle;"><h6 style="font-weight: normal;"><span style="color: #ffffff; font-size: 22px; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif; padding-left: 5px;"><a name="RemoveChannels"></a>Removing channels from your Shaw Gateway Guide</span><a href="https://community.shaw.ca/servlet/JiveServlet/showImage/102-2163-17-150401/gauge_easy_c.png"><img height="31" src="https://community.shaw.ca/servlet/JiveServlet/downloadImage/102-2163-17-150401/gauge_easy_c.png" style="float: right; padding-right: 10px; vertical-align: middle; max-width:40px;" width="40"></a></h6></th>
            </tr>
           </thead>
           <tbody>
            <tr style="background-color: #efefef; height: 122px;">
             <td colspan="2" style="background-color: #ffffff; height: 122px; padding: 20px; vertical-align: top;"><p style="border: 0px;"><span style="border: 0px; font-weight: inherit; font-size: 12pt;">Follow these channels to remove channels from your Shaw Gateway Guide. <br></span></p><p style="min-height: 8pt; padding: 0px; border: 0px;">&nbsp;</p>
              <ol>
               <li style="border: 0px;"><span style="border: 0px; font-weight: inherit; font-size: 12pt;">Press the <strong>MENU</strong> button on your Gateway remote</span></li>
               <li style="border: 0px;"><span style="border: 0px; font-weight: inherit; font-size: 12pt;">Navigate to <strong>Settings</strong> by using the <strong>RIGHT/LEFT</strong> arrows <br><br></span>
                <center>
                 <a href="https://community.shaw.ca/servlet/JiveServlet/showImage/102-2163-17-150402/pastedImage_1.png"><img alt="Shaw Gateway Menu Settings Option" class="image-46 jive-image j-img-original" height="110" src="https://community.shaw.ca/servlet/JiveServlet/downloadImage/102-2163-17-150402/pastedImage_1.png" style="height: auto;" width="402"></a>
                </center></li>
               <li style="border: 0px;"><span style="border: 0px; font-weight: inherit; font-size: 12pt;">Select <strong>Channel Listings</strong>, then press <strong>OK<br><br></strong></span>
                <center>
                 <a href="https://community.shaw.ca/servlet/JiveServlet/showImage/102-2163-17-150403/pastedImage_13.png"><img alt="Shaw Gateway Menu > Settings > Channel List" class="image-47 jive-image j-img-original" height="109" src="https://community.shaw.ca/servlet/JiveServlet/downloadImage/102-2163-17-150403/pastedImage_13.png" style="height: auto;" width="402"></a>
                 <br>
                 <br>
                </center></li>
               <li style="border: 0px;"><span style="border: 0px; font-weight: inherit; font-size: 12pt;">Scroll down the list and select <strong>OK</strong> on any channels you would like to remove from your listing</span></li>
              </ol><p style="min-height: 8pt; padding: 0px;">&nbsp;</p>
              <center>
               <a href="https://community.shaw.ca/servlet/JiveServlet/showImage/102-2163-17-150404/pastedImage_0.png"><img alt="Shaw Gateway Channel List" class="image-45 jive-image j-img-centered j-img-original" height="240" src="https://community.shaw.ca/servlet/JiveServlet/downloadImage/102-2163-17-150404/pastedImage_0.png" style="height: auto;" width="417"></a>
              </center><p style="min-height: 8pt; padding: 0px;">&nbsp;</p><p><span style="border: 0px; font-weight: inherit; font-size: 12pt;">Channels which <strong>will appear</strong> on your guide will display a yellow/orange icon next to their names <br><br>To re-add channels to your Gateway's guide simply perform the same steps listed above and re-select the channels you wish to appear.<br><br>If you add a new channel or package to your account that contains channels you've previously hidden, you will need to re-add those channels to the Gateway guide before you will be able to view those channels.</span></p></td>
            </tr>
           </tbody>
          </table>
         </div>
         <div __default_attr="info" alert="info" class="jive_macro_alert jive_text_macro" name="alert">
          <p><span style="color: #0082bb; font-size: 15px;"><strong>Note:</strong></span></p>
          <p style="min-height: 8pt; padding: 0px;">&nbsp;</p>
          <p><span style="color: #333333; font-size: 13px;">If you wish to block a channel/channels on multiple Gateway Portals, you will need to follow the above process for <strong>each individual Portal</strong> in your home.</span></p>&nbsp; &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; &nbsp;
