         <h1 class="post-title"> <span itemprop="name" class="usercontent">Nudity and Adult Material</span> <span class="old-post">This post is outdated</span> </h1> 
         <div itemprop="description" class="usercontent">
           FYI: I sent an email to the staff regarding this subject, but wanted to know what everyone else thought. I was just searching toys on shopify.com ... kids toys ... and some resulted in adults toys, vibrators, and even porn (1 with full on nudity in the image). I am pretty sure these are allowed to be sold but to be so easily searchable is a different story. Shouldn't there be some type of verification of age to be allowed to search/view it? Imagine if some kid was looking for toys, came across those adult toys, and their parents saw. First shopify would never be allowed again, then people will use the 'marketplace' less and it would never grow. I might be exaggerating, but I certainly would want shopify to grow. Is this something that may be corrected or it it something we just deal with?

           <div itemprop="description" class="usercontent"> 
            <p>Hi Rob -</p> 
            <p>You probably came across my site. (sex toys, not porn – no nudity).</p> 
            <p>Though I’m pretty sure there’s nothing on my site that would traumatize or shock any kid, I understand your concern, and I do what I can to keep children away.</p> 
            <p>I have a splash page, to prevent minors from coming in, but I hadn’t given a thought to the easy accessibility of marketplace.</p> 
            <p>I would not be against a system whereby shopowners (such as myself) would rate their store “adults only”, and then to have a filter in place on the marketplace search, which would screen those shops out for people who opt for a “child-proof” search or something.</p> 
            <p>In any case, I agree with you that sex toys shouldn’t be popping up in the kids’ toys department. Not quite the same thing.</p> 
            <p>This might be a little difficult to implement reliably though.</p> 
            <p>In the meantime, I wouldn’t really think there are that many kids using shopify.</p>
