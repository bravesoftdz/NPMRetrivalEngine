          <h1 class="entry-title">How to Enable Kodi XBMC Adult Content</h1> 
          <p class="post-meta"> by <span class="author vcard"><a href="https://gameroomsolutions.com/author/ouguitarman/" title="Posts by Ryan" rel="author">Ryan</a></span> | <span class="published">Aug 11, 2015</span> | <a href="https://gameroomsolutions.com/category/blog/" rel="category tag">Blog</a>, <a href="https://gameroomsolutions.com/category/blog/hometheater/" rel="category tag">Home Theater</a>, <a href="https://gameroomsolutions.com/category/uncategorized/" rel="category tag">Uncategorized</a> | </p>
          <img src="https://gameroomsolutions.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/08/content-e1445534909268.jpg" alt="How to Enable Kodi XBMC Adult Content" width="1080" height="675"> 
         </div> 
         <div class="entry-content"> 
          <p>Kodi XBMC has many addons that can really bring your streaming experience to life for Movies, TV Shows, PPV events etc. Another batch of addons you can enable if you so choose on Kodi XBMC adult content. &nbsp;We offer pre-configured Fire TV and Fire TV sticks that are ready to go with all the most popular streaming addons. &nbsp;By default they are adult content ready, but not enabled.</p> 
          <p><a href="https://gameroomsolutions.com/product-category/home-theater/">Checkout our Fire TV and Fire TV Sticks Fully loaded!</a></p> 
          <p>Here is a video walk through on how to enable Kodi XBMC adult content on your installation</p> 
          <div class="et_post_video">
           <iframe width="1080" height="608" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/HDk9-5LTCQ0?feature=oembed&amp;wmode=opaque" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe>
          </div> 
          <p>You will want to learn how to install <a href="https://gameroomsolutions.com/free-tv-and-movies-with-1channel-xbmc-kodi-library-integration/" target="_blank">addon installer</a> if you did not get a <a href="https://gameroomsolutions.com/product-category/home-theater/">pre-configured unit</a> from us. &nbsp;If you received a unit from usor have addon installer installed, then proceed.</p> 
          <p>Once addon installer is ready you will find it under programs on the Kodi XBMC home page.</p> 
          <ul> 
           <li>Click programs 
            <ul> 
             <li>Highlight addon installer and press the C key on your keyboard, the menu key on your remote, or the button with three horizontal lines on your fire tv remote</li> 
             <li>Go into the addon settings</li> 
             <li>In there you will notice Adult is not highlighted by default, navigate there and enable it</li> 
             <li>Ok out of the addon settings</li> 
            </ul> </li> 
           <li>Select Addon installer 
            <ul> 
             <li>In Addon installer you will now see the adult content as a selection</li> 
             <li>In there will be some adult content repositories 
              <ul> 
               <li>Install any or all of them</li> 
              </ul> </li> 
            </ul> </li> 
          </ul> 
          <p>At this point you have only unlocked the ability to install the Kodi XBMC adult content addons. &nbsp;To install the actual video addons</p> 
          <ul> 
           <li>Navigate back to the Kodi XBMC homepage</li> 
           <li>Highlight videos and press the down key and select addons</li> 
           <li>From there a good practice is to continue pressing select on the two dots .. at the top of the list until you get where you see a few entries like Get more or install from zip file</li> 
           <li>You will want to select Get more 
            <ul> 
             <li>In there you will see the repositories you added</li> 
             <li>Select one and you will see the addons</li> 
             <li>Simply select and install the addons you want</li> 
            </ul> </li> 
          </ul> 
          <p>Now you can navigate to the home screen on Kodi XBMC. &nbsp;Again navigate to videos, press down. and select video addons. &nbsp;All of your newly installed Kodi XBMC Adult content will be available for streaming.</p> 
          <p>&nbsp;</p>
