       </svg></a>Intro</h2> 
     <p>Simple abstraction layer for use the services offered by the OpenWeatherMap.org website through its API's. You can easy reach the weather informations you need on over 400k weather stations. For get the APPID follow the OpenWeather.org <a href="http://openweathermap.org/appid">link</a> and complete the request process.</p>
