          <p>The Arsenal man has really started to shine with the Cherries after a slow start on loan and picked up another MotM award for his display this weekend.</p>

          <p><em>“To be honest, I don’t think even he knows what he is going to be doing,”</em> Afobe <a href="http://www.espnfc.com/story/3044581/jack-wilshere-unsure-on-his-arsenal-future-bournemouth-striker-benik-afobe">said</a>. <em>“We have conversations all the time and he just wants to play week in, week out and see what happens.”</em></p>
