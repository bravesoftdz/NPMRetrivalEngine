         <h1 data-swiftype-index="false">Add age verification to your online store</h1> 
         <div class="note note-caution">
          <h4>Caution</h4> 
          <p> This is an advanced tutorial and is <a href="/themes/customization/#unsupported-customizations">not supported by Shopify</a>. Knowledge of web design languages such as HTML, CSS, Javascript and Liquid is required. We suggest hiring a <a href="//experts.shopify.com/">Shopify Expert</a> if you are not comfortable proceeding with the following tutorial.</p> 
         </div> 
         <p>If you sell products such as wine, cigarettes, or dangerous goods, then you might want to discourage visitors under a certain age from browsing your website. You can do this by adding an age selection form to your storefront.</p> 
         <div class="note note-caution">
          <h4>Caution</h4> 
          <p>This method does not meet the age-verification requirements associated with current <a href="http://www.fda.gov/TobaccoProducts/Labeling/RulesRegulationsGuidance/ucm394909.htm">FDA regulations</a> that govern the sale of e-cigarettes, cigars, and tobacco products.</p> 
         </div> 
         <p>Keep in mind that age verification is not the best way to prevent visitors from browsing your website, as there's nothing preventing them from lying about their age. It might even be a nuisance to regular visitors, who will have to make an additional click to access your store.</p> 
         <figure data-image-path="/manual/customize/age-check.jpg"> 
          <img src="/assets/images/manual/customize/age-check.jpg?1505497472" alt="An age verification dialog overlaid on an image of a red squirrel" width="720" height="742"> 
         </figure> 
         <h2 id="create-and-include-an-age-check-snippet">Create and include an age-check snippet</h2> 
         <div class="section-tag-block"> 
          <div class="styleguide-horizontal-in-page-menu">
           <ul class="in-page-menu in-page-menu--horizontal">
            <li class="js-is-active"><a href="#" class="desktop marketing-button marketing-button--small">Desktop</a></li>
            <li><a href="#" class="iphone marketing-button marketing-button--small">iPhone</a></li>
            <li><a href="#" class="android marketing-button marketing-button--small">Android</a></li>
           </ul>
          </div>
          <div class="desktop changeable-content">
           <ol> 
            <li> From your Shopify admin, click <strong>Online Store</strong>, and then click <strong>Themes</strong>.</li> 
           </ol> 
          </div>
          <div class="iphone changeable-content"> 
           <ol> 
            <li>From the <a href="https://www.shopify.com/install/detect">Shopify app</a>, tap <strong>Store</strong>.</li> 
            <li>Tap the <code>...</code> button beside <strong>Online Store</strong>, and then tap <strong>Manage sales channel</strong>.</li> 
            <li>Tap <strong>Themes</strong>.</li> 
           </ol> 
          </div>
          <div class="android changeable-content"> 
           <ol> 
            <li>From the <a href="https://www.shopify.com/install/detect">Shopify app</a>, tap <strong>Store</strong>.</li> 
            <li>Tap the <code><strong>?</strong></code> button beside <strong>Online Store</strong>, and then tap <strong>Manage sales channel</strong>.</li> 
            <li>Tap <strong>Themes</strong>.</li> 
           </ol> 
          </div> 
          <p> </p>
         </div>
         <p></p> 
         <ol> 
          <li> <p>Find the theme you want to edit, click <strong>Actions</strong>, and then click <strong>Edit code</strong>. </p></li> 
          <li> <p> Click on the <strong>Snippets</strong> folder to expand and view its content.</p></li> 
          <li> <p> Under the Snippets folder, click on <strong>Add a new snippet</strong>.</p></li> 
          <li> <p> Name your new snippet <strong>age-check</strong>, and click <strong>Create snippet</strong>. Your age-check snippet will open in the online code editor.</p> 
           <figure data-image-path="/manual/customize/age-check-snippet.jpg"> 
            <img src="/assets/images/manual/customize/age-check-snippet.jpg?1505497472" alt="The 'Add a new snippet' dialog box, with a highlighted entry field containing the text 'age-check'" width="617" height="213"> 
           </figure></li> 
          <li> <p> In a new browser tab, open the following <a href="https://github.com/carolineschnapp/age-check/blob/master/age-check.liquid">file</a>.</p></li> 
          <li> <p> Copy all of the code you see there and return to your <a class="tooltip" data-tooltip-label="Opens the Themes page page of your Shopify admin" href="//www.shopify.com/admin/themes">Themes page</a>.</p></li> 
          <li> <p> In the online code editor, paste the code you copied into your <code>age-check.liquid</code> snippet.</p></li> 
          <li> <p> <strong>Save</strong> your changes.</p></li> 
          <li> <p> In the <strong>Layouts</strong> folder, locate and click on your <code>theme.liquid</code> file to open it in the online code editor.</p></li> 
          <li> <p>In the online code editor, scroll down a bit until you see the opening <strong>&lt;body&gt;</strong> tag.</p></li> 
          <li> <p>Right after the opening <strong>&lt;body&gt;</strong> tag, paste the following code:</p> <pre class="highlight liquid"><code>
<span class="p">{%</span><span class="w"> </span><span class="nt">include</span><span class="w"> </span><span class="s1">'age-check'</span><span class="w"> </span><span class="p">%}</span>

</code></pre> <p>This will include the <code>age-check.liquid</code> snippet at the very beginning of the <strong>body</strong> content of your theme file.</p></li> 
          <li> <p>Click <strong>Save</strong> to save your changes.</p></li> 
         </ol> 
         <p>To see the age verification in action, take a look at this <a href="http://adult-only-3.myshopify.com">demo store</a>.</p> 
         <h2 id="what-if-i-want-to-show-an-image-in-the-background">What if I want to show an image in the background?</h2> 
         <p>Upload a large JPG image (at least 1024 by 1024 pixels) to your theme assets on the Edit HTML/CSS page. Rename that file so that its name is exactly:</p> 
         <pre class="highlight html"><code>age-check-background.jpg
</code></pre> 
         <h2 id="changing-the-age-limit">Changing the age limit</h2> 
         <p>In your <code>age-check.liquid</code> snippet, change the following code on line number 2:</p> 
         <pre class="highlight liquid"><code>
<span class="p">{%</span><span class="w"> </span><span class="nt">assign</span><span class="w"> </span><span class="nv">age</span><span class="w"> </span><span class="o">=</span><span class="w"> </span><span class="mi">18</span><span class="w"> </span><span class="p">%}</span>

</code></pre> 
         <p>to:</p> 
         <pre class="highlight liquid"><code>
<span class="p">{%</span><span class="w"> </span><span class="nt">assign</span><span class="w"> </span><span class="nv">age</span><span class="w"> </span><span class="o">=</span><span class="w"> </span><span class="mi">21</span><span class="w"> </span><span class="p">%}</span>

</code></pre> 
         <p>You can also edit the text on lines 6 and 10.</p> 
         <h2 id="what-if-i-want-to-add-a-date-of-birth-picker">What if I want to add a date of birth picker?</h2> 
         <p>In your <code>age-check.liquid</code> snippet, change the following code on line number 1:</p> 
         <pre class="highlight liquid"><code>
<span class="p">{%</span><span class="w"> </span><span class="nt">assign</span><span class="w"> </span><span class="nv">enter_date_of_birth</span><span class="w"> </span><span class="o">=</span><span class="w"> </span><span class="kc">false</span><span class="w"> </span><span class="p">%}</span>

</code></pre> 
         <p>to:</p> 
         <pre class="highlight liquid"><code>
<span class="p">{%</span><span class="w"> </span><span class="nt">assign</span><span class="w"> </span><span class="nv">enter_date_of_birth</span><span class="w"> </span><span class="o">=</span><span class="w"> </span><span class="kc">true</span><span class="w"> </span><span class="p">%}</span>

</code></pre> 
         <h2 id="testing-the-functionality">Testing the functionality</h2> 
         <p>Once you have clicked on the Enter link, you are not prompted about your age again for another 2 weeks. To bring on the age verification again, you need to manually delete a cookie called <strong>isAnAdult</strong>. To delete cookies, you can use <a href="https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/edit-this-cookie/fngmhnnpilhplaeedifhccceomclgfbg">the following extension in Google Chrome</a>.</p> 
         <h2 id="if-customers-disable-javascript">If customers disable JavaScript</h2> 
         <p>If you want to keep out customers that have JavaScript disabled, add the following to the head section of your <code>theme.liquid</code> file in the Edit HTML/CSS page:</p> 
         <pre class="highlight html"><code><span class="nt">&lt;noscript&gt;</span>
<span class="nt">&lt;meta</span> <span class="na">http-equiv=</span><span class="s">"refresh"</span> <span class="na">content=</span><span class="s">"1; url=/pages/age-check"</span> <span class="nt">/&gt;</span>
<span class="nt">&lt;/noscript&gt;</span>
</code></pre> 
         <p>Replace 'age-check' with the handle of the page you want to redirect to on your website.</p>
