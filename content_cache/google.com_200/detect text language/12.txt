  <h1>Languages with JAWS and MAGic on the Internet</h1> 
  <p><acronym title="Job Access With Speech">JAWS</acronym> and MAGic provide language switching with any supported speech synthesizer. When a Web page author indicates the language of an entire page, or just part of a page, if the synthesizer in use supports that language, JAWS or MAGic use the language specific version of the synthesizer to speak the text. The synthesizer must support the language of the information.</p> 
  <p>If the synthesizer does not support the language specified for a page or part of a page, JAWS (or MAGic) still indicates the language of the information.</p> 
  <p>To demonstrate automatic language detection, we start in English and write:<br> "My computer speaks English."</p> 
  <p>Then we write the same sentence in French,<br> <span lang="fr" xml:lang="fr">mon ordinateur parle français</span> <br> and then in Spanish,<br> <span lang="es" xml:lang="es">mi computadora habla español</span> <br> and in Portuguese,<br> <span lang="pt" xml:lang="pt">meu computador fala o português </span><br> and the same in German,<br> <span lang="de" xml:lang="de">mein computer spricht deutsch</span> <br> and in Russian,<br> <span lang="ru" xml:lang="ru">&#x41c;&#x43e;&#x439; &#x43a;&#x43e;&#x43c;&#x43f;&#x44c;&#x44e;&#x442;&#x435;&#x440; &#x433;&#x43e;&#x432;&#x43e;&#x440;&#x438;&#x442; &#x43f;&#x43e;-&#x440;&#x443;&#x441;&#x441;&#x43a;&#x438;</span> <br> and in Finnish,<br> <span lang="fi" xml:lang="fi">Tietokoneeni puhuu suomea</span> <br> and in Italian,<br> <span lang="it" xml:lang="it">il mio computer parla italiano</span> <br> and in Greek,<br> <span lang="el" xml:lang="el"> &#x39f; &#x3c5;&#x3c0;&#x3bf;&#x3bb;&#x3bf;&#x3b3;&#x3b9;&#x3c3;&#x3c4;&#x3ae;&#x3c2; &#x3bc;&#x3bf;&#x3c5; &#x3bc;&#x3b7;&#x3bb;&#x3ac;&#x3b5;&#x3b9; &#x3b5;&#x3bb;&#x3bb;&#x3b7;&#x3bd;&#x3b9;&#x3ba;&#x3ac;,</span> <br> and in Polish,<br> <span lang="pl" xml:lang="pl">Mój komputer mówi po polsku</span><br> and in Chinese,<br> <span lang="zh-cn" xml:lang="zh-cn">&#x6211;&#x7684;&#x96fb;&#x8166;&#x8aaa;&#x4e2d;&#x6587;</span><br> </p> 
  <p><acronym title="Job Access With Speech">JAWS</acronym> or MAGic switch between the languages very smoothly, when they can. If JAWS or MAGic only said, "Russian," "Greek," or "Polish," instead of reading the text in those languages, this indicates that your speech synthesizer (Eloquence, for example) does not support those languages. If you are using Vocalizer Expressive, it indicates that you do not have those particular voices installed. Please visit the <a href="http://www.freedomscientific.com/Downloads/Synthesizers" target="_blank">Synthesizers download page</a> to locate more free voices and languages for your use.</p>
