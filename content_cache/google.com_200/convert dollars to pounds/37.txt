        <h1 class="page-header__title-group"> <span class="page-header__section"></span><span class="page-header__title">Currencies</span> </h1>

      <hr class="page-rule"> 
      <article class="flex--constrained main-content js-outlet js-reference-view"> 
       <div class="message message--note">
        <span class="message__title message__title--note">note</span>
        <p class="markdown-p">The following currency types are supported in the Braintree API. Available currencies will differ depending on your account setup and the country your company is located in. <a href="https://articles.braintreepayments.com/risk-and-security/compliance/prohibited-transactions">Restrictions that prohibit transactions from specific countries</a> may also be in place.</p> 
        <p class="markdown-p"><a href="https://articles.braintreepayments.com/get-started/currencies">Learn more about currencies and how to accept multiple currencies.</a></p>
