          <h1> Foreign Exchange Services</h1> 
          <p class="buttonheading"> <img src="/templates/ByondStandardCategory/images/trans.gif" alt="Attention: open in a new window."> <a href="/index.php?view=article&amp;catid=6%3Ageneral&amp;id=665%3Afinancial-services--foreign-exchange-services&amp;tmpl=component&amp;print=1&amp;layout=default&amp;page=&amp;option=com_content&amp;Itemid=715" title="Print" onclick="window.open(this.href,'win2','status=no,toolbar=no,scrollbars=yes,titlebar=no,menubar=no,resizable=yes,width=640,height=480,directories=no,location=no'); return false;" rel="nofollow"><img src="/templates/ByondStandardCategory/images/printButton.png" alt="Print"></a></p> 
          <p> SERVICE ONE can offer you a complete foreign exchange service to ensure you get value for money and quality service.</p> 
          <p> So whether you are taking money, sending money, or receiving money from overseas, we can help. More information on each of these services is offered below.</p> 
          <h2> Telegraphic Transfers</h2> 
          <p> A Telegraphic Transfer transmits funds electronically to an overseas bank account in a specified currency. Funds are deposited directly into the payee’s bank account where they can be easily accessed by the payee.</p> 
          <p> These payments can be made between local and overseas organisations or people and you can send a payment in most major currencies. They are generally faster than drafts as the payee normally receives payment within 24 - 72 hours of the transfer being sent.</p> 
          <h2> Drafts</h2> 
          <p> A Draft is either a bank or company cheque, made out to your nominated payee in a foreign currency. If you have no time constraints, and you are happy using the mail and you don’t need the funds to be paid directly into an account, then a Draft would be suitable.</p> 
          <p> Drafts may be enclosed with letters or invoices and forwarded direct to the payee. A Draft is an economical method of transferring funds to a specific company or person overseas and provides security of payment as it is a non-negotiable instrument and can only be credited to the payee’s bank account unless the payee has endorsed the Draft. Choose from 24 different currencies such as the United States Dollar, Euro Dollar, British Pound, Singapore Dollar and more.</p> 
          <h2> Foreign Cash and Cheques</h2> 
          <p> We can supply foreign cash in over 40 currencies in a variety of denominations and we will convert foreign currency cheques into Australian Dollars (subject to acceptability).</p>
