         <p>Having current accounts in multiple currencies provides an efficient way of banking when you are on the move. As long as the Citigold current account you're using is in the local currency, you can make debit card purchases or withdraw cash from a Citi ATM without incurring transaction or conversion charges.</p>

It's quick to change your card linkage from Sterling to Euros or US Dollars, so you can pay for your breakfast in pounds and your lunch in dollars using your current account. </p>
