   <p>If you embed our plug in onto your website you will acquire such a comfortable tool:</p>

   <p>This plugin will help you to add a convenient and simple BTC/USD converter to your website, so that your customers will not just stay updated regarding the bitcoin exchange rates, but also convert any amount of BTC to USD.</p> 
   <p>The Converter works in two directions. In case you enter a price, you will get a bitcoin quantity on the left field.</p> 
   <p>The module memorizes the converting direction. Thus, if the BTC/USD exchange rate changes, the number you inserted will remain the same.</p>

   <p>All the features are optional. In case of module embedding without any custom settings (Quick Connection), , the converter will appear in its official theme Mining-Profit.com. You can see the example in the top website panel.</p>
