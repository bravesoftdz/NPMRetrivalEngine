     <section id="homeWhy"> 
      <div class="mainWr"> 
       <h1>What are we fighting for?</h1> 
       <section class="fight"> 
        <i></i> 
        <div class="copy"> 
         <h2 id="eliminate-technical-debt">Eliminate technical debt!</h2> 
         <h3 id="let-your-ci-server-do-the-work">Let your CI Server do the work</h3> 
         <p>Maintaining a well-tested codebase is mission-critical.</p> 
         <p>Figuring out where your tests are lacking can be painful. You're already running your tests on a continuous integration server, so shouldn't it be doing the heavy lifting?</p> 
         <p>Coveralls works with your CI server and sifts through your coverage data to find issues you didn't even know you had before they become a problem.</p> 
        </div> 
       </section> 
       <section class="computer"> 
        <div class="copy"> 
         <h2 id="discover-trends">Discover Trends</h2> 
         <h3 id="data-data-data">Data, data, data.</h3> 
         <p>If you're just running your code coverage locally, you won't be able to see changes and trends that occur during your entire development cycle.</p> 
         <p>Coveralls lets you inspect every detail of your coverage with unlimited history.</p> 
        </div> 
        <i></i> 
       </section>

         <h3 id="share-your-sweet-suite-with-the-world">Share your sweet suite with the world.</h3> 
         <p>Display your coverage percentage like a badge of honor with our — well — Badges of Honor. Encourage others to join the movement for code coverage!</p> 
        </div>

       <section class="stomp"> 
        <div class="copy"> 
         <h2 id="stomp-out-bugs">Stomp out bugs!</h2>
