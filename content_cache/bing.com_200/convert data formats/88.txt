        <p style="margin-left: 13px;">In fact, if you have <strong>Kutools for Excel</strong>, you can use its <strong>Split Cells</strong> and<strong> Combine</strong> utilities to convert dd.mm.yyyy to dd/mm/yyyy.</p> 
        <p style="margin-left: 13px;">After <span style="text-decoration: underline;"><em><strong><span style="color: #f655ab; text-decoration: underline;"><a href="/downloads/KutoolsforExcelSetup.exe" onclick="ga('send', 'pageview', '3784-KTE-sun-download-middle.html');"><span style="color: #f655ab; text-decoration: underline;">free installing</span> </a> </span> </strong> </em> </span> Kutools for Excel, please do as below:</p> 
        <p style="margin-left: 13px;">1.         <p style="margin-left: 13px;">2. See screenshot:<br><img alt="doc convert date format 6" src="/images/stories/doc-excel/convert-date-format/xdoc-convert-date-format-6.png.pagespeed.ic.8DQUcIvAYJ.png"></p> 
        <p style="margin-left: 13px;">3. Select the split data, and click<strong> Kutools</strong> &gt; <strong>Combine</strong>.
