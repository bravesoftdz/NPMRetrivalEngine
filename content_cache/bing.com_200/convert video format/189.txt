             Converts between FLV, AVI, MP4, WMV, MPG and other video formats, WAV, MP3, WMA, M4A, OGG and other music formats or JPEG, PNG, ICO, BMP, TIFF and other image formats. Rips the content from DVDs as well as CDs and supports watermarking.
            </div>
            <p>As its name implies, FormatFactory allows you to change the format of different types of audio, video and image files. <br> The program is really easy to use. First, you need to select the format to which you want to convert your files. Then, a window will open, from where you need to choose the files or folders to convert. Then, they will appear on the main screen, where you need to click on the Start button. The conversion process will start. It will take very little time on each file, which is very convenient. This version also includes several advanced features. For example, you can use the program to join two or more audio or video files, and to make audio and video mixes, among other&nbsp;functions.</p> 
            <p>A nice feature of this new version is the possibility of changing the language and the color of the skin of the program. A little disadvantage in comparision to the earlier version is the fact that the Start button now appears on the upper menu and is hard to&nbsp;find.</p> 
            <p>The installation wizard asks you to install additional software. Nevertheless, you can decline to do so and still have FormatFactory installed on your&nbsp;system.</p> 
            <p>If you find the program useful and want to donate, you can do it at the developer's&nbsp;website.</p>
