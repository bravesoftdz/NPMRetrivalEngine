         <h3>Video Pack</h3> 
         <p>Do more with video when you download the Video Pack. Video Pack allows you to convert MPEG-2 and VC-1 files* to high-quality DivX formats for major file size savings.</p> 
         <div style="height:200px;"> 
          <div class="grid-5 alpha"> 
           <img class="left" src="http://elstatico.divx.com/files/images/divx-plus-software/hero-mpeg-2-convert.png">
          </div> 
          <div class="grid-6" style="margin-top:30px;"> 
           <h3> What is MPEG-2?</h3> 
           <p style="line-height:1.4em"> MPEG-2 is a digital video standard upon which DVD and ATSC video (most digital TV broadcasts) is based. MPEG-2 based videos are typically identified by a TS, VOB, MPG or SVCD file extension. Learn more on our <a href="/software/technologies/mpeg2">technologies page</a>.</p> 
          </div> 
         </div> 
         <h3>What is VC-1?</h3> 
         <p>VC-1 is a compression standard for high-definition digital video used in Blu-ray Discs, Windows Media and other streaming formats. VC-1 based videos can be found with M2TS and TS file extensions. Learn more on our <a href="/software/technologies/vc1">technologies page</a>.</p> 
         <h3> Why do I need Video Pack?</h3> 
         <p>If you have stacks of DVDs taking up space on your shelves, going on a road trip and dont want to pack all the kids movies, or want to stream high-definition Blu-ray or Windows Media to your TV or gaming console, Video Pack is for you. It adds support for MPEG-2 video formats (MPG, TS, VOB, SVCD) and VC-1 formats (M2TS, TS) in DivX Converter, which already enables quick and easy conversion of MKV, WMV, AVI, MOV and MP4 files. </p> 
         <p> *DivX Software accepts non-encrypted files only. Files encrypted for copyright protection cannot be converted in DivX products.</p> 
         <p>Simply put, Video Pack gives you more conversion and streaming power by letting you convert more video formats into high-quality, highly-compressed DivX, DivX Plus (MKV), HEVC and MP4 video for playback on a range of devices. </p> 
         <p>Download DivX Software today for free and enjoy a 15-day trial of Video Pack. </p>
