                    <span class="rendered_qtext"><p class="qtext_para">Your business requiremens and skills - this is what defines what shopping cart system is the best for this particular case. Each solution has its pros and cons:</p>

                      <li>Magento has a lot on the board, but probably too much which makes it a bit monstrous and often too slow.</li>
                      <li>Preshashop is faster, but its modules and themes are quite expensive as compared with Magento.</li>
                      <li>X-Cart 5 is in Green zone of Google speed test, it can be customized AND remain pretty upgradable even after multiple custom mods, but still theres is a learning curve which can be difficult for some developers.</li>
                     </ul><p class="qtext_para">You may ask, what X-Cart is doing here. It used to be only a commercial PHP shopping cart, but back in 2013 it went Freemium. Today it has a Free Edition ( lifetime license, lifetime upgrades, unlimited products, includes THE SAME set of shipping and payment gateways integrations, SEO tools, as the commercial plan, but the set of professional marketing tools is not included ) and a paid package called Business ( with best hand-picked marketing modules aboard). You can easily upgrade the Free package to Business in a few clicks for $495, or you can develop the missing features from scratch, as the code is 100% open.</p><p class="qtext_para">As <span class="qlink_container"><a href="https://www.x-cart.com/" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank" onclick="return Q.openUrl(this, 32021948);" class="external_link" data-qt-tooltip="x-cart.com">X-Cart</a></span> employee, I would like to shed more light on its technological side:</p>
