              <p>Gone are the days where consumers restricted their shopping to malls and outlets next to them. The tendency to go online and click to purchase whatever they want has grown exponentially and is assumed to only grow further. Thus, sellers have moved online. They all want to build their online stores, create a store that gives a great and unique experience and not just sell their products.
