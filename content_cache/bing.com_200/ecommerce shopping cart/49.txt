       <p> <span> “The future of shopping is <i>e-commerce</i> .” Yes, it sounds like a cliche but there is no denying that <b>online shopping</b> is rapidly becoming a widely accepted and preferred methods of shopping. The success of general <u>e-commerce</u> portals like eBay and Amazon as well as specialized shopping portals like Etsy is a testimony to the fact that e-commerce is convenient, fast, and reliable way of shopping.&nbsp; </span> No wonder, most retail stores, service providers, financial institutions are shifting to paperless e-commerce environment. </p> 
       <p>The need of e-commerce has become more evident since the induction of social media as a popular form of marketing, introducing and promoting products. More and more businesses are using social media campaigns to attract potential buyers. And the success ratio is noticeable. It just takes a click to get a facebook fan from your facebook page to your online store. Having said that, I cannot emphasize enough on the need for e-commerce for small as well as emerging businesses. It is economical, fast, and convenient way of buying and selling products online.</p> 
       <p> Fortunately, building an <i>online shopping</i> environment is not a bid deal especially if you already have a website. Following is a list of <strong>25+ free e-commerce solutions</strong> that you can use for free. These open source e-commerce provide all major functions that you will require to build an <u>online shopping</u> cart and connect it to a payment processing system. </p>

       <h2>Magento</h2> 
       <p> <img title="e-commerce" alt="  Magento" src="http://img0.tuicool.com/Fneeqa.jpg!web" class="alignCenter"> </p> 
       <p>Magento is by far the most versatile and “heavy weight” e-commerce platform of them all. You have only got to look at Magento’s clients to see they are a big player. Companies such as: Nike, Lindt, Olympus and OfficeMax use Magento to power their online stores. Magento is used by over 30,000 online merchants and is the world’s fastest growing e-commerce platform. The Community Edition is free, but is only advised to be used by developers. The Enterprise Edition is extremely powerful and is jam-packed full of useful and amazing features, but this can be expensive. If you are not a developer, your best bet is to pay a web developer to build a shop using the Community Edition.</p> 
       <h2>PrestaShop</h2> 
       <p> <img title="e-commerce" alt="  PrestaShop" src="http://img0.tuicool.com/QvU7Rr.jpg!web" class="alignCenter"> </p> 
       <p>Powering over 150,000 online shops, PrestaShop is a fast growing free open source e-commerce solution. With 310+ features and over 2,500 modules and templates, PrestaShop is suitable for either big or small e-commerce websites. PrestaShop is proud of its powerful and easy-to-use product management, site management, product displays, and client accounts. PrestaShop also comes with powerful Analytics and Reporting features that goes side by side with marketing and SEO capabilities. Plus, you can easily reach customer support when you need help to resolve issues.</p> 
       <h2>osCommerce</h2> 
       <p> <img title="e-commerce" alt=" osCommerce" src="http://img2.tuicool.com/IV7N7f.jpg!web" class="alignCenter"> </p> 
       <p>osCommerce is one of the older players in the e-commerce game and has proven to be a very popular choice for people looking to set up their shops online powering over 228,700 online stores. The set-up and running of an osCommerce store is incredibly easy and with all of the themes and plugins out there, it enables non-developers to set up a shop with little or no help. Although this can be a drawback, as it is hard to differentiate your site from others using the same theme.</p> 
       <h2>Free E-commerce Platform built with PHP, jQuery and HTML5 – LiteCart</h2> 
       <p> <img title="e-commerce" alt="  Free E-commerce Platform built with PHP, jQuery and HTML5 - LiteCart" src="http://img2.tuicool.com/7Rfe2q.jpg!web" class="alignCenter"> </p> 
       <p>LiteCart is a free and open source shopping cart application that is built with PHP-MySQL. LiteCart relies on the latest HyperText standard HTML 5, the latest CSS 3 for styling, the amazing jQuery framework for client-side dynamics, and the well popular web scripting language PHP for server-side dynamics.</p> 
       <h2>OpenCart</h2> 
       <p> <img title="e-commerce" alt="  OpenCart" src="http://img0.tuicool.com/YZFfm2.jpg!web" class="alignCenter"> </p> 
       <p>OpenCart is a very scalable, powerful e-commerce platform that can power an online shop of any size. You can create an unlimited number of categories, sell an unlimited number of products, accept multiple currencies, use multiple languages, choose from over 20 payment and 8 shipping methods. OpenCart is also know to be very search engine friendly so requires less work to achieve a good ranking in the search engines. One drawback to OpenCart is that it’s lesser know than other e-commerce platforms, therefore documentation and help may be hard to come by.</p> 
       <h2>Open Source E-Commerce App To Create Online Store With Ease: Uni-eCart</h2> 
       <p> <img title="e-commerce" alt="   Open Source E-Commerce App To Create Online Store With Ease: Uni-eCart" src="http://img0.tuicool.com/YFRZNz.jpg!web" class="alignCenter"> </p> 
       <p>Uni-eCart is a free and open source e-commerce application built with PHP. The application supports multiple payment gateways, can display related-items, allows users to discuss + comment and share items on social networks. A detailed admin panel helps managing the whole site easily and stores with any design can be crated with the support for theming.</p> 
       <h2>Open Source PHP E-commerce Solution – Sylius</h2> 
       <p> <img title="e-commerce" alt="  Open Source PHP E-commerce Solution – Sylius" src="http://img1.tuicool.com/fM7jui.jpg!web" class="alignCenter"> </p> 
       <p>Sylius is an open source and free e-commerce solution for PHP (and based on Symfony2). Sylius is free, open-source, well tested, has an extensible architecture, clean code and robust features.</p> 
       <h2>Spree Commerce</h2> 
       <p> <img title="e-commerce" alt="  Spree Commerce" src="http://img1.tuicool.com/e63YF3.jpg!web" class="alignCenter"> </p> 
       <p>Spree is an open-source ecommerce platform for Ruby on Rails. Using Spree’s extension system, you’ll be able to customise your store and mark yourself out from your competitors. Useful features include support for over 50 payment gateways, single page checkout and custom tax logic, which can save merchants lots of time and effort. It also comes with Google Analytics built in.</p> 
       <h2>Open Source PHP E-Commerce Software – Forward</h2> 
       <p> <img title="e-commerce" alt="   Open Source PHP E-Commerce Software – Forward" src="http://img0.tuicool.com/3mAJVv.jpg!web" class="alignCenter"> </p> 
       <p>The open source platform for custom e-commerce — with MongoDB. Forward is a full-featured, developer centric e-commerce platform that makes custom code easy, with powerful templates &amp; expressive syntax. It has a REST-like API for interacting with every feature from orders to customers or products.</p> 
       <h2>VirtueMart</h2> 
       <p> <img title="e-commerce" alt="  VirtueMart " src="http://img1.tuicool.com/ERzeYvZ.jpg!web" class="alignCenter"> </p> 
       <p>VirtueMart, which has been designed to work alongside Joomla!, is a really neat cart which customers feel instantly comfortable with. Not only does it let customers buy things, it lets them create an account, add addresses and access their order history. Multiple languages and currencies, and unlimited products and categories are supported. 2.5 million people have downloaded VirtueMart so far and the vast majority is happy with the results.</p> 
       <h2>Tutorial: An AJAX Based Shopping Cart with PHP, CSS &amp; jQuery</h2> 
       <p> <img title="e-commerce" alt="     Tutorial: An AJAX Based Shopping Cart with PHP, CSS &amp; jQuery" src="http://img1.tuicool.com/e6Nf6r.jpg!web" class="alignCenter"> </p> 
       <p>In this tutorial we are going to create an AJAX-driven shopping cart. All the products are going to be stored in a MySQL database, with PHP showing and processing the data.</p> 
       <h2>AspxCommerce – Open Source ASP.NET Shopping Cart</h2> 
       <p> <img title="e-commerce" alt="     AspxCommerce - Open Source ASP.NET Shopping Cart" src="http://img2.tuicool.com/vqyANf.jpg!web" class="alignCenter"> </p> 
       <p>AspxCommerce offers a complete e-Commerce solution that allows you to build and run your fully functional online store in minutes. You can create your storefront; manage the products through categories and subcategories, accept payments through credit cards and ship the ordered products to the customers.</p> 
       <p>AspxCommerce is enriched with several features which you can use to build and run your own online store. We have taken care of every little feature that you need. Therefore, once you download AspxCommerce, you can straight away focus on building your online store.</p> 
       <h2>Open Source ASP.NET Shopping Cart: nopCommerce</h2> 
       <p> <img title="e-commerce" alt="     Open Source ASP.NET Shopping Cart: nopCommerce" src="http://img1.tuicool.com/jmqu6j.jpg!web" class="alignCenter"> </p> 
       <p>nopCommerce is an open source shopping cart software that is built with ASP.NET &amp; uses MSSQL for storing the data. It comes with a powerful backend &amp; highly customizable frontend.</p> 
       <h2>Free ASP.NET Shopping Cart: dashCommerce</h2> 
       <p> <img title="e-commerce" alt="     Free ASP.NET Shopping Cart: dashCommerce" src="http://img1.tuicool.com/BjABNf.jpg!web" class="alignCenter"> </p> 
       <p>dashCommerce is a flexible and robust ASP.NET Open Source e-commerce application that you can use to set up your online store. dashCommerce has been available since early 2008 and has been used in countless online stores and is wildly successful.</p> 
       <h2>Ubercart</h2> 
       <p> <img title="e-commerce" alt="   Ubercart" src="http://img0.tuicool.com/RfU73i.jpg!web" class="alignCenter"> </p> 
       <p>Ubercart is specifically designed for people selling things like file downloads, event registrations, website access passes and event tickets, rather than physical products. Like VirtueMart, which must be integrated with Joomla!, Ubercart must be integrated with Drupal. Drupal users will feel comfortable choosing the ideal modules and themes to customise their shop with, but if you’re not a Drupal user, I suggest choosing a platform that’s a little more straightforward.</p> 
       <h2>Open Source PHP E-Commerce Framework (Based on Zend and Symphony 2) – Enlight</h2> 
       <p> <img title="e-commerce" alt="    Open Source PHP E-Commerce Framework (Based on Zend and Symphony 2) - Enlight" src="http://img1.tuicool.com/BRJz2u.jpg!web" class="alignCenter"> </p> 
       <p>Enlight is an open source framework, based on Zend and Symphony 2, for creating e-commerce applications easier and faster. It is built by the experienced e-commerce company Shopware and also powers the base behind their commercial product.</p> 
       <h2>Zeuscart</h2> 
       <p> <img title="e-commerce" alt="Zeuscart" src="http://img0.tuicool.com/uEf2Az.jpg!web" class="alignCenter"> </p> 
       <p>The best thing about Zeuscart is its user interface, which is rich, attractive, user-friendly and generally less boring than most of the open-source UIs that I have to look at on a daily basis. Designed with small and medium businesses in mind, users can make the most of SEO friendly URLs, gift cards, discounts, email templates and tier-pricing, which makes it possible to decrease prices for bulk orders.</p> 
       <h2>CodeIgniter-Powered Open Source E-Commerce Software – GoCart</h2> 
       <p> <img title="e-commerce" alt="  CodeIgniter-Powered Open Source E-Commerce Software - GoCart" src="http://img0.tuicool.com/6v6faa.jpg!web" class="alignCenter"> </p> 
       <p>GoCart is an open source e-commerce application which is focused on offering an easy to use/customize solution and not having a huge number of features but only the important ones.</p> 
       <p>The built-in shipping methods include a flat or table rate, USPS, FedEx + UPS and has support for Paypal, Authorize.net and “COD (collect on delivery)” payment methods.</p> 
       <h2>Free Shopping Cart Software – AFCommerce</h2> 
       <p> <img title="e-commerce" alt="   Free Shopping Cart Software - AFCommerce " src="http://img0.tuicool.com/QbaqAn.jpg!web" class="alignCenter"> </p> 
       <p>AFCommerce is a full and complete online store with both a store front and administration area directly Out-Of-The-Box, which can be easily installed, configured, and maintained over a web-based interface. AFCommerce now includes our Ecommerce Website Building Software integrated with our free shopping cart. This allows you to create an unlimited number of custom web pages, customize font / colors of all your pages, as well as, the structural layout of your website.</p> 
       <p>It has a speedy one-page checkout, customer help pop-up windows and customer accounts are generated automatically from orders.</p> 
       <h2>Open Source Shopping Cart Application – LiveCart</h2> 
       <p> <img title="e-commerce" alt="    Open Source Shopping Cart Application - LiveCart" src="http://img0.tuicool.com/me6bmy.jpg!web" class="alignCenter"> </p> 
       <p>LiveCart is an open source shopping cart application that comes with many features to create simple-to-advanced e-commerce websites. LiveCart allows to change and tweak the look of store pages with unseen ease. The flexible architecture enables custom changes of any complexity. It offers a surprisingly powerful and effective admin backend, which puts you in complete control of every part of your store.</p> 
       <p>LiveCart is perfectly suited for local markets. Everything can be presented in multiple languages, and products can be priced in several currencies.</p> 
       <h2>JavaScript Shopping Cart Framework – SimpleCart JS</h2> 
       <p> <img title="e-commerce" alt="    JavaScript Shopping Cart Framework - SimpleCart JS" src="http://img0.tuicool.com/7ZVnM3.jpg!web" class="alignCenter"> </p> 
       <p>A free and open-source javascript shopping cart that easily integrates with your current website. No databases, no programming, no headaches. A simple javascript shopping cart that you can setup in minutes. It’s lightweight, fast, simple to use, and completely customizable. All you need to know is basic HTML.</p> 
       <p>The simpleCart(js) free core comes with the ability to checkout with paypal express, google checkout, and amazon payments. We’re always working on adding new payment gateways and checkout options.</p> 
       <h2>AJAX Shopping Cart with Paypal &amp; Google Checkout – Easy Basket</h2> 
       <p> <img title="e-commerce" alt="   AJAX Shopping Cart with Paypal &amp; Google Checkout – Easy Basket" src="http://img2.tuicool.com/ZvMjai.jpg!web" class="alignCenter"> </p> 
       <p>Easy Basket is a free shopping cart application which enables us to sell items inside any web page. EasyBasket takes a different approach at creating a shopping cart; although technically complex in its execution, it is extremely simple for the user to install. With EasyBasket you can quickly insert a shopping cart anywhere on your web page with a single line of code. No need to install a database or a dozen class files. Google Checkout and PayPal Checkout are integrated into the code, all you need to do is change the credentials and your cart is ready to go.</p> 
       <h2>Free PHP/Ajax Shopping Cart -jCart</h2> 
       <p> <img title="e-commerce" alt="   Free PHP/Ajax Shopping Cart -jCart" src="http://img0.tuicool.com/aIbEve.jpg!web" class="alignCenter"> </p> 
       <p>jCart is a free Ajax shopping cart that’s easy to install and customize. Based on jQuery and PHP, jCart handles visitor input without reloading the page and is fully functional even without javascript.</p> 
       <h2>Tomato Cart</h2> 
       <p> <img title="e-commerce" alt="  Tomato Cart" src="http://img0.tuicool.com/bYZjAz.jpg!web" class="alignCenter"> </p> 
       <p>TomatoCart is the new generation of open source shopping cart solution. It is branched from osCommerce 3 as a separate project. As web applications become more and more sophisticated, modern web 2.0 technology such as Ajax and Rich Internet Applications offers significant usability improvements and makes interacting with the web interfaces faster and more efficient.</p> 
       <h2>A Free ECommerce Solution for Building Online Stores – LiteCommerce</h2> 
       <p> <img title="e-commerce" alt="   A Free ECommerce Solution for Building Online Stores - LiteCommerce" src="http://img1.tuicool.com/rQVJBb.jpg!web" class="alignCenter"> </p> 
       <p>LiteCommerce is an open source and free e-commerce application that integrates with Drupal to make use of the powerful CMS capabilities. Unlimited number of products or categories can be created, a product can belong to multiple categories and it has support for inventory tracking.</p> 
       <h2>Shopping Cart for Joomla – RokQuickCart</h2> 
       <p> <img title="e-commerce" alt="    Shopping Cart for Joomla - RokQuickCart" src="http://img2.tuicool.com/UbeiYv.jpg!web" class="alignCenter"> </p> 
       <p>RokQuickCart is a very, very simple cart for Joomla!. Its simplicity, however, is both a help and a hindrance as although it can be set up in minutes, it lacks some features which you’ll find in other platforms in this list, for example, it only accepts payments through PayPal and Google Checkout. Having said that, it generally looks good and product image display is impressive.</p> 
       <h2>Digistore – Free Open Source Ecommerce</h2> 
       <p> <img title="e-commerce" alt="     Digistore - Free Open Source Ecommerce" src="http://img0.tuicool.com/faABZbJ.jpg!web" class="alignCenter"> </p> 
       <p>Digistore is free, open source e-commerce software package based on OScommerce. It allows users to be ‘live’ and selling only minutes after installation is complete. Digistore is a full content management system that requires no HTML or programming knowledge to operate. Now available for download completely free of charge under the General Public License Agreement.</p>
