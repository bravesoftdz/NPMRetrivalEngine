        <h2 id="description-header">Description</h2> 
        <p>Since 2006, we’ve been helping entrepreneurs just like you realize their goal of selling online. Whether you’re hoping to sell digital downloads, physical inventory, or subscriptions and memberships – with WP eCommerce, you’re only minutes away from your next sale.</p> 
        <ul> 
         <li>Make the plugin your own with CSS and HTML customizations</li> 
         <li>Learn quickly with our <a href="http://docs.wpecommerce.org/category/video-tutorials/" rel="nofollow">video tutorials</a></li> 
         <li>Integration with <a href="https://wpecommerce.org/store/" rel="nofollow">dozens of different payment gateways</a></li> 
         <li>Integrate with many popular WordPress plugins</li> 
         <li>Use built-in marketing tools to sell more</li> 
         <li>Shipping is easy with built in shipping tools and integration with popular couriers</li> 
         <li>Secure check-out with SSL</li> 
         <li>Manage your orders and catalogues with powerful built-in tools</li> 
         <li>Extend your eCommerce site with <a href="https://wpecommerce.org/store/" rel="nofollow">additional modules</a></li> 
         <li>Integrate with WordPress multisite</li> 
         <li>With hundreds of available hooks, developers can make WP eCommerce do anything you can imagine</li> 
        </ul>

        <h2 id="installation-header">Installation</h2> 
        <ol> 
         <li>Upload the folder ‘wp-e-commerce’ to the ‘/wp-content/plugins/’ directory</li> 
         <li>Activate the plugin through the ‘Plugins’ menu in WordPress</li> 
        </ol> 
        <h4>Updating</h4> 
        <p>Before updating please make a backup of your existing files and database. Just in case.<br> After upgrading from earlier versions look for link “Update Store”. This will update your database structure to work with new version.</p>
       </div> 
       <div id="faq" class="plugin-faq section "> 
        <h2 id="faq-header">FAQ</h2> 
        <dl> 
         <dt>
          Installation Instructions
         </dt> 
         <dd> 
          <ol> 
           <li>Upload the folder ‘wp-e-commerce’ to the ‘/wp-content/plugins/’ directory</li> 
           <li>Activate the plugin through the ‘Plugins’ menu in WordPress</li> 
          </ol> 
          <h4>Updating</h4> 
          <p>Before updating please make a backup of your existing files and database. Just in case.<br> After upgrading from earlier versions look for link “Update Store”. This will update your database structure to work with new version.</p> 
         </dd> 
         <dt>
          How do I customize WP eCommerce
         </dt> 
         <dd> 
          <p>First of all, you should check out the Presentation settings which are in the Settings -&gt; Store page.</p> 
          <p>For advanced users, all of our eCommerce templates and CSS can be moved into your active theme folder for customization. Less advanced users have <a href="https://wpecommerce.org/hire-an-expert/" rel="nofollow">several fantastic WP eCommerce consultants</a> to get help from.</p> 
         </dd> 
         <dt>
          What payment gateways does WP eCommerce integrate with?
         </dt> 
         <dd> 
          <p>Our <a href="https://wpecommerce.org/store/premium-plugins/gold-cart/" rel="nofollow">Gold Cart plugin</a> contains nearly a dozen payment gateway options, including Authorize.net, LinkPoint and DPS. Our <a href="https://wpecommerce.org/store/premium-plugins/mijireh-99-payment-gateways-pack/" rel="nofollow">Mirijeh integration plugin</a> provides access to nearly one hundred different gateway options. Beyond that, we have individual integration plugins for <a href="https://wpecommerce.org/store/product/paypal-powered-by-braintree/" rel="nofollow">Braintree</a>, <a href="https://wpecommerce.org/store/premium-plugins/stripe/" rel="nofollow">Stripe</a>, <a href="https://wpecommerce.org/store/premium-plugins/authorize-net-sim-payment-gateway-for-wordpress/" rel="nofollow">Authorize.net SIM</a> and more.</p> 
         </dd> 
         <dt>
          I want to integrate a payment gateway that you don’t support. Can you help with that?
         </dt> 
         <dd> 
          <p>Absolutely! We have a <a href="https://wpecommerce.org/hire-an-expert/" rel="nofollow">growing list of trusted consultants</a> who would be happy to partner with you to build a custom gateway. Alternatively, you’re more than welcome to request that we build it as a plugin or part of our core plugin. The more requests we get, the more likely we are to build it. If you happen to be a talented developer, we have a great documentation site with helpful information on building payment gateways</p> 
         </dd> 
         <dt>
          Which couriers does the plugin integrate with?
         </dt> 
         <dd> 
          <p>We have table rate, weight rate and flat rate shipping calculators.</p> 
          <p>We also currently integrate with UPS, USPS, Australia Post and Shipwire for real-time shipping rates. We have a premium plugin for <a href="https://wpecommerce.org/store/product/fedex-shipping-module/" rel="nofollow">FedEx</a> as well.</p> 
         </dd> 
         <dt>
          Which multi-lingual plugins is WP eCommerce compatible with?
         </dt> 
         <dd> 
          <p>We currently provide basic support for WPML and qTranslate. We’re excited about an up-and-coming multi-lingual project called <a href="https://github.com/cftp/babble" rel="nofollow">Babble</a>. We intend to fully and canonically support Babble as our primary multi-lingual partner in releases to come.</p> 
         </dd> 
         <dt>
          Which additional modules are available for use with WP eCommerce?
         </dt> 
         <dd> 
          <ul> 
           <li><a href="https://wpecommerce.org/store/premium-plugins/gold-cart/" rel="nofollow">Gold Cart</a> – adds more options, gateways and functionality to your store</li> 
           <li><a href="https://wpecommerce.org/store/premium-plugins/mp3-player-plugin/" rel="nofollow">MP3 Audio Player</a> – Preview audio clips on your website</li> 
           <li><a href="https://wpecommerce.org/store/product/membership-subscriptions/" rel="nofollow">Members Plugin</a> – Integrates with PayPal Standard and Authorize.Net, allows you to create a Subscription-Based Product, as well as a “Members-Only” website with paid access to restricted content.</li> 
          </ul> 
         </dd>

          How do you provide support?
         </dt> 
         <dd> 
          <p>Each Addon comes with a license key that you can use to receive high-touch, 1-on-1 support from our trained support staff for any issues related to the addon in question. If you submit a support request here, you’ll get a helpful response within 24 hours or less (during business hours).</p> 
          <p>You can also receive premium support for general WP eCommerce issue if you purchase and have a valid Premium Support subscription (https://wpecommerce.org/premium-support-pricing/)</p> 
          <p>For the rest of our users, we do our best to monitor the WordPress.org support forums and respond in as timely a manner as we are able. We appreciate the countless helpful volunteers who also take time to respond to forum posts here.</p>
