      <h1 itemprop="headline" class="single-title">Free Shopping Cart, Payment and Other E-commerce Icons</h1> 
      <div id="single-meta"> 
       <span> <span itemprop="author" itemscope itemtype="http://schema.org/Person"> <span itemprop="name" class="author-name"><a href="https://designmodo.com/author/adrian/" title="Posts by Andrian" rel="author" data-wpel-link="internal">Andrian</a></span> &nbsp;&nbsp;•&nbsp;&nbsp;<a href="https://designmodo.com/resources/icons-resources/" rel="category tag" data-wpel-link="internal">Icons</a>&nbsp;&nbsp;•&nbsp;&nbsp; </span> <span><span itemprop="datePublished" content="2016-05-10">May 10, 2016</span> &nbsp;&nbsp;•&nbsp;&nbsp;<a href="https://designmodo.com/ecommerce-icons/#respond" data-wpel-link="internal">0 Comments</a> </span> </span> 
       <meta itemprop="dateModified" content="September 13, 2017"> 
       <meta itemscope itemprop="mainEntityOfPage" itemType="https://schema.org/WebPage" itemid="https://google.com/article"> 
      </div>
      <!--end single meta--> 
      <span itemprop="articleBody"> 
       <div id="adsense-top"> 
        <!-- BuySellAds Zone Code --> 
        <div id="bsap_1304901" class="bsarocks bsap_7c274c44627d36b4e9e91f6d789f59f4"></div> 
        <!-- End BuySellAds Zone Code --> 
       </div>
       <!--end adsense-top--> <p>The friendlier the interface to targeted audiences, the better chance you will have to <strong>increase conversion rates and generate greater profit</strong>. Icons play a vital role in creating a comfortable environment for potential customers. They indicate function and unambiguously communicate meaning. Not only do they look coherent across various devices providing consistency in style, but they also add to the aesthetics, making design eye-pleasing, intuitive and modern. They can speed up surfing by offering visitors visual cues.</p> <p>There are many benefits of putting the effort into choosing icons that are active, meaningful and intentional.</p> <p>We have gathered 20 free and <strong>fresh bundles of e-commerce icons</strong>, in several trendy styles. As a rule, each package includes common elements such as shopping carts, bags, credit cards, receipts, currencies, money, baskets, and tags, yet there are some that will surprise you with original items.</p> <h2>Free E-commerce Icons</h2> <h3>Responsive E-Commerce Icon Set</h3> 
       <div class="figure">
