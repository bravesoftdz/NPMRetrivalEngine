       <h1 class="blog-title entry-title">20 Best E-commerce WordPress Plugins of 2014</h1> 
       <div class="entry entry-content post-entry blog-post-content clr">
        <p>What can’t you possibly do with WordPress? You can create a nice tiny blog for your animal friend – the dog. You can build an impressive professional website complete with a portfolio to woo clients. Big brands&nbsp;have a thing for WordPress too, and the best part is&nbsp;you can set up an e-commerce store (e-store) on the platform as well.</p> 
        <p>WordPress is no longer just a blogging platform, <a title="History of WordPress: The Good, The Bad &amp; The Ugly" href="http://www.wpexplorer.com/history-wordpress/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">it has come of age</a>. Speak of new and exciting functions, and it hasn’t stopped evolving yet.</p> 
        <p>So, what exactly do you need to transform WordPress into a <strong>fully-fledged</strong> digital shop? Fifty points to you if your first guess was&nbsp;<strong>plugins!&nbsp;</strong>You need a few e-commerce plugins, an appealing e-commerce theme and, of course, great products.</p> 
        <p>In today’s post, we will explore twenty e-commerce plugins you need to build and run your own successful e-store. Enjoy!</p> 
        <h2>The Shopping Cart</h2> 
        <p>The first step to building an e-store involves priming your WordPress platform. You need to transform WordPress from a mere blogging software into an e-commerce engine that &nbsp;will showcase products, collect orders and ultimately, take money from people. ?</p> 
        <p>The following ten (10) WordPress e-commerce plugins will help you set up shop in no time.</p> 
        <h3>WooCommerce</h3> 
        <p><a title="Courtesy Of WordPress.org" href="https://wordpress.org/plugins/woocommerce/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><img class="fullimg" src="http://www.wpexplorer.com/wp-content/uploads/woocommerce-ecommerce-plugin-for-wordpress-wpexplorer.png" alt="woocommerce-ecommerce-plugin-for-wordpress-wpexplorer" width="676" height="216"></a></p> 
        <p>With over two (2) million downloads and an impressive rating of 4.1/5.0 (<a title="Courtesy Of WordPress.org" href="https://wordpress.org/plugins/woocommerce/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">courtesy of WordPress.org</a>), WooCommerce is the plugin you need to build and run your online business.</p> 
        <p>According to WooThemes – the guys behind the plugin – WooCommerce will “…transform your WordPress website into a thoroughbred e-commerce store.” It’s not only powerful, but also very extendable, which gives you the power to sell any product under the sun in a beautiful way.</p> 
        <p>This WordPress e-commerce plugin is popular for the following features:</p> 
        <ul> 
         <li>Flexibility and strength since it’s built around WordPress best practices</li> 
         <li>An intuitive dashboard with widgets and detailed reports to help you keep track of sales, stock, store performance and statistics</li> 
         <li>Fully customizable</li> 
         <li>Fully compatible with WordPress themes</li> 
         <li>WordPress 3.8 compatible</li> 
         <li>PayPal gateway</li> 
         <li><a title="Mijireh Checkout" href="http://www.mijireh.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Mijireh</a> checkout to secure all payments</li> 
         <li>Several payment options including credit cards, cash on delivery and cheque</li> 
         <li>Google Analytics</li> 
         <li>Social sharing powered by ShareThis and Sharedaddy</li> 
         <li>Et cetera</li> 
        </ul> 
        <p>WooCommerce is free but you can purchase powerful extensions on <a title="Code Canyon Premium WordPress Plugins" href="http://www.wpexplorer.com/code-canyon" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">CodeCanyon</a>.</p> 
        <p><a title="Courtesy Of WordPress.org" href="https://wordpress.org/plugins/woocommerce/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Download WooCommerce</a></p> 
        <h3>WP E-COMMERCE</h3> 
        <p><a title="WP e-Commerce WordPress Plugin" href="https://wordpress.org/plugins/wp-e-commerce/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><img class="fullimg" src="http://www.wpexplorer.com/wp-content/uploads/wp-e-commerce-plugin-for-wordpress-wpexplorer.png" alt="wp-e-commerce-plugin-for-wordpress-wpexplorer" width="674" height="216"></a></p> 
        <p>With over 2.6 million downloads, WP e-commerce beats WooCommerce in popularity, but with a rating of 2.8 out of 5 stars, it has very little (or nothing) on the latter in terms of functionality.</p> 
        <p>However, WP e-commerce has some great features such as:</p> 
        <ul> 
         <li>Seamless integration to WordPress</li> 
         <li>Compatibility with most WordPress themes and other plugins</li> 
         <li>WordPress widget support</li> 
         <li>Shortcode and template tag features</li> 
         <li>Works out of the box</li> 
         <li>Compatible with WordPress and WordPress Multisite</li> 
         <li>Fully customizable using custom HTML and CSS</li> 
         <li>Priority support for the premium version</li> 
         <li>Numerous Paypal options i.e. PayPal Payments Standard, PayPal Payments Pro and PayPal Payments Express</li> 
         <li>Multiple payment gateways including Google Wallet, Chronopay, iDEAL, BluePay, Sagepay, Paystation, Authorize.net and FirstData/LinkPoint amongst others</li> 
         <li>Marketing-ready with multiple options such as New Products widgets, Cross-sells, flexible coupon rules, quantity discounts, Google Analytics, Google Sitemaps, Google Base integration etc</li> 
         <li>Drag and drop shopping for better user experience</li> 
         <li>SEO-ready</li> 
         <li>SSL certificates</li> 
         <li>Catalog management</li> 
         <li>And much much more</li> 
        </ul> 
        <p>WP e-commerce might have little on WooCommerce in terms of functionality based on user ratings, but feature-wise, it is well endowed. The plugin was developed by <a title="Dan Milward's profile at WordPress.org" href="https://profiles.wordpress.org/mufasa/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Dan Milward</a>, <a title="Gary Cao's Profile at WordPress.org" href="https://profiles.wordpress.org/garyc40/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Gary Cao</a> and <a title="Justin Sainton's Profile at WordPress.org" href="https://profiles.wordpress.org/justinsainton/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Justin Sainton</a> and is maintained at <a title="WordPress Ecommerce - A WordPress Shopping Cart Plugin - getshopped.org" href="http://getshopped.org/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Getshopped.org</a>.</p> 
        <p><a title="Download WP e-commerce WordPress Plugin " href="https://wordpress.org/plugins/wp-e-commerce/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Download WP e-commerce</a></p> 
        <h3>ITHEMES EXCHANGE: SIMPLE ECOMMERCE</h3> 
        <p><a title="iThemes Exchange e-commerce WordPress plugin" href="https://wordpress.org/plugins/ithemes-exchange/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><img class="fullimg" src="http://www.wpexplorer.com/wp-content/uploads/ithemes-exchange-ecommerce-plugin-for-wordpress-wpexplorer.png" alt="ithemes-exchange-ecommerce-plugin-for-wordpress-wpexplorer" width="672" height="216"></a></p> 
        <p>Are you looking for a simple yet powerful e-commerce solution for your WordPress site? Well, look no further, iThemes Exchange got you covered.</p> 
        <p>The plugin, which was developed by <a title="iThemes - Premium WordPress Plugins, Themes, Training" href="http://www.wpexplorer.com/out/ithemes-main" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">iThemes</a>, has over fourteen (14) thousand downloads and a rating of 5/5. It enables you to set up and run an e-commerce store quickly by eliminating the challenging aspects and concentrating on simplicity.</p> 
        <p>Some of its unique features include a simplified interface that helps you to add product easily, PayPal and Stripe integration, fully extendable, and custom management to let you run your estore your way. The plugin boasts of so many other features not mentioned above. You can also purchase the <strong>Pro pack</strong> that comes with additional features including 24/7 support.</p> 
        <p><a title="Download iThemes Exchange e-commerce WordPress plugin" href="https://wordpress.org/plugins/ithemes-exchange/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Download iThemes Exchange: Simple Ecommerce</a></p> 
        <h3>CART66 LITE :: WORDPRESS ECOMMERCE</h3> 
        <p><a title="Cart66 Lite e-commerce plugin for WordPress" href="https://wordpress.org/plugins/cart66-lite/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><img class="fullimg" src="http://www.wpexplorer.com/wp-content/uploads/cart66-lite-ecommerce-plugin-for-wordpress-wpexplorer.png" alt="cart66-lite-ecommerce-plugin-for-wordpress-wpexplorer" width="675" height="216"></a></p> 
        <p>According to <strong>reality66</strong>, the author, Cart66 is “…the easiest to use WordPress e-commerce shopping cart plugin.” Let us look at some of its features and make our own judgement.</p> 
        <p>To begin with, Cart66 allows you to receive credit card payments securely with the Mijireh checkout system.</p> 
        <p>Other than that, you can integrate Cart66 to Amazon S3, manage orders, run promotions, calculate taxes, provide multiple shopping options to your customers, place products whenever you want on your website, integrate affiliate platforms, send out custom-made email receipts and set custom currencies.</p> 
        <p>Other features are available in <strong>Cart66 Professional</strong> even though the free version gives you everything you need to get your store up and running.</p> 
        <p>It’s easy to configure and use, and with the numerous options, nothing should stand between you and your online dreams.</p> 
        <p><a title="Download Cart66 Lite e-commerce plugin for WordPress" href="https://wordpress.org/plugins/cart66-lite/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Download Cart66 Lite :: WordPress E-commerce</a></p> 
        <h3>JIGOSHOP</h3> 
        <p><a title="Jigoshop e-commerce plugin for WordPress" href="https://wordpress.org/plugins/jigoshop/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><img class="fullimg" src="http://www.wpexplorer.com/wp-content/uploads/jigoshop-ecommerce-plugin-for-wordpress-plugin-wpexplorer.png" alt="jigoshop-ecommerce-plugin-for-wordpress-plugin-wpexplorer" width="676" height="217"></a></p> 
        <p>Jigoshop is a professional WordPress e-commerce plugin built by designers with years of experience developing e-commerce solutions for big brands.</p> 
        <p>To guarantee excellent customizability and performance, the authors developed the feature-rich Jigoshop upon WordPress core functionality.</p> 
        <p>While still keeping it simple and straightforward, Jigoshop offers you all the features you need “…to set up an e-commerce website lickety-split.”</p> 
        <p>The plugins boasts of unique e-commerce features such as:</p> 
        <ul> 
         <li>Multiple product options i.e. you can offer downloadable digital products, affiliate products, variable products and grouped products</li> 
         <li>Detailed reports of sales, orders and store performance</li> 
         <li>Stock tracker</li> 
         <li>Multiple shipping methods</li> 
         <li>Fully extendable</li> 
         <li>Multiple payment gateways</li> 
         <li>Easy to set up and use</li> 
         <li>Secure and highly customizable</li> 
         <li>An ever-growing support community to hold your hand</li> 
        </ul> 
        <p>Jigoshop is free, has over about 300,000 downloads and a user rating of 3.9/5.0. If you’re looking for a feature-packed e-commerce plugin for WordPress, this is it.</p> 
        <p><a title="Download Jigoshop e-commerce plugin for WordPress" href="https://wordpress.org/plugins/jigoshop/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Download Jigoshop</a></p> 
        <h3>MARKETPRESS – WORDPRESS ECOMMERCE</h3> 
        <p><a title="WPMUdev MarketPress WordPress Plugin" href="https://wordpress.org/plugins/wordpress-ecommerce/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><img class="fullimg" src="http://www.wpexplorer.com/wp-content/uploads/marketpress-ecommerce-plugin-for-wordpress-wpexplorer.png" alt="marketpress-ecommerce-plugin-for-wordpress-wpexplorer" width="678" height="221"></a></p> 
        <p>If you’re a fan of <a title="Premium WordPress Plugins by WPMU DEV" href="http://www.wpexplorer.com/out/wpmu" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">WPMUdev plugins</a>, you will most likely love MarketPress. According to WPMUdev, “…MarketPress is the easiest to use, best designed and most powerful e-commerce/shopping cart plugin available for WordPress today.”</p> 
        <p>MarketPress is a simple WordPress e-commerce plugin that will help you set up a stylish store from the ground up in a few minutes. The plugin offers all the features you need such as:</p> 
        <ul> 
         <li>Compatible with all WordPress themes</li> 
         <li>AJAX based</li> 
         <li>Several payments gateways including PayPal, Google Checkout, Authorize.net, MoneyBookers (Skrill), 2checkout etc</li> 
         <li>Internationalization options</li> 
         <li>100% BuddyPress, WordPress and WordPress Multisite compatible</li> 
         <li>Google Analytics</li> 
         <li>Countless product variations</li> 
         <li>Shipping coupons</li> 
         <li>Sales pricing</li> 
         <li>And so much more!</li> 
        </ul> 
        <p><span style="line-height: 1.8em;">WPMUdev developed MarketPress because “…so many of the existing WordPress e-commerce plugins out there offer a pretty awful user experience.”</span></p> 
        <p>Therefore, if you are looking for an e-commerce plugin that has a great user interface and offers an experience worth a lifetime, MarketPress is your answer.</p> 
        <p><a title="Download MarketPress - WordPress ecommerce plugin" href="https://wordpress.org/plugins/wordpress-ecommerce/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Download MarketPress – WordPress eCommerce</a></p> 
        <h3>EASY DIGITAL DOWNLOADS</h3> 
        <p><a title="Easy Digital Downloads by Pippin Williamson" href="https://wordpress.org/plugins/easy-digital-downloads/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><img class="fullimg" src="http://www.wpexplorer.com/wp-content/uploads/easy-digital-downloads-ecommerce-plugin-for-wordpress-wpexplorer.png" alt="easy-digital-downloads-ecommerce-plugin-for-wordpress-wpexplorer" width="672" height="218"></a></p> 
        <p>Easy Digital Downloads was made just for that – to facilitate easy digital downloads. If your e-store is all about selling digital products (i.e. you don’t sell physical products), Easy Digital Downloads is the best e-commerce plugin for you.</p> 
        <p>As opposed to having all the e-commerce features under the sun, Easy Digital Downloads offers just the features you need to take the challenge out of selling digital products.</p> 
        <p>On top of 200,000 downloads and a rating of 4.8/5.0, the following are the toned down features that make Easy Digital Downloads such a darling:</p> 
        <ul> 
         <li>Multiple payment gateways</li> 
         <li>Payment history feature</li> 
         <li>Promotional code system</li> 
         <li>Cart system</li> 
         <li>Multiple files per download</li> 
         <li>Bundle purchases</li> 
         <li>Extensible</li> 
         <li>Sales charts</li> 
         <li>Ability to re-download files</li> 
         <li>Et cetera, et cetra</li> 
        </ul> 
        <p>While I did mention the features are toned down, the plugin is still very powerful and elegant at the same time.</p>
