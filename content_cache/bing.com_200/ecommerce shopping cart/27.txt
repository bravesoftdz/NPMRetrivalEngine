          <td><p>There are two ways to install a new template in your Modular Merchant shopping cart. Following are instructions for both methods:</p> <p><span class="p-title">Option 1: Install the template with one click.</span><br> Use this option to install one of the many free templates that are available.</p> <p>First, log in to your store's Administration Area, and navigate to the <i>Get Template Packages</i> page at [Design &gt; Get Template Packages]. This page lists the templates that can be installed directly within your store's Administration Area.<i> Multiple templates can be installed, but only one will be applied to your public storefront at a time.</i></p> <p class="justify-center"><img src="img/tp_get_templates.jpg" alt="Installing templates with one click" width="950" height="170"><br> <span class="secondary-text">An example of browsing a list of available templates within the store's Administration Area.</span></p> <p>Browse the selection of templates, and locate one that you would like to install. If it has not yet been installed in your account, it will have a link titled <b><i>Install</i></b>. Clicking this link will automatically install the latest version of the selected template into your shopping cart. It will then be ready to use!</p> <p>If a template has already been installed in your store, then a link titled <b><i>Reinstall</i></b> will be listed. This link can be used to install a fresh copy of the template in your shopping cart. Any previous versions, including any customizations that have been made to the template since it was first installed, will be replaced with a copy of the latest version.</p> <p class="justify-center"><img src="img/3d_divider.gif" width="950" height="15" vspace="10"></p> <p><span class="p-title">Option 2: Upload and install a template.</span><br> Use this option to upload a template that you have downloaded or designed yourself.</p> <p>First, log in to your store's Administration Area, and navigate to the <i>Template Package Sandbox</i> page at [Design &gt; Template Package Sandbox]. This page contains several options for working with templates. Locate the section of the page titled <b><i>Select a Template Package</i></b>. Select <b><i>Upload a Template Package</i></b> from the dropdown menu in this section.</p> <p class="justify-center"><img src="img/tp_sandbox_001.png" alt="Preparing to upload a template" width="682" height="105"><br> </p> <p> Selecting the <i>Upload a Template Package</i> option will display a field on the right that allows you to browse your computer for a template package and upload it.</p> <p class="justify-center"><img src="img/tp_sandbox_002.png" alt="Locate a template to install in your shopping cart" width="682" height="89"><br> </p> <p> Click the <b>Browse</b> button to search your computer for a zip file containing the template package that you would like to upload. Once you have located it, click the <b>Upload</b> button to automatically upload and install it. The shopping cart software will automatically upload and unzip the zip file, copy all of the template package's files to the appropriate locations on the website. (Your original copy of the files on your computer that you uploaded will remain on your computer.) </p> <p>After the template has been uploaded and installed, it will be available for use. To apply it to your store, make it your store's "live" template!</p>
