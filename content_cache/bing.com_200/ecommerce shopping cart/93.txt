        <h2 id="description-header">Description</h2> 
        <p>The Professional WordPress eCommerce Plugin. Use it as shopping cart, catalog or framework.</p> 
        <p>You can extend its functionality with plugins and themes to customize your store exactly in same way that you do with WordPress. It is compatible with many existing plugins in the WordPress repository.</p> 
        <p>100% WordPress, eCommerce naturally flexible &amp; Scalable. Powerful backend tools to make your site, store or shop Easyly and Useful</p> 
        <ul> 
         <li><a href="http://thecartpress.com/extend" rel="nofollow">TheCartPress Extend</a>: plugins, themes and custom development</li> 
         <li><a href="http://thecartpress.com" rel="nofollow">TheCartPress Site</a></li> 
         <li><a href="http://pluginsmaker.com" rel="nofollow">Pluginsmaker Site</a></li> 
        </ul> 
        <h4>Universal theme compatibility, making it compatible also with all WordPress themes</h4> 
        <h4>eCommerce 100% WordPress, naturaly flexible &amp; Scalable</h4> 
        <ul> 
         <li>Native integration with WordPress since conception of TheCartPress.</li> 
         <li>New WordPress user roles: Customer and Merchant.</li> 
         <li>WordPress (MU) network compatibility.</li> 
         <li>Powerful backend tools to make your Store Easy and Useful.</li> 
         <li>Use it as a complete ecommerce solution, as a catalog or as a framework.</li> 
         <li>WordPress dashboard adapted to an eCommerce use.</li> 
        </ul> 
        <h4>International Sales</h4> 
        <ul> 
         <li>Multicurrency, flexible and custom price formats.</li> 
         <li>International weight units.</li> 
         <li>International taxes. 
          <ul> 
           <li>Prices with or without taxes. Rules for taxes. Flexibility and power.</li> 
          </ul> </li> 
         <li>Payment and Shipping methods filter by buyer country. 
          <ul> 
           <li>Restrict Countries for shipping and payment.</li> 
          </ul> </li> 
         <li>Multilanguage eCommerce.</li> 
         <li>TheCartPress is compatible with all major multilanguage plugins. We have tested successfully with WPML (WordPress multilingual plugin) and qTranslate.</li> 
        </ul> 
        <h4>Physical and downloadable products</h4> 
        <ul> 
         <li>New WordPress custom post type for products: tcp_product.</li> 
         <li>Create as many saleable post type as you need to structure different types of stores and take advance of WordPress template hierarchy to customize the design/layout. Custom saleable post types are ideal for stores with very different products in nature (megastores, etc.).</li> 
         <li>Auto embed media player for audio products.</li> 
        </ul> 
        <h4>Complex Products and Catalog Structures</h4> 
        <ul> 
         <li>Grouped products with excerpt, content, images, author, date, comments, cross content, etc.</li> 
         <li>Simple products with price, stock, sku, excerpt, content, images, author, date, comments, cross content, attributes, etc.</li> 
         <li>Product variations with price, stock, sku, excerpt, content, images, author, date, comments, etc.</li> 
         <li>Attributes. 
          <ul> 
           <li>“n” levels of attributes for simple products.</li> 
           <li>Attribute sets: Group your attributes and optimize the work of the merchant assigning a set of attributes for each product.</li> 
           <li>Dynamic options: Product variations based on product attributes.</li> 
          </ul> </li> 
         <li>Buy options format for attributes (extending).</li> 
         <li>Default taxonomies for the products: Category, supplier and tag.</li> 
         <li>Custom taxonomies to sort your products.</li> 
         <li>Classic or Layered navigation to filter the catalog by taxonomies (plugin).</li> 
        </ul> 
        <h4>Marketing highlights</h4> 
        <ul> 
         <li>Search Engine Friendly URL’s. Custom permalinks for product archives, single product and taxonomy pages.</li> 
         <li>Free Shipping Option.</li> 
         <li>Discounts.</li> 
         <li>Coupons.</li> 
         <li>Recently Viewed Products.</li> 
         <li>Cross-sells and cros-content in many ways. 
          <ul> 
           <li>Single product – Single products.</li> 
           <li>Single product – Single posts.</li> 
           <li>Single product – Product categories.</li> 
           <li>Single product – Post categories.</li> 
           <li>Category of product – Categories of products.</li> 
           <li>Category of product – Categories of posts.</li> 
           <li>Single post – Single posts.</li> 
           <li>Single post – Single products.</li> 
           <li>Single post – Post categories.</li> 
           <li>Single post – Product categories.</li> 
           <li>Category of post – Categories of Posts.</li> 
           <li>Category of post – Categories of Products.</li> 
          </ul> </li> 
         <li>Compatibility with more relevant SEO WordPress plugins: WordPress SEO (Yoast), All in one SEO pack, etc.</li> 
        </ul> 
        <h4>Customer Accounts Offering</h4> 
        <ul> 
         <li>New WordPress user role: Customer.</li> 
         <li>Customizable Order Emails.</li> 
         <li>Account Dashboard for customers.</li> 
         <li>Order status and history.</li> 
         <li>Downloadable area.</li> 
         <li>Address Book with unlimited addresses.</li> 
         <li>Default Billing and Shipping addresses.</li> 
         <li>Product Reviews submitted.</li> 
        </ul> 
        <h4>Product listing, Configurable loops</h4> 
        <ul> 
         <li>Use configurable TCP loops and custom WordPress loops to list your products.</li> 
         <li>Sort panel by: price, title, suggested, date, popular. Asc or Desc.</li> 
         <li>2 default and configurable loops: Grid and Listing loops with options: 
          <ul> 
           <li>Number of columns.</li> 
           <li>Number of products.</li> 
           <li>See pagination.</li> 
           <li>See sort panel.</li> 
           <li>Order products by.</li> 
           <li>See Title.</li> 
           <li>See buy options.</li> 
           <li>See image.</li> 
           <li>See excerpt.</li> 
           <li>See content.</li> 
           <li>See taxonomies.</li> 
           <li>See utilities.</li> 
           <li>See author.</li> 
           <li>See custom areas.</li> 
          </ul> </li> 
         <li>Use custom and configurable loops, also in shortcode generator and widgets.</li> 
        </ul> 
        <h4>Full Catalog Browsing Offering</h4> 
        <ul> 
         <li>Filter by Product archives and Product taxonomies: Archive, Author, Date and taxonomies: Category, Supplier, Tag and custom taxonomies.</li> 
         <li>Widgets. 
          <ul> 
           <li>TCP Navigation Tree and TCP Navigation Cloud widgets: Product categories and Product custom taxonomies trees and clouds.</li> 
           <li>TCP Layered navigation. (plugin)</li> 
           <li>Product comments widget.</li> 
           <li>Product archives widget: yearly, monthly, weekly, daily.</li> 
           <li>Custom and configurable product and post type lists widget.</li> 
           <li>Last visited widget.</li> 
           <li>Related items in many ways. Cross-sales, Cross-contents. (see marketing section).</li> 
           <li>Product listing in configurable grid or list format. You can have as many loop templates as you need. The widgets and shortcodes let you choose the template loop and let you set the visibility of each of the elements when working with Tcp loops.</li> 
          </ul> </li> 
         <li>Shortcodes, insert configurable product lists when writing in the editor. See some shortcode examples in TheCartPerss Basic Demo. Also you can use your shortcodes in your in BUDDYPRESS installation.</li> 
        </ul>
