       <h2>Ecommerce Website Design Package</h2> 
      </div> 
     </div> 
     <div class="col-md-9"> 
      <p><span style="font-size: 17px;">This mobile friendly affordable ecommerce website design package includes all the functions that you'll need to sell your products online. But it's also a whole lot more than that, because your website will also be&nbsp;</span><span style="font-size: 17px;"><strong>custom designed</strong> specifically to integrate with your branding and marketing strategy.</span></p> 
      <p>Typically we build our ecommerce websites in the&nbsp;<strong>Magento </strong>or&nbsp;<strong>concrete5</strong>&nbsp;<a title="Content Management System" href="/services/content-management-system/">content management systems</a> depending on your requirements. If you only intend to sell a small number of products, then concrete5 is just fine, but if you're wanting a large merchandise store then Magento could be the better option. But don't panic,&nbsp;we discuss the options with you to decide which is the best system for you.&nbsp;</p> 
      <p>Whichever system we choose, this package includes all the bells and whistles for incorporating blogs, videos, image galleries, a newsletter signup form, FAQs and so on - and your website will be <a title="Increase Your Google Ranking with Responsive Website Design" href="/blog/increase-your-google-ranking-responsive-website-design/">fully responsive</a> (i.e. mobile friendly). Like all our websites, this package includes&nbsp;professional copywriting and search engine optimisation.</p> 
      <p><strong>If you haven't already done so, check out our <a title="Online Shopping Websites Portfolio" href="/portfolio/online-shopping-websites-portfolio/">portfolio of ecommerce websites</a>.&nbsp;</strong></p> 
      <p>&nbsp;</p> 
      <h2>&nbsp;Our Ecommerce&nbsp;<span style="color: #33cccc; font-family: Roboto, sans-serif; font-size: 28px;">Website Design Package Includes:</span></h2> 
      <ul> 
       <li><strong>Custom graphic design</strong>&nbsp;</li> 
       <li>Up to<strong><span style="color: #000000;"> nine designed page templates </span></strong>(including the homepage&nbsp;<span>and up to eight other page layouts)</span></li> 
       <li>Website build in a <strong><a title="Content Management" href="http://www.designerwebsites.co.nz/services/content-management/">Content Management System</a></strong> (so you can update your website yourself) with full training</li> 
       <li>Fully responsive (i.e. mobile friendly)</li> 
       <li><strong>Keyword research</strong><em> (so you know what search terms potential customers are using to find your products online)</em></li> 
       <li><strong><a href="http://www.designerwebsites.co.nz/website-packages/website-packages/#copywriting">Professional copywriting</a> for 10 pages</strong><em>&nbsp;(incorporating the above keywords)</em></li> 
       <li>All required content and products loaded onto to the new site*</li> 
       <li>Image galleries</li> 
      </ul> 
      <div> 
       <ul> 
        <li><strong>Unlimited products and categories</strong> with control over editing, adding or deleting products</li> 
        <li>Basic <strong>shipping options</strong> to charge for delivery of your products</li> 
        <li>Shopping cart system with <strong>full check-out process</strong> including ability for customers to purchase multiple products</li> 
        <li>Integration with secure <strong>third party payment gateway</strong> for accepting credit card payments</li> 
        <li><strong>‘You may also like’ function</strong>, displaying complementary products to the one being viewed by the customer</li> 
       </ul> 
       <em>NOTE: If you're selling digital products, such as music files or documents, then the shopping cart can be easily configured to handle this at no extra cost.</em>
       <br>&nbsp;
       <br> 
       <ul> 
        <li>Product image zoom function</li> 
        <li>Image 'mouse over' changes between two images, e.g. front and back of a product, or product and packaging</li> 
       </ul> 
      </div> 
      <ul> 
       <li>Ability to<strong> add video</strong> from YouTube or Vimeo</li> 
       <li><strong>Full blog capability</strong> with unlimited posts and the <strong>AddThis social media share </strong>button</li> 
       <li><strong>Send to Friend </strong>form, so visitors can share your site with people they know</li> 
       <li><strong>Integration with Mailchimp</strong> so visitors can subscribe to your marketing material from your website</li> 
       <li><strong>Search</strong> function</li> 
       <li>Basic <strong>FAQ’s</strong> page</li> 
       <li>Contact form and Google map&nbsp;</li> 
      </ul> 
      <ul> 
       <li><strong>Initial <a href="http://www.designerwebsites.co.nz/website-packages/website-packages/#SEO">Search Engine Optimisation</a></strong></li> 
       <li>We'll set up Google Analytics so you can measure traffic to your website</li> 
       <li>Timeframe, approx. 10 – 12 weeks from&nbsp;<a title="Our Design &amp; Development Process" href="/about/website-design/">design brief meeting</a></li>
