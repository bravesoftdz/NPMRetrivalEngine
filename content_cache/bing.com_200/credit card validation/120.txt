        <p>Adds some minimal validation rules for credit card.</p> 
        <h2><a id="user-content-install" class="anchor" href="#user-content-install" rel="nofollow noopener external"></a>Install</h2> 
        <p>Install via composer</p> 
        <pre><code>composer require rap2hpoutre/laravel-credit-card-validator
</code></pre> 
        <p>Add Service Provider to <code>config/app.php</code> in <code>providers</code> section</p> 
        <pre><span class="pl-s1"><span class="pl-c1">Rap2hpoutre\LaravelCreditCardValidator\</span><span class="pl-c1">ServiceProvider</span><span class="pl-k">::</span><span class="pl-c1">class</span>,</span></pre> 
        <h2><a id="user-content-usage" class="anchor" href="#user-content-usage" rel="nofollow noopener external"></a>Usage</h2> 
        <p>Add this to your validation rules:</p> 
        <pre><span class="pl-s1"><span class="pl-c"><span class="pl-c">//</span> Add this in your controller method</span></span>
<span class="pl-s1"><span class="pl-smi">$this</span><span class="pl-k">-&gt;</span>validate(<span class="pl-smi">$request</span>, [</span>
<span class="pl-s1">    <span class="pl-s"><span class="pl-pds">'</span>credit-card-number<span class="pl-pds">'</span></span> <span class="pl-k">=&gt;</span> <span class="pl-s"><span class="pl-pds">'</span>required|ccn<span class="pl-pds">'</span></span>,</span>
<span class="pl-s1">    <span class="pl-s"><span class="pl-pds">'</span>credit-card-date<span class="pl-pds">'</span></span> <span class="pl-k">=&gt;</span> <span class="pl-s"><span class="pl-pds">'</span>required|ccd<span class="pl-pds">'</span></span>,</span>
<span class="pl-s1">    <span class="pl-s"><span class="pl-pds">'</span>credit-validation-code<span class="pl-pds">'</span></span> <span class="pl-k">=&gt;</span> <span class="pl-s"><span class="pl-pds">'</span>required|cvc<span class="pl-pds">'</span></span>,</span>
