        <h1>Voice recognition software for Windows</h1> 
        <p><strong>An introduction to the main voice recognition packages for Windows</strong></p> 
        <h2>More about voice recognition</h2> 
        <p>Although there is voice recognition software built into Office XP, there are also several widely used packages for sale that offer excellent accuracy and even complete 'hands-free' use . For example Dragon Naturally Speaking Voice Recognition and IBM's ViaVoice Software. This is a very powerful option for those who are not able to use a physical keyboard or mouse, or just want to speed up their text input.</p> 
        <p>The systems take time to &#x2018; train &#x2019; to recognise the speaker, but with practice it is possible to input text at a rate in excess of the fastest typist.</p> 
        <p><img alt="Dragon Naturally Speaking software" height="176" src="images/Voice-recognition-software-for-Windows-img-0.jpg" width="297"></p>
        <p><strong>Fig 1</strong></p> 
        <p>More recently these systems have become much more affordable - a full list of options is described in our factsheet ' Voice recognition software - an Introduction' which is available below as text and PDF files.</p>
