  <blockquote>
    Here is some advice that will help use the applet: 
   <ul> 
    <li>Press <tt>"Start"</tt> to restart the algorithm. When you press the <tt>"Start"</tt> button the algorithm will sort the numbers in the textfields. </li>
    <li>Use the scrollbar to define how many numbers you want to sort </li>
    <li>If you press <tt>"Randomize"</tt> you can produce random numbers to sort. </li>
    <li>If you want to input your own numbers, just edit the textfields located at the bottom of the applet. </li>
    <li>If the Textfields contain the numbers of your will then press <tt>"Start"</tt>. </li>
    <li>You can go backwards and forwards and examine the algorithm's steps using the <tt>"Back"</tt> and <tt>"Next"</tt> buttons. </li>
   </ul>
