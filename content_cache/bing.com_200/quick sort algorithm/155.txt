             <p><strong>Objec­tive:&nbsp;</strong>Write an algo­rithm to sort an array in increas­ing or decreas­ing order using Quick&nbsp;Sort.</p> 
             <p><strong>Input:</strong>&nbsp; An Array&nbsp;arrA[]</p> 
             <p><strong>Out­put:</strong> A sorted array.</p> 
             <p><strong>Approach:</strong></p> 
             <ul> 
              <li>Choose any ele­ment from the array and call it as pivot ele­ment, Exam­ple here we have selected mid­dle ele­ment as&nbsp;pivot</li> 
              <li>Place all the ele­ments smaller than pivot in the left side of&nbsp;pivot.</li> 
              <li>Place all the ele­ments greater than pivot in the right side of&nbsp;pivot.</li> 
              <li>Sort left side and right side recursively.</li> 
             </ul>
