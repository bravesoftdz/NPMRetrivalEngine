           <div class="text-center"> 
            <div class="image-wrapper"> 
             <p><img src="https://superdevresources.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/free-sound-effects-games.jpg" alt="free sound effects for games"></p> 
            </div> 
           </div> 
           <p>In this post, we bring to you many sound effect&nbsp;sources that we believe will help you in getting the kind of audio you are looking for your game. Some of these sites offer royalty free sound effects that you can download and use in commercial games for free. While some require you to give attribution to the artist. Always make sure you read the terms and conditions regarding their usage before downloading music tracks or sound effects from these sites.</p> 
           <p>The sites listed below offer audio files in different formats including MP3, OGG, and uncompressed WAV. If you are planning to use MP3 files in your game, you may want to read our earlier post on <a href="http://superdevresources.com/using-mp3-in-your-apps-and-games-read-this-first/">MP3 licensing in games</a>.</p> 
           <h2 id="freesoundhttpwwwfreesoundorgbrowse"><a href="https://www.freesound.org/browse/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">1. Freesound</a></h2> 
           <div class="image-wrapper"> 
            <p><img src="https://superdevresources.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/freesoundorg.jpg" alt="FreeSound.org"></p> 
           </div> 
           <p><strong>Freesound</strong> has a huge collection of sound effects, loopable audio snippets, and bleeps released under Creative Commons licenses. They support 4 file formats – .mp3, .flac or .fla, .ogg and .aiff/.wav. You can browse their collection by licenses, tags (suspense, terror, etc.) and file types, they also have featured sections for most downloaded sounds and latest sounds. If you like a particular sound effect, you can also browse sounds similar to it.</p> 
           <h2 id="soundbiblehttpsoundbiblecomroyalty-free-sounds-1html"><a href="http://soundbible.com/royalty-free-sounds-1.html" target="_blank" rel="noopener">2. SoundBible</a></h2> 
           <div class="image-wrapper"> 
            <p><img src="https://superdevresources.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/soundbible.jpg" alt="SoundBible.com"></p> 
           </div> 
           <p><strong>SoundBible</strong> offers royalty free sound effects that you can use commercially for free. Some of these sound clips may require you to provide attribution. These sound clips are available for download in either .wav or .mp3 format. You can easily browse these sounds by their <a href="http://soundbible.com/tag-cloud.php">tags</a>. Some of the popular sound effects are, fight sounds, machine sounds, and laugh sounds.</p> 
           <h2 id="audio--music-on-unity-asset-storehttpswwwassetstoreunity3dcomencategory6page1sortbypopularity"><a href="https://www.assetstore.unity3d.com/en/#!/search/page=1/sortby=relevance/query=category:6&amp;price:0" target="_blank" rel="noopener">3. Audio &amp; Music on Unity Asset Store</a></h2> 
           <div class="image-wrapper"> 
            <p><img src="https://superdevresources.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/unity-store-audio.jpg" alt="Unity asset store"></p> 
           </div> 
           <p>If you are a game developer, chances are that you are using Unity game engine and are familiar with its asset store. <strong>Unity asset store</strong> has a collection of free sound FX, music and ambient sounds. It also has a section of top free audio files. These free sounds can be downloaded within Unity only which itself is free for developing commercial games as well.</p> 
           <h2 id="flash-kithttpwwwflashkitcomsoundfx"><a href="http://www.flashkit.com/soundfx/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">4. Flash Kit</a></h2> 
           <div class="image-wrapper"> 
            <p><img src="https://superdevresources.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/flash-kit.jpg" alt="Flash Kit"></p> 
           </div> 
           <p><strong>Flash Kit</strong> has an archive of royalty free sound effects and <a href="http://www.flashkit.com/loops/">sound loops</a> for download. You can either keyword search these sounds or browse them by categories like ambience, nature sounds, and cartoony sounds.</p> 
           <h2 id="music--sound-effects-on-opengamearthttpopengameartorgart-search-advancedkeysfieldarttypetid5b5d13"><a href="http://opengameart.org/art-search-advanced?keys=&amp;field_art_type_tid%5B%5D=13" target="_blank" rel="noopener">5. Music &amp; Sound Effects on OpenGameArt</a></h2> 
           <div class="image-wrapper"> 
            <p><img src="https://superdevresources.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/opengameart.jpg" alt="OpenGameArt.org"></p> 
           </div> 
           <p><strong>OpenGameArt</strong> is a repository of 2D and 3D game art, textures, music and sound effects for game development. It has a huge library of music and sound effects contributed by different artists. These audio files can be easily searched and filtered by licenses and tags.</p> 
           <p><a title="AudioJungle" href="http://audiojungle.net/?ref=monikaratan" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener"><br> <img src="https://superdevresources.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/audio-jungle-h.jpg" alt="AudioJungle"><br> </a></p> 
           <h2 id="pacdv-free-sound-effectshttpwwwpacdvcomsoundsindexhtml"><a href="http://www.pacdv.com/sounds/index.html" target="_blank" rel="noopener">6. PacDV Free Sound Effects</a></h2> 
           <div class="image-wrapper"> 
            <p><img src="https://superdevresources.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/pacdv.jpg" alt="PacDV"></p> 
           </div> 
           <p><strong>PacDV</strong> has a collection of royalty free sound effects and royalty free music. The sound effects are grouped under different categories like mechanical sound effects, ambient background sounds, human sound effects and music tracks. You can use the sound effects and audio clips free of charge in your multimedia productions and games.</p> 
           <h2 id="free-audio-resources-by-kenneyhttpkenneynlassets"><a href="http://kenney.nl/assets/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">7. Free Audio Resources by Kenney</a></h2> 
           <div class="image-wrapper"> 
            <p><img src="https://superdevresources.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/kenney-audio-assets.jpg" alt="Assets by Kenney"></p> 
           </div> 
           <p>Kenney, a game designer, and developer, provides <strong><a href="http://superdevresources.com/free-game-resources-commercial-use-kenney/">CC Zero licensed game resources</a></strong> on his site. These game assets also include audio files that are available in different packs like RPG audio, casino audio, music jingles and UI audio. I used one of his music jingles in my <a href="http://superdevresources.com/cross-platform-game-using-gamemaker/">cross-platform game Spooky Planet</a>.</p> 
           <h2 id="zapsplathttpwwwzapsplatcom"><a href="http://www.zapsplat.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">8. ZapSplat</a></h2> 
           <div class="image-wrapper"> 
            <p><img src="https://superdevresources.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/zapsplat-sound-library.jpg" alt="ZapSplat"></p> 
           </div> 
           <p><strong>ZapSplat</strong> provides free sound effects that can be used in any non-commercial, commercial and broadcast production in exchange of attribution. You can browse their sound library by categories like animals, bells, cartoons, explosion, and household or you can keyword search according to your requirement. You need to register for a free account to download sounds from their site.</p> 
           <h2 id="sonicshttpwwwsonicsio"><a href="http://www.sonics.io/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">9. Sonics</a></h2> 
           <div class="image-wrapper"> 
            <p><img src="https://superdevresources.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/sonics-sound-packs.jpg" alt="Sonics"></p> 
           </div> 
           <p><strong>Sonics</strong> have free email subscription that you can sign-up for to receive royalty free UI sound effects in your inbox every month. These sounds can be used for free in apps, games, websites and videos.</p> 
           <h2 id="soundgatorhttpwwwsoundgatorcom"><a href="http://www.soundgator.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">10. SoundGator</a></h2> 
           <div class="image-wrapper"> 
            <p><img src="https://superdevresources.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/soundgator.jpg" alt="SoundGator.com"></p> 
           </div> 
           <p><strong>SoundGator</strong> offers free sound effects that you can download and use for your videos, presentations, apps and games. Sounds are put together under different categories like beep sound effects, vehicle sound effects, sport sound effects and many more for easy browsing.</p> 
           <h2 id="freesfxhttpwwwfreesfxcouksoundeffects"><a href="http://www.freesfx.co.uk/soundeffects/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">11. freeSFX</a></h2> 
           <div class="image-wrapper"> 
            <p><img src="https://superdevresources.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/freesfx.jpg" alt="freeSFX.co.uk"></p> 
           </div> 
           <p><strong>freeSFX</strong> provides free sound effects for use in commercial and non-commercial projects including those that will be broadcast. In exchange attribution is needed. It has a library of high definition sound effects and music tracks available under many different categories including sounds of animals/insects, fire &amp; explosions and Sci-Fi sounds.</p> 
           <h2 id="sound-jayhttpwwwsoundjaycom"><a href="http://www.soundjay.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">12. Sound Jay</a></h2> 
           <div class="image-wrapper"> 
            <p><img src="https://superdevresources.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/sound-jay.jpg" alt="SoundJay.com"></p> 
           </div> 
           <p><strong>Sound Jay</strong> offers music and soundtracks that you can use in your games free of charge. Sound effects are available as both uncompressed .wav and .mp3 files. They are placed under different categories like button sounds and background sounds for easy browsing.</p> 
           <h2 id="incompetechhttpincompetechcommusic"><a href="http://incompetech.com/music/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">13. Incompetech</a></h2> 
           <div class="image-wrapper"> 
            <p><img src="https://superdevresources.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/incompetech.jpg" alt="Incompetech"></p> 
           </div> 
           <p><strong>Incompetech</strong> has royalty free music tracks available under Creative Commons attribution license. You can search its music library by genre and feel.</p> 
           <h2 id="incompetechhttpincompetechcommusic"><a href="http://soundimage.org/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">14.&nbsp;Sound Image</a></h2> 
           <div class="image-wrapper"> 
            <p><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-8633" src="https://superdevresources.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/sound-image-free-music.jpg" alt="sound-image-free-music" width="858" height="406" srcset="https://superdevresources.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/sound-image-free-music.jpg 858w, https://superdevresources.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/sound-image-free-music-300x142.jpg 300w, https://superdevresources.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/sound-image-free-music-768x363.jpg 768w, https://superdevresources.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/sound-image-free-music-500x237.jpg 500w, https://superdevresources.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/sound-image-free-music-800x379.jpg 800w" sizes="(max-width: 858px) 100vw, 858px"></p> 
            <p><strong>SoundImage.org </strong>provides&nbsp;600+ tracks of free music and soundscapes that are free for developers to use. These sound effects are original work of Eric Matyas and are available in many popular genres&nbsp;such as Action, Fantasy, Classical, Dark, Drama, Horror, &nbsp;Sports, Urban, Techno, and Sci-Fi.&nbsp;All these music on SoungImage are&nbsp;free to use for both non-commercial projects as well as commercial projects. An attribution is required when you use them in your game development project.</p> 
           </div> 
           <p>We will be adding more websites that offer free sounds and keep this list growing. If you want to make a suggestion that we can add to this list, you are welcome to share it with us and our readers in the comments below. And do remember to share this post with your friends and followers, we would greatly appreciate it.</p>

We regularly share many <a href="http://superdevresources.com/tag/free-resources/">free resources</a>, <a href="http://superdevresources.com/tag/free-fonts/">fonts</a>, <a href="http://superdevresources.com/tag/free-icons/">icons</a> and <a href="http://superdevresources.com/tag/textures/">textures</a> that can be of help for your game design.</p>
