       <h1 class="title">Bing Speech Services</h1> 
       <div class="lw_vs"> 
        <div id="curversion"> 
         <strong> Bing </strong> 
        </div> 
       </div> 
       <div style="clear:both;"></div> 
       <div id="mainSection"> 
        <div id="mainBody">
         &nbsp;
         <div class="section">
          <p>Bing Speech Services provide speech capabilities for Windows and Windows Phone.</p>
         </div>
         <div class="introduction">
          <p><strong>Published date: April 2, 2014</strong></p>
         </div>
         <div>
          <h2 class="LW_CollapsibleArea_TitleDiv">
           <div>
            <a class="LW_CollapsibleArea_TitleAhref" title="Collapse" role="heading"><span class="cl_CollapsibleArea_expanding LW_CollapsibleArea_Img"></span><span class="LW_CollapsibleArea_Title">Speech</span></a>
            <div id="Anchor_0" class="LW_CollapsibleArea_Anchor_Div">
             <a href="/en-us/library/dn303461.aspx#Anchor_0" class="LW_CollapsibleArea_Anchor_Img" title="Right-click to copy and share the link for this section"></a>
            </div>
            <div class="LW_CollapsibleArea_HrDiv">
             <hr class="LW_CollapsibleArea_Hr">
            </div>
           </div></h2>
          <div class="sectionblock">
           <div class="section">
            <p>Microsoft Speech technologies consist of three main capabilities:</p>
            <ol class="ordered">
             <li><p><strong>Voice Commands</strong> allow users to operate apps with their voices. This capability is currently only provided through Windows Phone.</p></li>
             <li><p><strong>Speech Recognition</strong> accepts user speech as input to an app and returns printed text. Available for Windows 8 and Windows 8.1 through the <a href="https://msdn.microsoft.com/en-us/library/dn434583.aspx">The Bing Speech Recognition Control</a>, and to Windows Phone 8.1 through the <a href="https://msdn.microsoft.com/en-us/library/windowsphone/develop/windows.media.speechrecognition.aspx">Windows.Media.SpeechRecognition namespace</a>.</p></li>
             <li><p><strong>Speech Synthesis</strong>, otherwise known as <strong>Text to Speech (TTS)</strong>, reads printed text in a natural sounding voice. Available to Windows 8.1 and Windows Phone 8.1 through the <a href="https://msdn.microsoft.com/en-us/library/windows/apps/windows.media.speechsynthesis.aspx">Windows.Media.SpeechSynthesis</a>.</p></li>
