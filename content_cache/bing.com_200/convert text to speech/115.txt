              <p>Two steps to this solution;</p> 
              <ol> 
               <li><p>Text to speech: There are a number of approaches you can take to best solve your particular need: <a href="https://help.ubuntu.com/community/TextToSpeech" rel="nofollow">Ubuntu Community Text to Speech</a>. Pick the one you find to be preferable.</p></li> 
               <li><p>Redirect the audio out to the microphone input: Here is one solution that fully <a href="http://www.kirsle.net/blog/kirsle/redirect-audio-out-to-mic-in--linux-" rel="nofollow">redirects the audio out to the microphone input</a>. </p></li> 
              </ol> 
              <p>I have not prototyped this yet, but this should set you firmly on the right path. Let us know of your progress.</p>
