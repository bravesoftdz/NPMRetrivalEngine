              <div class="wpmud-entry__title"> 
               <h1>How to Convert Text to Speech in WordPress</h1> 
              </div> 
              <div class="wpmud-entry__before-content"></div> 
              <div class="wpmud-entry__content"> 
               <p>If youre running a WordPress-powered blog, the <a href="http://wordpress.org/extend/plugins/vocalyze/" target="_blank">Vocalyze widget</a> is a clever little tool for <strong>converting text to speech</strong> and reaching out to a wider audience.</p> 
               <figure id="attachment_56875" class="wp-caption aligncenter" data-caption="true">
                <a rel="attachment wp-att-56875" class="blog-thumbnail" href="https://premium.wpmudev.org/blog/daily-tip-convert-text-to-speech-with-the-vocalyze-wordpress-widget/vocalyze_widget/"><img class="size-full wp-image-56875" src="https://premium.wpmudev.org/blog/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/vocalyze_widget.jpg" alt="Vocalyze Widget for WordPress" width="440" height="293"></a>
                <figcaption class="wp-caption-text">
                 Raise your blogging voice with the Vocalyze widget for WordPress
                </figcaption>
               </figure> 
               <p>Vocalyze is a <a href="http://www.vocalyze.com/web/index.html" target="_blank">free mobile audio app</a> which <strong>reads web pages aloud</strong>, in an artificial voice, allowing users to follow their favourite sites while theyre away from the computer or too busy to read. Vocalyze is typically used for news sites, RSS feeds and Twitter, but <strong>the app can read content from anywhere on the web</strong>. Vocalyze is available for all Apple iOS and Android devices, as well as any web browser.</p> 
               <h2>Why offer text-to-speech on your WordPress site?</h2> 
               <p>By installing the Vocalyze widget, you can make your WordPress site available to your audience <strong>at times when they normally wouldnt be reading your content</strong>  during the commute to work, for example, or while cooking dinner.</p> 
               <p>This is an easy way to <strong>keep your readers engaged longer and more often</strong>, and the novelty appeal of Vocalyze is sure to earn you a few brownie points for creativity.</p> 
               <h2>Vocalyze offers a new audience for your site</h2> 
               <p>Theres also <strong>publicity to be gained</strong> by using this widget. Vocalyze has an established user base who rely on the app for much of their content delivery. By making your WordPress site available to Vocalyze users, youre tapping into a dedicated and growing audience with lots of <strong>potential to increase your blogs exposure</strong>.</p> 
               <h2>How to get started with text-to-speech on your WordPress site</h2> 
               <p>The <a href="http://wordpress.org/extend/plugins/vocalyze/" target="_blank">Vocalyze widget</a> is very simple to install and configure, and its available free of charge. You can even <strong>install this widget on the WordPress.com hosted service</strong>, however youll need to use a different method  instructions can be found <a href="http://www.vocalyze.com/blogs/?p=111" target="_blank">here</a>. </p> 
               <p>This is a video demo of the Vocalyze iPhone app in action (labelled as VoKnow here).</p> 
               <p><span class="embed-youtube" style="text-align:center; display: block;"><iframe class="youtube-player" type="text/html" width="640" height="390" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/YWNfucWCd5g?version=3&amp;rel=1&amp;fs=1&amp;autohide=2&amp;showsearch=0&amp;showinfo=1&amp;iv_load_policy=1&amp;wmode=transparent" allowfullscreen="true" style="border:0;"></iframe></span></p> 
               <h2>Learn more about WordPress accessibility</h2> 
               <p>Looking to expand your online audience? Back in January we wrote an article about <a href="https://premium.wpmudev.org/blog/wordpress-is-it-accessible/" target="_blank">WordPress accessibility</a>, which is worth reading if you want to learn more about <strong>improving the reach and usability of your website.</strong></p> 
              </div>
