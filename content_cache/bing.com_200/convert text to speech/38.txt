        <p><span style="font-family: georgia, palatino; font-size: medium;"><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>Text To Speech Converter Java Project</strong></span> is a web-based application for converting text documents into audio files that are played with media players with effective speech for listening.&nbsp;</span></p> 
        <p>In our software,&nbsp; we have focused on <strong>SEMESTER RECOGNIZATION SYSTEM</strong> which is beneficial for students as they can know about their subjects and respectively search best books according to their semester &amp; branch.</p> 
        <p><span style="font-family: georgia, palatino; font-size: medium;">In this project, we can record different types of files like documents, PDF files, and power point presentation into speech automatically and the file will be generated in WAV audio format.&nbsp;</span></p> 
        <p>This project is tested for semester reorganization system which is used for converting books into audio files and uses it in phones.</p> 
        <p><span style="font-family: georgia, palatino; font-size: medium;">This application is implemented in java&nbsp;</span><span style="font-family: georgia, palatino; font-size: medium;">platform.</span></p>
