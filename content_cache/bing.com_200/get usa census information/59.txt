        <div class="col-sm-6"> 
         <p> <a href="../../maptovu.htm">Maptitude</a> Geographic Information System (GIS) software is the intelligent mapping solution for business, government, and education. Maptitude is a powerful combination of software and geographic data that provides everything you need to realize the benefits of desktop mapping and spatial analysis with a single, easy-to-use package. Priced at only US$695 (US$395 for upgrades), Maptitude<strong> </strong>is the best buy for those looking for mapping software. </p> 
         <p><strong>In addition, <a href="../../maptovu.htm">Maptitude</a> includes one FREE Country Package of your choice</strong> (a <span class="NavigateItem">US</span>$595 value). Choose the U.S. Country Package to get U.S. maps and detailed demographic data down to the Census Tract level.</p> 
         <h3><strong>Key Features of Maptitude with the United States Country Package: </strong> </h3> 
         <ul class="ulLeft"> 
          <li> Includes nationwide maps such as 5-Digit ZIP Codes with Census and ACS demographic data and ZIP Code Business Pattern Data</li> 
          <li>Includes nationwide streets with address ranges for pin-mapping/geocoding your data.</li> 
          <li>Has highly detailed maps for the U.S. </li> 
          <li>Is versatile demographic software that includes demographic data describing population, income, age, race, gender, households, housing units, ethnicity, ancestry, employment, buying power, population projections, health insurance coverage, and more. Click the Demographic Data tab above for more information.</li> 
          <li>Includes a Map Librarian Wizard with demographic maps by ZIP Code, tract, county, and state</li> 
          <li>Integrates with Google Earth for any location both inside and outside of the U.S.</li> 
          <li>Includes extensive Census demographic information and American Community Survey (ACS) income data (see the Demographic Data tab above)</li> 
          <li>Supports data in most spreadsheet, database, and geographic formats (<a href="http://www.caliper.com/Maptitude/DataAccess.htm">more...</a>) </li> 
          <li>Includes sophisticated analysis tools for building rings (bands/buffers), facility areas of influence, hot-spots, origin-destination flow lines, districts, drive-time rings, drive-time territories, routes, and 3D surfaces (<a href="../GeographicAnalysis.htm">more...</a>)</li> 
          <li>Includes MapWizard<sup class="sup">®</sup> instant mapping technology for creating color, scaled-symbol, dot-density, and pie and bar chart thematic maps</li> 
          <li>Includes GISDK application development platform (<a href="../Development.htm">more...</a>)</li> 
          <li>Includes 60 days of email and phone technical support! </li> 
         </ul> 
         <p>The 2017 United States Country Package requires Maptitude 2017 and it is available to existing Maptitude 2017 users for US$595.</p>

       <h3 style="margin-top: 0;padding-top: 0;"><strong>Demographic Data Included with the United States Country Package</strong></h3>

All of the Census boundaries that are included with Maptitude, plus 3- and 5-digit ZIP Code areas, contain several demographic profiles based on the data reported in the Census and the 2015 American Community Survey (ACS). </p> 
       <p> Census and ACS data are provided down to Census Tracts in Maptitude. There are also additional smaller boundaries called <a href="../../DataCDs/2010Blocks.htm">Blocks</a> and <a href="../../DataCDs/2010BlockGroups.htm">Block Groups</a> available separately for purchase should you need even greater detail. </p>
