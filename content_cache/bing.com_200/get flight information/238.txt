        <h1>Flight Centre re-introduces Captain’s Platinum price drop protection package</h1> 
        <div class="box has-margin-top-xsmall has-margin-bottom-xsmall hide-desktop"> 
         <a href="https://www.finder.com.au/author/stephanie" class="fin-text-blue">Stephanie Yip</a> 
         <span class="fin-text-grey text-x-small">28 September 2017</span> 
        </div> 
        <h2 style="text-align: center;"><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-1282004" src="https://d1ic4altzx8ueg.cloudfront.net/finder-au/wp-uploads/2017/09/amsterdam_1shutterstock_534783616-738.jpg" alt="amsterdam_1shutterstock_534783616-738" width="738" height="410"></h2>
        <h2>Nab cheap flights, price-drop protection and flight cancellation options.</h2>
        <p>It's never too early to start thinking about your next trip. And when gthe price is right you can now scoop it up with <a href="https://www.finder.com.au/flight-centre-promo-codes">Flight Centre</a> without regrets and at the very best price.</p>
        <p>That's all thanks to the Captain's Platinum package.</p>
        <p>Launched in previous years, the package is being re-introduced for a limited time only for early bird flights to Europe during the summer. Sample fares that are currently running include <a href="https://www.finder.com.au/cathay-pacific-sale-get-flights-to-rome-from-1099-return">Sydney to Rome from $1,099</a>, Sydney to Amsterdam from $1,165 flying Cathay Pacific and Sydney to Barcelona from $1,169 return flying Singapore Airlines.</p>
        <p>While these aren't the cheapest flights we've seen headed to Europe through Flight Centre (that award belonging to <a href="https://www.finder.com.au/flights-to-london-drop-to-799-return">$799 return flights on two budget airlines</a>) they are bang on for peak summer dates and all-inclusive service.</p>
        <p>The Captain's Platinum package does come at a price of $149 though does come with numerous benefits. This includes:</p>
        <ul>
         <li><strong>Free cancellations:</strong> You can cancel your trip for a full refund</li>
         <li><strong>$150 accommodation discount</strong></li>
         <li><strong>30-day price drop protection:</strong> Get the difference back if the price drops within 30 days of completing your booking</li>
         <li><strong>Transferable deposit:</strong> Put your deposit to another trip, if required</li>
         <li><strong>$100 insurance excess cashback</strong></li>
        </ul>
        <p>Essentially, you're paying for flexibility with Flight Centre's head of product, advertising, sales and marketing Darren Wright saying, "Travellers can lock in a low-priced airfare, enjoy the Christmas holiday period and then formalise - or cancel - their plans next year if they wish".</p>
        <p>As part of the terms and conditions, you have the capability to cancel your flight within eight weeks of departure for a full refund. Bookings eligible for the Captain's Platinum Package also have to be made by 22 November, and on selected airlines only.</p>
        <p>For more information, visit the <a href="https://www.finder.com.au/redirect/deals/flight-centre-international-secure.html?b=https://www.flightcentre.com.au/deals/choice%2F%3Futm_source%3Dfinder.com.au%26utm_medium%3Dcpc%26utm_campaign%3Dinternational-flight" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">Flight Centre website</a>. You can also check out <a href="https://www.finder.com.au/redirect/deals/flight-centre-international-secure.html?b=https://www.flightcentre.com.au/flights/international-flights2F%3Futm_source%3Dfinder.com.au%26utm_medium%3Dcpc%26utm_campaign%3Dinternational-flight" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">current prices on European flights here</a>.</p>
