       <h1>Pack up and Go with AAA Last Minute Travel Deals</h1> 
       <p>Feel like getting away? Need to book a last minute business trip? You can get away this weekend and save up to 70% with AAA's Last Minute Deals. Our team works hard to bring you the best deal available from 14 days to 3 hours in advance. Our flexible packages include a wide range of options and prices. Flight + Hotel packages usually start at less than $200 per person, while Flight + Car packages and Hotel + Car start at less than $150. One click of the <a href="http://www.aaa.com/LastMinuteDeals?WT.mc_id=999EbusinessTravelSubdomain" onclick="return LogSubmitClick();">"GO"</a> button directs you to your local AAA club website, where you?ll learn about the things best deals.</p> 
       <h1>Hundreds of Hotel, Car and Flight Packages</h1> 
       <p>With hundreds of packages to domestic and international destinations to choose from, Last Minute Deals include your choice of exact flights, hotels and rental car so you get the trip you want with big savings. Enter your frequent flier number when booking your trip and you'll earn miles for every Last Minute Deals flight package.</p> 
       <h1>Grab Your Deal and Go!</h1> 
       <p>You can log on to Last Minute Deals at lunchtime and be on your way to Miami, with <a href="http://travel.aaa.com/hotels.htm">hotel</a>, <a href="http://travel.aaa.com/air-reservations.htm">airfare</a> and optional <a href="http://travel.aaa.com/car-rental.htm">rental car included</a>, by 5pm! But don't delay. Inventory moves fast at the last minute and prices and availability can change quickly. See a deal you like? Pick a package, grab it and go! Last Minute Deals cannot be reserved.</p>
