            <div class="text_style" style="text-align: justify;">
             <p> <a href="http://www.palawan4less.com/palawan/" rel="" style="" target="" title=""><img alt="Palawan Islands" class="alignleft size-medium wp-image-284" height="193" src="http://www.palawan4less.com/images/2013/04/palawan-islands-300x193.jpg" style="margin: 3px 7px;" title="Palawan Islands" width="300"></a><span style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51); line-height: 1.6em;">Welcome to </span><a href="http://www.palawan4less.com/" style="line-height: 1.6em;">Palawan 4 Less</a><span style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51); line-height: 1.6em;"> where you get more vacation for your money. Palawan is one of the Philippines top five destinations for tourist looking to experience the wonderful world of nature, and Palawan Island has that in abundance.</span></p>
             <p> <a href="http://www.palawan4less.com/palawan/" style="line-height: 1.6em;">Palawan</a><span style="line-height: 1.6em;"> like many other Philippines Islands has some fantastic white beaches that are covered with powder soft white sand, very similar to what you will find on </span><a href="http://www.palawan4less.com/boracay-promo-packages/">Boracay</a><span style="line-height: 1.6em;">, the top tourist spot in the Philippines</span></p>
             <p> Here at Palawan 4 Less we have collected some of the best Palawan hotels and packaged them with fantastic <a href="http://www.palawan4less.com/palawan-packages/">3 Day &amp; 2 Night Palawan Packages</a>, this includes wonderful full day tours of some of Palawan's most attractive tourist attractions like the <a href="http://www.palawan4less.com/palawan/palawan-tours/palawan-underground-river-tour/">Puerto Princesa Underground River Tour</a> and a fun filled day of island hopping through <a href="http://www.palawan4less.com/palawan/palawan-tours/honda-bay-island-hopping-tour/">Honda Bay</a>, where you will experience excellent snorkeling in the clear blue oceans that surround this extremely beautiful island.</p>
             <h2> <strong><em>The Main Attraction, Puerto Princesa Underground River</em></strong><br></h2>
             <p> <a href="http://www.palawan4less.com/palawan/palawan-tours/" rel="lightbox" style="" target="" title=""> <img alt="Puerto Princesa Palawan Underground River" class="size-medium wp-image-287 alignright" height="193" src="http://www.palawan4less.com/images/2013/04/palawan-underground-river-300x168.jpg" style="margin: 3px 7px;" title="Puerto Princesa Palawan Underground River" width="300"> </a>If you have never experienced traveling to the Philippines then one of the things you will quickly realize is just how diverse the islands are, for example if you visit <a href="http://www.boracay-packages.com/boracay/">Boracay Island</a> you will experience one of the longest white beaches in the Philippines, at nearly 2.5 miles long and covered with powder soft sand that is just as white as snow.&nbsp;</p>
             <p> Now travel onto the <a href="http://www.packagesbohol.com/bohol/">island of Bohol</a> where you can see even more wonderful and exciting attractions like the Bohol Chocolate Hills, see over a thousand dome shaped hills covering hundreds of hectares of land. All of these hills are roughly the same shape and size, covered in green grass that during the dry season turns brown, earning them the name, The Chocolate Hills of Bohol.</p>
             <p> <a href="http://www.palawan4less.com/palawan/">Palawan</a> is most noted for having the longest underground river in the world, and you can visit it here at the Puerto Princesa Subterranean River National Park, the main attraction for Palawan tourists visiting here from all over the world. This awesome underground river is set inside of a mountian made of limestone. The <a href="http://www.palawan4less.com/palawan/palawan-tours/palawan-underground-river-tour/">Palawan Underground River</a> is located in Sabang, which is about a one and a half hour drive by van from Puerto Princesa City, the drive itself is very interesting and offers great opportunities for both amateur and professional photographers.</p>
             <h2> <strong><em>Palawan Hotels &amp; Resorts</em></strong><br></h2>
             <p> <a href="http://www.palawan4less.com/palawan-hotels/" rel="" style="" target="" title=""><img alt="Palawan Hotels" class="alignleft size-medium wp-image-293" height="232" src="http://www.palawan4less.com/images/2013/04/palwan-hotels-300x232.jpg" style="margin: 3px 7px;" title="Palawan Hotels" width="300"></a>Our Palawan 4 Less website features over&nbsp;(58) Palawan Island Hotels and <a href="http://www.palawan4less.com/palawan-hotels/">Palawan Beach Resorts</a>, many of the hotels and resorts listed here have been toured personally by the travel agents of <a href="http://www.palawan4less.com/wow-philippines-travel-agency/">WOW Philippines Travel Agency</a> in Manila. We have researched each of the <a href="http://www.palawan4less.com/palawan-hotels/">Palawan hotels</a> and resorts thoroughly with the most recent information, we have also where possible included both pictures and video of these Palawan resorts.</p>
             <p> What you may find unique about many Palawan resorts located on private islands is that they are not only unique in style, but also in how they operate, for example many of these hotels and beach resorts must provide their own power and water supply, this adds to the adventure that many Palawan tourist look forward to experience.</p>
             <p> All of the Palawan hotels located away from civilization provide their own restaurants, they provide full meals three times daily, you can aslo order up afternoon snacks if hunger strikes.&nbsp;</p>
             <h2> <strong><em>Palawan Flights</em></strong><br></h2>
             <p> <a href="http://www.palawan4less.com/palawan-flights/" rel="lightbox" style="" target="" title=""> <img alt="Palawan Flights" class="alignright size-medium wp-image-692" height="176" src="http://www.palawan4less.com/images/2013/04/palawan-flights1-300x176.jpg" style="margin: 3px 7px;" title="Palawan Flights" width="300"> </a>Traveling to Palawan by airplane from Manila is the best choice, and our Palawan website offers complete information on all <a href="http://www.palawan4less.com/palawan-flights/">Palawan Flights</a>. We give you weekly updates on the latest rates for flights departing Manila and arriving at the Puerto Princesa Airport, Busuanga Airport and the El Nido Islands Airport.</p>
             <p> By using our Palawan flights page you can also compare the airline rates of other local domestic carriers, after all we are sure you will want to get the best rates on your flights to Palawan. There are four local Philippine Airline companies that offer regular daily flights to Palawan from the Manila International Airport.</p>
             <h2> <strong><em>Palawan Beaches</em></strong><br></h2>
             <p> <a href="http://www.palawan4less.com/palawan/palawan-beaches/" rel="" style="" target="" title=""><img alt="Palawan Beaches" class="alignleft size-medium wp-image-298" height="197" src="http://www.palawan4less.com/images/2013/04/palawan-beaches-300x197.jpg" style="margin: 3px 7px;" title="Palawan Beaches" width="300"></a>The islands of Palawan like so many of the other Philippine Islands offers some really beautiful beaches, many of these <a href="http://www.palawan4less.com/palawan/palawan-beaches/">Palawan beaches</a> are also covered with soft powdery white sand.</p>
             <p> Tourist visiting Palawan for the first time are generally impressed, most are not expecting to find such nice beaches in Palawan, as its mostly noted for it's dense tropical forest.</p>
             <p> You will see many really nice white sand beaches&nbsp;during your&nbsp;Honda Bay Island Hopping Tour, especially Pandan Island and Snake Island are really nice. As you travel out amoung the other Palawan Islands like&nbsp;Busuanga and the El Nido Islands you will find that the&nbsp;beaches there are also really nice.</p>
             <h2> <strong><em>Palawan Tours</em></strong><br></h2>
             <p> <a href="http://www.palawan4less.com/palawan/palawan-tours/" rel="lightbox" style="" target="" title=""> <img alt="Palawan Tours" class="size-medium wp-image-301 alignright" height="180" src="http://www.palawan4less.com/images/2013/04/palawan-underground-river-tour-300x180.jpg" style="margin: 3px 7px;" title="Palawan Tours" width="300"> </a>If you are looking for adventure then <a href="http://www.palawan4less.com/palawan/palawan-tours/">Palawan tours</a> are definitely what you are seeking, one of the best tours is the Puerto Princesa Palawan Underground River Tour, where you will explore the inside of the worlds longest underground river.</p>
             <p> There is also the Crocodile Farm where you will be treated to a fantastic tour of how the fantastic creatures are raised from hatch-lings to adulthood.</p>
             <p> There is also the Puerto Princesa City Tour, where you will have a chance to see many of the city's top tourist attractions, like the Governors Mansion, Bakers Hill, Mitras Ranch and the beautiful Immaculate Conception Church.</p>
