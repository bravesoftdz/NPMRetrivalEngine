        <div id="content-text-ri16win">
         Readiris 16 for Windows is an optical character recognition (OCR) software package that converts an image, a PDF file, or a scanned document into a fully editable text file. Thanks to the power and accuracy of its recognition engine, Readiris 16 for Windows extracts the text from your documents with precision while preserving the layout of the original file.

     <h3 style="text-align:center">About OCR</h3> 
     <div class="content-text-ri16win">
      OCR stands for Optical Character Recognition. This is the process whereby an image of a paper document is captured and the text is then extracted from the resulting image.
      <br> 
      <br> Readiris 16 is an OCR software package that automatically converts text from paper documents, images or PDF files into fully editable files without having to perform all the tedious retyping work!
      <br> The optical character recognition (OCR) technology used in Readiris 16 allows very accurate document recognition whilst preserving the original page layout.
