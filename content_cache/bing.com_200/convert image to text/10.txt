But you can study a bit of the PHP GD library.Here are a few pointers:Create an image: <a href="http://php.net/manual/en/function.imagecreatetruecolor.php" rel="external nofollow">imagecreatetruecolor()</a>Put a string in an image: <a href="http://php.net/imagestring" rel="external nofollow">imagestring()</a> Make the image the right size depending on the length of the text: <a href="http://php.net/manual/en/function.imagefontwidth.php" rel="external nofollow">imagefontwidth()</a> / <a href="http://php.net/manual/en/function.imagefontheight.php" rel="external nofollow">imagefontheight()</a>You'll have to work a lot if you want to use other fonts than the default.
