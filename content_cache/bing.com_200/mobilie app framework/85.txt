   <h2>How is Trigger.io better than...</h2> 
   <div class="row-fluid"> 
    <div class="span6"> 
     <h3>PhoneGap / Cordova</h3> 
     <p>Trigger.io has many more APIs that work out-of-the-box including <a href="http://trigger.io/cross-platform-application-development-blog/2012/04/30/how-to-build-hybrid-mobile-apps-combining-native-ui-components-with-html5/">native UI modules</a> and cloud integrations with <a href="https://trigger.io/modules/parse/current/">Parse</a>, <a href="https://trigger.io/modules/facebook/current/">Facebook</a>, <a href="https://trigger.io/modules/flurry/current/">Flurry</a>, and more. Our simple dev process that does not require you to setup your machine for local compiles.<br> </p>
    </div> 
    <div class="span6"> 
     <h3>Appcelerator</h3> 
     <p><a href="https://trigger.io/examples">Our customers</a> tell us our dev process is much simpler with no need to use a particular IDE like Aptana. It's so familiar to you that you can <a href="http://trigger.io/cross-platform-application-development-blog/2012/03/12/introducing-%E2%80%98build-to-web%E2%80%99-simple-deployment-of-your-mobile-codebase-as-a-web-app/">re-use much of your web / mobile web code</a>.<br> <a href="https://trigger.io/reload">Reload</a> allows you to update your mobile apps at web speed. </p> 
    </div> 
   </div> 
   <div class="row-fluid"> 
    <div class="span6"> 
     <h3>PhoneGap Build</h3> 
     <p>Most builds with Trigger.io take 2 seconds rather than 2 minutes, and are extensible with hooks and custom modules.<br> <a href="http://trigger.io/cross-platform-application-development-blog/2012/01/26/how-forge-works-and-why-were-proud-of-it/">Here's how Forge works and why we're proud of it</a></p> 
    </div> 
    <div class="span6"> 
     <h3>Sencha</h3> 
     <p>Trigger.io has <a href="http://trigger.io/cross-platform-application-development-blog/2012/04/30/how-to-build-hybrid-mobile-apps-combining-native-ui-components-with-html5/">real native UI components</a> for the best look and performance with no compromises. Combine the best of HTML5 and native using Trigger.io native modules.</p>
