The service offers multiple domain-specific models: Conversational (English, Arabic, French, Portuguese and Spanish), News (English, Arabic, French, German, Italian, Japanese, Portuguese, Korean and Spanish) and Patent (English, Chinese, Spanish, Korean and Portuguese). </p>

            <p> Customize the translations based on your unique terminology and language. Language Translator supports three types of customization: forced glossary, parallel phrases and corpus-level customization. </p>
