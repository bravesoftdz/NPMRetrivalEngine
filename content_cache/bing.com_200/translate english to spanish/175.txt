       <th colspan="2">» Description</th> 
      </tr> 
      <tr> 
       <td colspan="2">With Numbers_Words class you can convert numbers<br> written in arabic digits to words in several languages.<br> You can convert an integer between -infinity and infinity.<br> If your system does not support such long numbers you can<br> call Numbers_Words::toWords() with just a string.<br> <br> With the Numbers_Words::toCurrency($num, $locale, 'USD') method<br> you can convert a number (decimal and fraction part) to words with currency name. <br> <br> The following languages are supported:<br> * bg (Bulgarian) by Kouber Saparev<br> * cs (Czech) by Petr 'PePa' Pavel<br> * de (German) by Piotr Klaban<br> * dk (Danish) by Jesper Veggerby<br> * en_100 (Donald Knuth system, English) by Piotr Klaban<br> * en_GB (British English) by Piotr Klaban<br> * en_IN (Indian English) by Piotr Klaban<br> * en_US (American English) by Piotr Klaban<br> * es (Spanish Castellano) by Xavier Noguer<br> * es_AR (Argentinian Spanish) by Martin Marrese<br> * es_MX (Mexican Spanish) by Martin Marrese and Pavel Oropeza<br> * es_VE (Venezuelan Spanish) by Martin Marrese and Pavel Oropeza<br> * et (Estonian) by Erkki Saarniit<br> * fr (French) by Kouber Saparev<br> * fr_BE (French Belgium) by Kouber Saparev and Philippe Bajoit<br> * he (Hebrew) by Hadar Porat<br> * hu_HU (Hungarian) by Nils Homp<br> * id (Indonesian) by Ernas M. Jamil and Arif Rifai Dwiyanto<br> * it_IT (Italian) by Filippo Beltramini and Davide Caironi<br> * lt (Lithuanian) by Laurynas Butkus<br> * lv (Latvian) by Andrius Virbicianskas<br> * nl (Dutch) by WHAM van Dinter<br> * pl (Polish) by Piotr Klaban<br> * pt_BR (Brazilian Portuguese) by Igor Feghali<br> * ro_RO (Romanian) by Bogdan Stancescu<br> * ru (Russian) by Andrey Demenev<br> * sv (Swedish) by Robin Ericsson<br> * tr_TR (Turkish) by Shahriyar Imanov<br> * ua (Ukrainian) by Andrey Demenev and Vital Leshchyk</td>
