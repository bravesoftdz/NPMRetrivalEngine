          <h2 style="text-align: justify;"><strong>Ufone Daily SMS Package 2016:</strong></h2> 
          <p style="text-align: justify;">Ufone has started to give facility of daily SMS Package to its customers in very economical rates. You have been allowed to send 1500 SMS to any network in Pakistan. The validity of Daily SMS package is one day (24 hours). No other network in Pakistan is providing the facility of sending 1500 SMS in one day. The subscription charges of Daily SMS package are Rs.3.99 + tax. Once you have activated this package it continuously activates each day if you have sufficient amount in your account. How to activate and deactivate daily SMS package is the question answered below:-</p> 
          <ul style="text-align: justify;"> 
           <li>To activate write in message&nbsp;<strong>Sub&nbsp;</strong>and send it to 605.</li> 
           <li>Activation charges are 3.99 + tax.</li> 
           <li>Number of SMS are 1500.</li> 
           <li>Validity of package is 24 hours.</li> 
           <li>To deactivate daily sms package write in message <strong>unsub and send it to 506.</strong></li> 
          </ul> 
          <h2 style="text-align: justify;">&nbsp;<strong>Ufone Weekly SMS Package 2016:</strong></h2> 
          <p style="text-align: justify;">&nbsp;Weekly SMS Package offered by Ufone is another exceptional package wherein twelve hundred (1200) messages are allowed to send to online and offline networks in Pakistan. The period of this package is 07 days and activation amount required for this package is only Rs.10 + tax. This package is for those customers who are found habitant to share feelings through SMS to colleagues and family members because only and only 10 rupees are being charged for weekly SMS package. Here you can know about how to activate and deactivate Ufone Weekly SMS Package:-</p>

          <p style="text-align: justify;">Ufone fortnightly SMS package contains ten thousands (10,000) SMS. This package is valid for 15 days. The subscription charges of the fortnightly SMS package are&nbsp;30 + tax.&nbsp;You can send more than 10,000 SMS to any network in Pakistan. This package is expired after 15 days however, if you have sufficient balance in your account than this offer will automatically activated and in shortage of amount you will have to send sms again as per following method:-</p> 
          <ul style="text-align: justify;"> 
           <li>Write in message&nbsp;<strong>Sub&nbsp;</strong>and send it to 603.</li> 
           <li>Activation charges are 30 + tax.</li> 
           <li>Number of SMS are 10,000.</li> 
           <li>Validity of package is 15 days.</li> 
           <li>To deactivate fortnightly sms package write in message <strong>unsub and send it to 506</strong></li>

          <h2 style="text-align: justify;"><strong>&nbsp;Ufone Monthly SMS Package 2016:</strong></h2> 
          <p style="text-align: justify;">The management of Ufone Company is always found to bring something new, reliable and exceptional for their customers therefore, it is the reason that people are rabidly joining Ufone network. Ufone monthly SMS package is tremendous offer by the personnel of Ufone. You can send unlimited SMSs to offline and online in the country. The subscription charges of monthly (20,000) or unlimited SMS package are 80 + tax or almost 100 including tax.&nbsp; The validity of monthly SMS package is 30 days however you are being informed 02 days earlier before expiration of package. The detail of activation and deactivation of monthly SMS package is depicted below:-</p> 
          <ul style="text-align: justify;"> 
           <li>Write in message&nbsp;<strong>Sub&nbsp;</strong>and send it to 607.</li> 
           <li>Activation charges are 80 + tax.</li> 
           <li>Number of SMS are 20,000.</li> 
           <li>Validity of package is 30 days.</li> 
           <li>To deactivate Monthly sms package write in message <strong>unsub and send it to 506</strong></li> 
          </ul> 
          <h2 style="text-align: justify;"><strong>Ufone Yearly SMS Package 2016:</strong></h2> 
          <p style="text-align: justify;">Yearly SMS package by Ufone is another incredible offer. It requires only one time activation for a year than forget about activation and enjoy unlimited SMS to any network in Pakistan. The activation charges of Ufone yearly SMS package are 666 + tax. This package remains active for one year therefore you can share your feelings with family members and as well as friends. Yearly SMS package can be subscribed as method mentioned below:-</p> 
          <ul style="text-align: justify;"> 
           <li>Write in message&nbsp;<strong>Sub&nbsp;</strong>and send it to 601.</li> 
           <li>Activation charges are 666 + tax.</li> 
           <li>Number of SMS are 100,000.</li> 
           <li>Validity of package is 12 Months.</li> 
           <li>To deactivate Monthly sms package write in message <strong>unsub </strong>and send it to <strong>506</strong></li> 
          </ul> 
          <p style="text-align: justify;">This was all about Ufone SMS packages. I have tried level best to provide you latest and important information about activation and deactivation of all packages offered by Ufone. Furthermore if you have any question in mind or want to share your experience with others than please leave message.</p> 
          <p style="text-align: justify;">Ufone is most popular telecommunication company in Pakistan. It started its services from the month of January 2001. This company was merged into PTCL when Pakistan Government privatized Pakistan Telecommunication Company Limited. PTCL and Ufone mutually started to facilitate telecommunication services to the people of Pakistan. This network is spread across the country even in tribunal areas. Recent record issued by Ufone reveals that coverage of network is available more than 10,000 locations in the Pakistan and about 24 million customers are enjoying the services. They offer many attractive services such as call packages, internet packages and SMS packages to its customers. The SMS packages offered by Ufone are cheap in rates as compare to other networks in Pakistan. Due to extraordinary qualitative performance and management it has become 3<sup>rd</sup>&nbsp;largest telecommunication company in Pakistan.</p>

          <ul> 
           <li style="text-align: justify;">All SMS packages do not have facility to send message internationally.</li> 
           <li style="text-align: justify;">All SMS packages do not have facility of Premium SMS.</li> 
           <li style="text-align: justify;">Every SMS is being sent to 600 and 606 will be charged Rs.0.5+Tax.</li> 
           <li style="text-align: justify;">An additional amount of Rs.0.84% will be charged in areas such as AJK, Balochistan, Gilgit,/Baltistan and Fata.</li> 
           <li style="text-align: justify;">The rates of all services are exclusive of General Sale Tax.</li> 
           <li style="text-align: justify;">Standard rates are charged on sending SMS to respective Short Code.</li> 
           <li style="text-align: justify;">Validation of Daily SMS package is 24 hours.</li> 
           <li style="text-align: justify;">Daily SMS package will automatically be activated on daily basis unless you have sufficient amount in your account.</li> 
           <li style="text-align: justify;">Remaining SMS can be checked by sending SMS to 606.</li> 
           <li style="text-align: justify;">Only one time daily SMS can be subscribed in 24 hours.</li> 
           <li style="text-align: justify;">Any SMS package can be unsubscribed by sending SMS to 506.</li> 
           <li style="text-align: justify;">Weekly SMS package can be unsubscribed by sending SMS to 8066.</li>
