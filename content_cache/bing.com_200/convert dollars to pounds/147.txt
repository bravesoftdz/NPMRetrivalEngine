     <h3>Home Delivery</h3> 
     <p> </p>
     <p>Thomas Cook offer a home delivery service or you can reserve online for branch collection.</p> 
     <p>You can use their online click and collect service to purchase foreign currency, travellers cheques or a prepaid currency card.</p> 
     <p>Home delivery is free on orders over £500. You will be charged a handling fee of £4.99 for orders under £500.</p> 
     <p>Orders placed Monday-Thursday before 2pm will be delivered the next working day before 1pm. Orders placed Monday-Wednesday after 2pm will be delivered on the second working day after your order was placed (before 1pm). If you order on a Thursday after 2pm or before 2pm on a Friday your order will arrive on the following Saturday or Monday (before 1pm). Thomas Cook will not guarantee a Saturday delivery. If you order after 2pm on a Friday or during the weekend your order will arrive on Tuesday (before 1pm). </p>
