   <h2><font face="Times New Roman" color="#2F2F67">Directions</font></h2> 
   <p>Enter weight to be converted at top of calculator.&nbsp; Select the radio button to convert into kilograms or pounds .&nbsp; Press the "convert" button.&nbsp; The result will appear at the bottom of&nbsp; the calculator.</p> 
   <h2><font face="Times New Roman" color="#2F2F67">Formula</font></h2> 
   <p align="center"><font size="4">lbs / 2.2 = kilograms</font></p> 
   <p align="center"><font size="4">kg x&nbsp; 2.2 = pounds</font></p> 
   <h2><font face="Times New Roman" color="#2F2F67">Examples</font></h2> 
   <p align="left">A man weighing 70 kilograms weighs 154 pounds</p> 
   <p align="center">70 kg&nbsp; x&nbsp; 2.2 = 154 lbs</p> 
   <p align="left">A woman weighing 110 pounds weighs 50 kilograms</p> 
   <p align="center">110 lbs / 2.2 = 50 kg</p>
