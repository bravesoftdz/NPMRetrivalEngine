        <h2>Conversion Definitions</h2> 
        <p class="hidden-xs">The following is a list of definitions relating to conversions between pounds and kilograms.</p> 
        <h3>What is a pound (lb)?</h3> 
        <p>A pound is a unit of mass in both US Customary Units as well as the Imperial System. The symbol for pound is lb or lbs. There are 2.20462262 pounds in a kilogram.</p> 
        <h3>What is a kilogram (kg)?</h3> 
        <p>A kilogram is a unit of mass in the Metric System. The symbol for kilogram is kg. There are 0.45359237 kilograms in a pound.</p> 
       </div> 
       <div class="formula"> 
        <h2>Conversion Formulas</h2> 
        <p class="hidden-xs">Let's take a closer look at the conversion formulas so that you can do these conversions yourself with a calculator or with an old-fashioned pencil and paper.</p> 
        <p>The formula to convert from lb to kg is:</p> 
        <pre>kg = lb x 0.45359237</pre> 
        <p>The formula to convert from kg to lb is:</p> 
        <pre>lb = kg ÷ 0.45359237</pre> 
       </div>

        <h2>Conversion Examples</h2> 
        <p class="hidden-xs">Next, let's look at some examples showing the work and calculations that are involved in converting from pounds to kilograms (lb to kg) or converting from kilograms to pounds (kg to lb).</p> 
        <h3>Pound to Kilogram Conversion Example</h3> 
        <pre><strong><em>Task: Convert 50 pounds to kilograms (show work)</em></strong>
<em>Formula:</em>
lb x 0.45359237 = kg
<em>Calculations:</em>
50 lb x 0.45359237 = 22.6796185 kg
<em>Result:</em>
50 lb is equal to 22.6796185 kg</pre> 
        <h3>Kilogram to Pound Conversion Example</h3>

<em>Calculations:</em>
15 kg ÷ 0.45359237 = 33.06933933 lb
<em>Result:</em>
15 kg is equal to 33.06933933 lb</pre>
