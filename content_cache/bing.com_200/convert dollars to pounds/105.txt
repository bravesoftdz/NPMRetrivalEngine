       <h2>Currency Converter – About</h2> 
       <p>Currency Converter helps you convert a value to a wide variety of currencies, this tool can also calculate the comparison between these currencies and the gold price.</p> 
       <h3>Currency Converter&nbsp;– Formula<a href="http://www.oncalc.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/08/currency-converter.jpg"><img class="alignright size-thumbnail wp-image-592" title="currency-converter" src="http://www.oncalc.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/08/currency-converter-150x150.jpg" alt="Currency Converter" width="150" height="150"></a></h3> 
       <p><span style="color: #006600;">Exchange rate: R </span>= $1.85 / €1.00 = 1.85 $/€</p> 
       <p><span style="color: #990000;">Amount of dollars to<br> convert = D</span></p> 
       <p><span style="color: #000099;">Number of euros desired = E</span></p> 
       <p>Formula:</p> 
       <p><span style="color: #000099;">E</span> = <span style="color: #990000;">D</span> / <span style="color: #006600;">R</span></p> 
       <p>or</p> 
       <p><span style="color: #990000;">D</span> = <span style="color: #000099;">E</span> x <span style="color: #006600;">R</span></p> 
       <p>…and this is the basic formula for currency conversion used in currency converter.</p>
