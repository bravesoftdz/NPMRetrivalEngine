    <article id="toUnitDesc" class="typeDef"> 
     <h2><a href="/weight/kilograms-conversion.htm">Kilograms</a></h2> 
     <p></p>
     <p></p> 
     <p>The kilogram is the base unit of mass in the International (SI) System of Units, and is accepted on a day-to-day basis as a unit of weight (the gravitational force acting on any given object).</p>
     <p>The kilogram is almost exactly equal to the mass of one <a href="/volume/liters-conversion.htm">litre</a> of water.</p>
     <p></p>
