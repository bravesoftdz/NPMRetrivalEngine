            <div class="Paragraph__component" data-reactid="49">
             <cite class="Paragraph__cite" data-reactid="50">(CNN) — </cite>
             <span data-reactid="51">Exchange rates: They sound dull, they are dull -- until all of a sudden, they make you rich.</span>
            </div>
            <div class="Paragraph__component" data-reactid="52">
             <span data-reactid="53">That's the happy scenario facing dollar-toting travelers to Europe at the moment with the greenbacks in their pockets worth more now than they've been for most of the last decade.</span>
            </div>
            <div data-reactid="54">
             <div class="Ad__component" data-reactid="55">
              <!-- react-empty: 56 -->
              <div id="ad_rect_atf_02" class="Ad__tag Article__adBody Ad__hasLabel" data-reactid="57"></div>
             </div>
            </div>
            <div class="Paragraph__component" data-reactid="58">
             <span data-reactid="59">Bad news, obviously, for anyone heading to United States with a wad of euros, but let's not dwell too heavily on that.</span>
            </div>
            <div class="Paragraph__component" data-reactid="60">
             <span data-reactid="61">Until the currency tides inevitably change back in favor of the euro, let's keep it all about the Benjamins and see what a fistful can buy compared to just 12 months ago.</span>
            </div>
            <div class="Paragraph__component" data-reactid="62">
             <span data-reactid="63">With $1 teetering close to equaling 1 euro, CNN Travel has opened its imaginary wallet to compare current dollar prices in Europe with those of March 2014, when $1 pulled in a paltry 0.72 euros.</span>
            </div>
            <div class="Paragraph__component" data-reactid="64">
             <span data-reactid="65">Here's a country-by-country guide -- for comparison's sake we're hypothetically valuing $1 at exactly $1 euro.</span>
            </div>
            <div class="Paragraph__component" data-reactid="66">
             <span data-reactid="67">And, no, we're not allowing for inflation, because we're travel journalists not accountants.</span>
            </div>
            <div class="Paragraph__component" data-reactid="68">
             <span data-reactid="69"><h3>Netherlands</h3></span>
            </div>
            <div class="Paragraph__component" data-reactid="70">
             <span data-reactid="71">For anyone needing a blindingly obvious metaphor for the flowering currency in their pocket, now's the time to head to Holland to check out the colorful spring tulip crop. </span>
            </div>
            <div class="Paragraph__component" data-reactid="72">
             <span data-reactid="73">Tours from Amsterdam of the <a href="http://www.viator.com/tours/Amsterdam/Keukenhof-Gardens-and-Tulip-Fields-Tour-from-Amsterdam/d525-2550LIN12A" target="_blank">Keukenhof Gardens and tulip fields</a> start at roughly $50 today, compared with $69.50 a year ago.</span>
            </div>
            <div class="Paragraph__component" data-reactid="74">
             <span data-reactid="75">The change can buy enough blooms to carpet a bed in the Mahler Suite of the luxurious 717 Hotel, converted from a 17th-century townhouse overlooking the <a href="http://717hotel.nl/en" target="_blank">Prinsengracht Canal</a> (<em>Prinsengracht 717, 1017 Amsterdam, Netherlands; +31 20 427 0717</em>). There guests may writhe in the petals, giddy with the knowledge they're paying $375 a night, not the $521 of a year ago.</span>
            </div>
            <div class="Paragraph__component" data-reactid="76">
             <span data-reactid="77">Those extra dollars will easily cover a cultural spin around the Rembranted walls of the glorious <a href="https://www.rijksmuseum.nl/en/online-tickets-and-shop?gclid=Cj0KEQjwlYqoBRDajuaTvsyq1PQBEiQAEhSjnPR-7IbaeQog9ldiaYzNZLR_JrEmxnG2O1hl4jKISJIaAtCN8P8HAQ" target="_blank">Rijksmuseum</a> (<em>Museumstraat 1, 1071 Amsterdam, Netherlands; +31 20 674 7000</em>), currently priced at $18, down from $25. </span>
            </div>
            <div class="Paragraph__component" data-reactid="78">
             <span data-reactid="79">Probably to be avoided are Amsterdam's legal(ish) highs, even if a massive 30 gram bag of hash now <a href="http://www.jellinek.nl/vraag-antwoord/hoeveel-kosten-drugs/" target="_blank">retails at $240</a>, a smokin' discount of $93 on 2014 prices.</span>
            </div>
            <div class="Paragraph__component" data-reactid="80">
             <span data-reactid="81"><h3>Italy</h3></span>
            </div>
            <div class="CaptionedImage__component" data-reactid="82">
             <div class="Image__component Image__hasAspectRatio" style="padding-top:56.25%;" data-reactid="83">
              <div style="height:100%;" class="lazyload-placeholder" data-reactid="84"></div>
             </div>
             <div class="CaptionedImage__caption" data-reactid="85">
              Now there's more of Italy's Amalfi coast for your dollars.
             </div>
            </div>
            <div class="Paragraph__component" data-reactid="86">
             <span data-reactid="87">"It's like Italy is on sale right now," says Ginger Pozzini of Minnesota-based Italy travel specialist <a href="http://designingitaly.com/" target="_blank">Designing Italy </a>(<em>+1 651 768 0542</em>). </span>
            </div>
            <div class="Paragraph__component" data-reactid="88">
             <span data-reactid="89">"The dollar hasn't been this strong since we crossed over from the lira to the euro [officially in 2002]."</span>
            </div>
            <div class="Ad__component" data-reactid="90">
             <!-- react-empty: 91 -->
             <div id="ad_rect_btf_01" class="Ad__tag Article__adBody Ad__hasLabel" data-reactid="92"></div>
            </div>
            <div class="Paragraph__component" data-reactid="93">
             <span data-reactid="94">Pozzini, whose company customizes trips for U.S travelers, says so far Italian hotels, restaurants and other businesses haven't raised prices to make up for the shortfall on their end.</span>
            </div>
            <div class="Paragraph__component" data-reactid="95">
             <span data-reactid="96">And many hotels are offering to take payment in advance to lock in exchange rates.</span>
            </div>
            <div class="Paragraph__component" data-reactid="97">
             <span data-reactid="98">"Across Italy, a three-star hotel room that last year went for $350, this year is about $270," says Pozzini.</span>
            </div>
            <div class="Paragraph__component" data-reactid="99">
             <span data-reactid="100">Tuscany villas are never out of style. A 3,000 euro villa rental for a week that would have cost $4,200 last year is now going for a little more than $3,000.</span>
            </div>
            <div class="Paragraph__component" data-reactid="101">
             <span data-reactid="102">Pozzini recently helped a party book an eight-hour private car tour of the Amalfi Coast.</span>
            </div>
            <div class="Paragraph__component" data-reactid="103">
             <span data-reactid="104">"Last year that would have cost $770, and this year it's going to be $594."</span>
            </div>
            <div class="Paragraph__component" data-reactid="105">
             <span data-reactid="106">Actually shopping for Italian fashion in Italy might be possible again for many.</span>
            </div>
            <div class="Paragraph__component" data-reactid="107">
             <span data-reactid="108">A men's <a href="http://www.massimoleather.it/Library/paginas/men/content/ji8_large.html" target="_blank">Avirex</a> leather jacket from<a href="http://www.massimoleather.it/" target="_blank"> Massimo Leather </a>(<em>Borgo la Noce, 13/15r, Florence, Italy; +39 0552 18634</em>) in Florence (the city is famous for its leather goods) is especially nice. </span>
            </div>
            <div class="Paragraph__component" data-reactid="109">
             <span data-reactid="110">Last year that gift to yourself or someone else would have cost $542. This year it's $390. </span>
            </div>
            <div class="Paragraph__component" data-reactid="111">
             <span data-reactid="112"><h3>Greece</h3></span>
            </div>
            <div class="Paragraph__component" data-reactid="113">
             <span data-reactid="114">Financially stricken Greece needs tourism income more than ever this year and dollars are just as welcome as euros, pounds, yuan, yen or krone. </span>
            </div>
            <div class="Paragraph__component" data-reactid="115">
             <span data-reactid="116">Peak-rate prices in the prime Med sunspots have remained steady despite turmoil in Athens, but the greenback's rebound means visitors with dollar-pegged currencies will enjoy some discounts on a year ago.</span>
            </div>
            <div class="Paragraph__component" data-reactid="117">
             <span data-reactid="118">Take Santorini, the ever-popular volcanic island where honeymooning couples witness nightly sunsets of such staggering beauty, the rest of their marriage is guaranteed to be an anticlimax.</span>
            </div>
            <div class="Paragraph__component" data-reactid="119">
             <span data-reactid="120">A stay on one of the island's <a href="http://www.astrasuites.com/en/Accommodation/pool_suite" target="_blank">premium pool suites</a> overlooking the Aegean Sea can currently be snapped up for $640 per night -- that's a $250 saving on 12 months ago.</span>
            </div>
            <div class="Paragraph__component" data-reactid="121">
             <span data-reactid="122">Ignore the fact that "Mamma Mia!" was filmed on the island of Skopelos, and a $1,500 <a href="http://edition.cnn.com/2015/01/16/travel/exclusive-mediterranean-cruises/index.html">tour of the surrounding Sporades archipelago</a> is a steal compared to $2,085 a year ago.</span>
            </div>
            <div class="Paragraph__component" data-reactid="123">
             <span data-reactid="124">That means there's more than enough change to redress the cultural imbalance created by an ABBA-themed musical by visiting the <a href="http://www.acropolisofathens.gr/aoa/the-acropolis/monument/" target="_blank">Acropolis</a> in Athens (<em>+30 21 0321 4172</em>). Entrance fee is now just $12, down from $17.</span>
            </div>
            <div class="Paragraph__component" data-reactid="125">
             <span data-reactid="126">It also means visitors can afford to lavish some pretty decent tips on their Greek hosts. After all, it's partly thanks to their economic pain that the eurozone has become such a dollar playground. </span>
            </div>
            <div class="Paragraph__component" data-reactid="127">
             <span data-reactid="128"><h3>Finland</h3></span>
            </div>
            <div class="Paragraph__component" data-reactid="129">
             <span data-reactid="130">A bank account full of Benjamins won't keep anyone warm at night -- unless they're using them to pay for a night under the northern lights in one of Finnish Lapland's <a href="http://www.kakslauttanen.fi/" target="_blank">glass-covered igloos</a>. </span>
            </div>
            <div class="Paragraph__component" data-reactid="131">
             <span data-reactid="132">Open from late August to early April, Hotel Kakslauttanen's glowing huts vary in price according to the season but average around $203 per night, per person (each one sleeps two). A year ago, the experience would have cost $282.</span>
            </div>
            <div class="Paragraph__component" data-reactid="133">
             <span data-reactid="134">The savings can help pad the $270 fee it's going to cost to head four hours south to Kemi and chill out on Finland's famed <a href="http://www.visitkemi.fi/en/sampo-icebreaker/four-hour-afternoon-cruise?id=43" target="_blank">ice-breaking Sampo ship</a>.</span>
            </div>
            <div class="Paragraph__component" data-reactid="135">
             <span data-reactid="136">Price includes an opportunity to squeeze into a waterproof survival suit for a float in the Gulf of Bothnia's freezing waters -- a teeth chattering thrill that would have cost $375 a year ago. </span>
            </div>
            <div class="Paragraph__component" data-reactid="137">
             <span data-reactid="138">Eventually, thoughts of "I never want to see another block of ice again" will strike. </span>
            </div>
            <div class="Paragraph__component" data-reactid="139">
             <span data-reactid="140">When this occurs, there are overnight trains from Kemi down to Helsinki that currently cost around $78 per ticket -- down from $108. </span>
            </div>
            <div class="Paragraph__component" data-reactid="141">
             <span data-reactid="142">Travelers can put the savings toward a $42 four-course lunch at Michelin-starred <a href="http://olo-ravintola.fi/" target="_blank">Restaurant Olo</a> (<em>Pohjoisesplanadi 5, 00170 Helsinki, Finland; +358 10 3206250</em>), a harbor-side restaurant famous for its Nordic cuisine. </span>
            </div>
            <div class="Paragraph__component" data-reactid="143">
             <span data-reactid="144">What to do with the $16 saved by not eating there a year ago? </span>
            </div>
            <div class="Paragraph__component" data-reactid="145">
             <span data-reactid="146">Purchase a $12 ticket to get into the <a href="http://www.kiasma.fi/en/" target="_blank">Museum of Contemporary Art Kiasma</a> (<em>Mannerheimplatsen 2, 00100 Helsingfors, Finland; +358 29 4500501</em>), which just reopened following a year and a half of renovations. </span>
            </div>
            <div class="Paragraph__component" data-reactid="147">
             <span data-reactid="148"><h3>France</h3></span>
            </div>
            <div class="CaptionedImage__component" data-reactid="149">
             <div class="Image__component Image__hasAspectRatio" style="padding-top:56.25%;" data-reactid="150">
              <div style="height:100%;" class="lazyload-placeholder" data-reactid="151"></div>
             </div>
             <div class="CaptionedImage__caption" data-reactid="152">
              Got dollars? Seeing the Mona Lisa just got cheaper.
             </div>
            </div>
            <div class="Paragraph__component" data-reactid="153">
             <span data-reactid="154">There's only one thing more incredible than the prices Parisian shopkeepers charge visitors for a single can of soda -- and that's the fact that tourists still pay them.</span>
            </div>
            <div class="Paragraph__component" data-reactid="155">
             <span data-reactid="156">The rebalancing of the exchange rate will buy many dollar visitors the satisfying knowledge they're getting bilked marginally less for routine refreshments.</span>
            </div>
            <div class="Paragraph__component" data-reactid="157">
             <span data-reactid="158">That should give them enough spring in their stride to tackle the enervating galleries of the <a href="http://www.louvre.fr/en/hours-admission/admission" target="_blank">Louvre</a> (<em>+33 1 40 20 50 50</em>), a snip at $12, compared to $16.68 at 2014 rates.</span>
            </div>
            <div class="Paragraph__component" data-reactid="159">
             <span data-reactid="160">Still dizzied by the size of those savings? </span>
            </div>
            <div class="Paragraph__component" data-reactid="161">
             <span data-reactid="162">Go bananas at the fabulously opulent <a href="http://paris.peninsula.com/en/default" target="_blank">Peninsula Paris</a> and book a night in the Terrace Suite for $7,700 (<em>19 Avenue Kleber, 75116 Paris, France; +33 1 58 12 28 88</em>?).</span>
            </div>
            <div class="Paragraph__component" data-reactid="163">
             <span data-reactid="164">A year ago, that would've cost $10,730 -- had it been open. </span>
            </div>
            <div class="Paragraph__component" data-reactid="165">
             <span data-reactid="166">It wasn't open until last August, though, so not only are you getting a (relative) bargain, but those lovely soft bathroom towels have been sullied by fewer strangers.</span>
            </div>
            <div class="Paragraph__component" data-reactid="167">
             <span data-reactid="168"><h3>Ireland</h3></span>
            </div>
            <div class="Paragraph__component" data-reactid="169">
             <span data-reactid="170">"We haven't seen a situation like this in ... I can't remember when. Quite a long time."</span>
            </div>
            <div class="Paragraph__component" data-reactid="171">
             <span data-reactid="172">That enthusiastic appraisal of deals available to Americans traveling in Ireland comes from Ellen McNulty, president of New York-based<a href="http://www.lynotttours.com/index.html" target="_blank"> Lynott Tours</a> (<em>+1 800 221 2474</em>), which has been helping Americans travel to the country for 42 years.</span>
            </div>
            <div class="Paragraph__component" data-reactid="173">
             <span data-reactid="174">"I don't even know if the American public is aware of the bargains to be had," she adds.</span>
            </div>
            <div class="Paragraph__component" data-reactid="175">
             <span data-reactid="176">One of the activities McNulty's happy clients have recently booked is the nine-night Irish Melody Family Vacation, which delivers kids activities (horseback rides, boat trips) along with regular musical events, including dances and traditional music nights in pubs.</span>
            </div>
            <div class="Paragraph__component" data-reactid="177">
             <span data-reactid="178">In 2014, the tour (hotels, transportation, food and other basics included, though not air fare) dinged travelers for $2,931 per person. Now it's $2,344 -- about $2,350 savings for a family of four.</span>
            </div>
            <div class="Paragraph__component" data-reactid="179">
             <span data-reactid="180">But you don't have to have kids to save money in Ireland (um, we think that sounds right).</span>
            </div>
            <div class="Paragraph__component" data-reactid="181">
             <span data-reactid="182">Kid-free beer fans can tour the <a href="http://www.guinness-storehouse.com/en/Index.aspx" target="_blank">Guinness Storehouse</a> (<em>St. James Gate, Dublin 8; +353 1 408 4800</em>) -- widely hailed as the most popular tourist attraction in Ireland -- for just $18. Last year tour tickets went for about $25.</span>
            </div>
            <div class="Paragraph__component" data-reactid="183">
             <span data-reactid="184">The $7 windfall is easily enough for another pint in an Irish pub, perhaps the most enjoyable way ever devised of being frugal while spending. </span>
            </div>
            <div class="Paragraph__component" data-reactid="185">
             <span data-reactid="186"><h3>Germany </h3></span>
            </div>
            <div class="Paragraph__component" data-reactid="187">
             <span data-reactid="188">Now would be the time to memorize the phrase, "Noch ein bier, bitte" ("One more beer, please.")</span>
            </div>
            <div class="Paragraph__component" data-reactid="189">
             <span data-reactid="190">These days, visitors exchanging their greenbacks will be able keep the taps flowing for longer during a visit to Munich's centuries <a href="http://edition.cnn.com/2015/03/17/travel/experts-guide-to-german-beer/index.html">old beer cellars and up-and-coming craft breweries</a>. </span>
            </div>
            <div class="Paragraph__component" data-reactid="191">
             <span data-reactid="192">Sleeping it all off in five-star surroundings is, of course, much cheaper too. </span>
            </div>
            <div class="Paragraph__component" data-reactid="193">
             <span data-reactid="194">A night in a superior double room the historic <a href="http://www.bayerischerhof.de/" target="_blank">Bayerischer Hof</a> (<em>Promenadeplatz 2-6, Munich, Germany; +49 89 21200</em>) will cost about $549 -- not bad, considering the same room would have cost $763 a year ago.</span>
            </div>
            <div class="Paragraph__component" data-reactid="195">
             <span data-reactid="196">Those savings will more than cover a pair of two-hour first class train tickets to Fussen, home to <a href="http://www.neuschwanstein.de/" target="_blank">Neuschwanstein</a> (<em>Neuschwansteinstrasse 20, 87645 Schwangau, Germany; +49 8362 930830</em>), one of the world's most famous castles.</span>
            </div>
            <div class="Paragraph__component" data-reactid="197">
             <span data-reactid="198">A one-way rail ticket between the two now costs about $40 -- a savings of about $15 over 2014.</span>
            </div>
            <div class="Paragraph__component" data-reactid="199">
             <span data-reactid="200">The change is enough to pay the $12 entrance fee to the stunning castle -- a bargain compared to the princely sum of roughly $17 dollar earners would have coughed up a year ago. </span>
            </div>
            <div class="Paragraph__component" data-reactid="201">
             <span data-reactid="202"><h3>Monaco</h3></span>
            </div>
            <div class="CaptionedImage__component" data-reactid="203">
             <div class="Image__component Image__hasAspectRatio" style="padding-top:56.25%;" data-reactid="204">
              <div style="height:100%;" class="lazyload-placeholder" data-reactid="205"></div>
             </div>
             <div class="CaptionedImage__caption" data-reactid="206">
              Now your casino dollar losses are worth more.
             </div>
            </div>
            <div class="Paragraph__component" data-reactid="207">
             <span data-reactid="208">While it's true Monaco has got slightly cheaper for dollar visitors, that doesn't put James Bond-style high rolling within the reach of most dollar visitors.</span>
            </div>
            <div class="Paragraph__component" data-reactid="209">
             <span data-reactid="210">At 2014's exchange rates, a platinum ticket for the <a href="http://tickets.formula1.com/en/3202-monaco-fom/" target="_blank">Monaco Grand Prix</a> would've cost $3,891. Today's price is less eye-watering at $2,799, but still eye-watering considering you're just paying to watch fast traffic. </span>
            </div>
            <div class="Paragraph__component" data-reactid="211">
             <span data-reactid="212">But, sorry, there's been no change in the <a href="http://www.casinomontecarlo.com/" target="_blank">Casino de Monte Carlo</a> (<em>Place du Casino, 98000 Monaco; +377 98 06 21 21</em>). </span>
            </div>
            <div class="Paragraph__component" data-reactid="213">
             <span data-reactid="214">Losing your life's savings still costs the same as it did 12 months ago.</span>
            </div>
            <div class="Paragraph__component" data-reactid="215">
             <span data-reactid="216"><h3>Belgium</h3></span>
            </div>
            <div class="Paragraph__component" data-reactid="217">
             <span data-reactid="218">Hard though it is to believe that a trip to Belgium could get any more exciting, the new exchange rate achieves the near-impossible.</span>
            </div>
            <div class="Paragraph__component" data-reactid="219">
             <span data-reactid="220">A Saturday evening of super-fancy seafood at Brussels' celebrated<a href="https://www.commechezsoi.be/en-us/Comme-chez-soi" target="_blank"> Comme Chez Soi </a>restaurant with a six course taster menu for $195, down from $271 at 2014 rates.</span>
            </div>
            <div class="Paragraph__component" data-reactid="221">
             <span data-reactid="222">Still got room for more? </span>
            </div>
            <div class="Paragraph__component" data-reactid="223">
             <span data-reactid="224">A tiered box of the finest Belgian chocs from <a href="http://www.marcolini-eboutique.com/Pages/product.aspx?category=Collections(Marcolini-Base)&amp;cat=Marcolini-fr-BE&amp;pid=000003(Marcolini-Base)&amp;rank=6&amp;SortBy=Rank&amp;Asc=1&amp;r=1" target="_blank">Pierre Marcolini</a> costs $95 -- that's $37 cheaper than a year ago.</span>
            </div>
            <div class="Paragraph__component" data-reactid="225">
             <span data-reactid="226">After finishing the night with a crate of 8.5% proof <a href="http://www.beerofbelgium.com/en/delirium-tremens-75cl.html" target="_blank">Delirium Tremens</a> beer (down to $6.95 a bottle from $9.70), maybe the best thing those shaky hands can do is buy a rail ticket away from the scene of whatever happened next.</span>
            </div>
            <div class="Paragraph__component" data-reactid="227">
             <span data-reactid="228">A last-minute standard <a href="http://www.eurostar.com" target="_blank">Eurostar</a> ticket one-way to London costs about $161, down from $224.</span>
            </div>
            <div class="Paragraph__component" data-reactid="229">
             <span data-reactid="230">Then it's time to convert those dollars to pounds -- and that's a whole other numbers game. </span>
            </div>
