     <h6>Extract Images from PDF</h6> 
     <p>PDF files can contain not just images but also complex clips and this fully automated example program allows you to extract each image and apply the clip associated with it. <em><b>But how do you get the images from the PDF files complete with clipping?</b></em><br>&nbsp;</p>
