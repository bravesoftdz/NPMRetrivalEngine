     <h1>InnerTrack™</h1> 
     <div class="image_after_heading">
      <div class="image ">
       <img src="/cms-files/size-992x992/products-solutions-01.k.jpg" alt="">
      </div>
     </div> 
     <p>For many organizations, the minute a shipment comes off a delivery truck onto the receiving dock, visibility stops. With manual processes, finding missing package or tracking delivered items takes hours of staff time, increasing costs and decreasing productivity.</p> 
     <p>In fact, many hospitals still have:</p> 
     <ul> 
      <li>Paper-based receiving processes</li> 
      <li>Lack of visibility to packages being received or transported from pick-up location to destination</li> 
      <li>Hours of staff time spent each week locating supplies or recovering lost shipments</li> 
      <li>Higher costs due to labor, errors and rework, and inaccurate information</li> 
      <li>Lack of an electronic audit trail for chain of custody documentation</li> 
     </ul> 
     <h2>Get to know InnerTrack Receiving and Package Tracking.</h2> 
     <p>InnerTrack helps you efficiently manage the receipt, delivery and tracking of packages from dock-to-destination — automating manual processes, creating visibility and documenting an electronic record of every transaction.</p> 
     <p>With InnerTrack, packages are seamlessly tracked and easily visible throughout the process — from dock-to-destination, or pick-up through delivery, from facility to facility. An electronic chain of custody record provides a complete audit trail for items moving within your organization.</p> 
     <p>InnerTrack reporting provides real-time status based on the latest scans; users can track route and delivery efficiencies, review integrated carrier details, and analyze overall performance. InnerTrack reports also provide a view of incoming freight handling and costs, identifying shipping methods such as overnight priority, and associating departments and specific items to help identify potential inventory issues.</p> 
     <p>It's easy to get started. The InnerTrack web portal allows quick and easy location and route creation, identification of team members, and set-up of any specific requirements. Then, incoming shipments, pick-ups and deliveries are managed with a simple mobile interface on a smart device. With visibility to the entire end-to-end transaction, web-based users can track the progress of deliveries anywhere in the health system.</p>
