             <h5>About this deal...</h5> 
             <div class="bus-about-text"> 
              <p>Other than chicken fingers, video games and dirt, the one thing kids can never seem to get enough of is fun. Enjoy good, clean fun with this offer from Ozzy’s Family Fun Center, good for 1 round of laser tag for 4 players plus one cheese pizza and one pitcher of soda for only $23.50, a $47 value.</p> 
              <p>There’s so much fun to be had at Ozzy’s Family Fun Center, you might have a hard time figuring out where to start.&nbsp;Leesport's home of laser-blasting family fun, the black-lit, 4,760 square-foot arena uses state-of-the-art Lasertron 11.5 Game System. Up to 36 people will dodge obstacles and flashing lights. Feel the adrenaline rush as you avoid laser&nbsp;blasts to help your side defeat your opponents, deactivate their headquarters, and bask in the glory of conquest. Birthday parties and group outings are welcomed, with various packages offered for large groups. When you've had your fill of space-age swashbuckling, take a break in the huge arcade, filled with all the latest video games and redemption games for prizes. They also have rollerskating, rock climbing, sky trail ropes course,&nbsp;a huge indoor play zone and so much more. When the weather's warm, check out the go-karts, blaster&nbsp;boats and mini golf.</p>

They feature a private room that's great for birthday parties and other events, and it's the perfect place for company picnics and other group outings. They're open seven days a week.</p>
