      <h1>Which Object Recognition Methods Apply to Your Project?</h1> 
      <h2>Traditional Method</h2> 
      <p>This method is appropriate when the object you want to recognize follows readily identifiable patterns, attributes and rules. These characteristics make it easier to distinguish among different types of objects. For example, such attributes can be the size, shape and pattern of the tread on a particular type of car tire.</p> 
      <h2>Machine Learning Method</h2> 
      <p>This method is appropriate when the objects of interest share common characteristics but must be distinguished and a huge number of variations must be taken into account. To use a machine learning solution, a large number of images is required for learning to occur.</p> 
      <h2>Blended Methods</h2> 
      <p>When both methods are needed, a blended approach is best.</p>
