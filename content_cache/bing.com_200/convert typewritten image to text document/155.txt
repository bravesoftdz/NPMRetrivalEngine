       <li> <a name="Convert"></a> <h3>Convert</h3> <p>This sample shows how to use PDFNet Convert Add-on (i.e. 'pdftron.PDF.Convert' namespace) for direct, high-quality conversion between PDF, XPS, EMF, SVG, TIFF, PNG, JPEG, and other image formats.
