          <p>***** Extract text from image and save as editable text file *****</p> 
          <p>ScanText OCR App will read text from paper and translate the images into editable text. With ScanText OCR App, you can take a photo of book page, newspaper and then you will get the text that could be edited.</p> 
          <p>Key Features:</p> 
          <p>* High OCR accuracy with a clear print text photo or image.<br>* Automatically detect document edge and correct perspective.<br>* Powerful crop function lets you OCR any part of a document.<br>* Support multi-language.<br>* Save OCR history and organize document with folders</p> 
          <p>OCR is abbreviation of optical character recognition. Optical character recognition is the mechanical or electronic conversion of images of typewritten or printed text into machine-encoded text. To get the best result with ScanText OCR App, please take a clear photo for the document with an even light.</p> 
          <p>***** Please note that current version does NOT support hand-writing OCR.</p>
