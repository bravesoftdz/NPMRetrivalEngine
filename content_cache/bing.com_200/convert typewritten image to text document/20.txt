          <h3 class="text-normal text-3-block dc-heading ">How to convert scanned PDFs to instantly editable text using automatic OCR software in Acrobat:</h3> 
         </div> 
         <div>

           <ol> 
            <li><span class="text-light">Open a PDF file containing a scanned image in Acrobat.<br> <br> </span></li> 
            <li><span class="text-light">Click on the Edit PDF tool in the right pane. Acrobat automatically applies optical character recognition (OCR) to your document and converts it to a fully editable copy of your PDF.</span><br> <br> </li> 
            <li><span class="text-light">Click the text element you wish to edit and start typing. New text matches the look of the original fonts in your scanned image.<br> <br> </span></li>
