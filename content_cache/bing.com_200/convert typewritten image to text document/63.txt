     <h2>What is JavaDocx ?</h2> 
     <p><strong>JavaDocx</strong> is a java library designed to generate completely dynamic and fully customizable Word documents.</p> 
     <p><strong>JavaDocx</strong> is hosted in your server providing a very and complete flexible solution for your document and reports generation needs.</p> 
     <p><strong>JavaDocx</strong> also provides an easy method to generate documents in other formats such as PDF or HTML.</p> 
     <a href="http://www.xmldocx.com" target="_blank"><img src="/wp-content/themes/lightword/images/banner_javadocx_2.1.png" alt="DOCX generation"></a> 
     <h2>What can I do with JavaDocx ?</h2> 
     <p>With a few lines of java code you may create a complete report that includes:</p> 
     <ul> 
      <li>Editable Text</li> 
      <li>Images and Graphic elements</li> 
      <li>Standard paragraphs</li> 
      <li>Bulleted or numbered lists</li> 
      <li>Customized tables</li> 
      <li>Dinamic tables</li> 
      <li>All kind of customizable and editable charts or graphs to display numerical data</li> 
      <li>Tables of content to simplify readability within long documents</li> 
      <li>Headers that will reflect your corporate branding</li> 
      <li>Footers with automatic page numbering</li> 
      <li>Embed external DOCX, HTML, RTF and MHT documents</li> 
      <li>Conversion plugin to transform DOCX to PDF</li> 
     </ul> 
     <p>You could also create and use your own templates or make Mailmerge of Reports and documents.</p> 
     <p><strong>JavaDocx</strong> allows programmers to generate documents in a fast and convenient way when used with its default settings.</p> 
     <p>When design is a priority,<strong> JavaDocx</strong> allows you to generate fully customized reports to match your company's branding or your previously created design.</p>
