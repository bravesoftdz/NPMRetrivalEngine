       <p class="entry-subtitle">Instantly Convert Scans, Image, JPG, PDF to Editable Text or Ms. Word - No Need to Retype Again</p>

     <div class="content-item-text content-item inner links-main" itemprop="text"> 
      <div class="block-full-content clear"> 
       <p><strong>Last Update May 6th, 2017</strong></p> 
       <p class="text-intro">Best OCR Software is software that is not only FAST converting the image, pdf or books into editable text (words) but it also has a <strong>100% accuracy</strong> and keep up <strong>formatting</strong> alike with original document.</p> 
       <p>Definition: <strong>OCR Software</strong> (optical character recognition software) is software for convert scans to editable text without RETYPING with keep format &amp; layout like your original document.</p> 
       <p>in simple word, OCR Software is <strong>converter</strong> for image to text or jpeg to word that editable.</p> 
       <p>OCR software helps you for converting your books or paper become digital document format. Either text format (like notepad) or word format (Microsoft Word), which is all <strong>editable</strong>.</p> 
       <p><strong>Are you finding what best ocr software for scanning books, paper documents or receipts?</strong></p> 
       <p>There are many choices OCR software that can ease your work <strong>without having to retype</strong> the document either desktop or online versions, free and paid. </p> 
       <p>Then, which one best OCR Software is <strong>suitable</strong> for you?</p> 
       <p><b>Criteria for a Good OCR Software?</b></p> 
       <p>Format must be maintained by the original:</p> 
       <ul class="list-check"> 
        <li>Layout (column)</li> 
        <li>Text fonts (type and size)</li> 
        <li>Text Formatting (<strong>Bold</strong>, <em>Italic</em>, <span style="text-decoration: underline;">Underline</span>).</li> 
        <li>Table convert to excel</li> 
       </ul> 
       <p>in simple words, a good ocr software while maintaining the format and layout as the <strong>original document</strong>.</p> 
       <p><span id="more-552"></span></p> 
       <p><strong>How I Test OCR Software?</strong>: </p> 
       <p>For quality and performance testing of best OCR software, I made a Word document containing text, a picture and a table and convert word to image (JPG with 300 dpi) (<a href="https://winbeginner-548d.kxcdn.com/wp-content/uploads/Optical-character-recognition.jpg" target="_blank">Image File For OCR Test (JPG)</a>).</p> 
       <p>and then, I used ocr software to change it back the image file into a Word.</p>

       <h3>2. Google Docs (Google Drive)</h3> 
       <p>Google Docs® is normally used for archiving documents, but do you know that Google Docs® also supports OCR?</p> 
       <p><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-586" src="https://winbeginner-548d.kxcdn.com/wp-content/uploads/Google-Docs-OCR.jpg" alt="Google Docs OCR" width="650" height="382"></p> 
       <p>You may convert your graphical files to editable text by uploading them to Google Docs®. However, you need to make sure to choose <strong>Convert text from PDF and image files to Google documents</strong> when uploading.</p>
