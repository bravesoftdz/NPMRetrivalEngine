   <p> These pages <b>introduce</b> some fundamentals of genetic algorithms. Pages are intended to be used for learning about genetic algorithms <b>without any previous knowledge</b> from this area. Only some knowledge of computer programming is assumed. You can find here several interactive <b>Java applets</b> demonstrating work of genetic algorithms. </p>
