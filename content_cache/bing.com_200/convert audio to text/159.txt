     <h3>Automatic audio to text conversion is largely science fiction...</h3> 
     <p class="narrow">Current tools fail miserably in handling real-world audios with multiple speakers and background noise.</p> 
     <p class="narrow">Instead, Transcribe eases the manual process of audio transcription by giving you a <strong>2x-3x speed up</strong>.</p>

     <h3>So, how does it work?</h3> 
     <p class="narrow">Transcribe offers an audio player that's tightly integrated with a text editor on the same screen.</p> 
     <p class="narrow">So, no more switching back and forth between the audio player and the editor.</p> 
     <img class="screen" src="/assets/transcribe_lite_screen1-80400ebb5fd75fad377dbfb7adc5a0dd.png" alt="Text editor with integrated audio player"> 
     <p class="narrow">You can slow down, pause or rewind audio anytime using single-keypress keyboard shortcuts.</p> 
     <p class="narrow">Want to insert the current timestamp? That's just another shortcut away.</p> 
     <img class="screen" src="/assets/transcribe_lite_screen2-3b737aa156361db060f0066414910662.png" alt="Text editor with integrated audio player"> 
     <h4 class="narrow">Hate typing? Convert your speech to text via fast &amp; accurate dictation!</h4> 
     <p class="narrow">Listening to the audio and speaking out what you hear is way faster than typing.</p> 
     <p class="narrow">Our online dictation engine supports all major European and Asian languages.</p> 
     <img class="screen" src="/assets/transcribe_lite_screen3-64adae560ecdf68a223eaffa5a2e7c17.png" alt="Text editor with integrated audio player"> 
     <p class="narrow"><strong>Breeze through your transcription with a foot pedal!</strong></p> 
     <p>By connecting your foot pedal to Transcribe, you can control audio playback using your foot.</p>
