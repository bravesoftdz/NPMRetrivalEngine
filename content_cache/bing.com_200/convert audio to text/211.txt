              <p>In this tutorial I will show how to convert the audio file into text by using <strong>Visual Basic 2008</strong>. This method helps you, how to recognize the content of your audio file. For instance, if the audio file cannot be heard or cannot be understood. It will be recognized, because the content of it will appear in the Box through text.</p> 
              <p>Let’s begin: </p>
