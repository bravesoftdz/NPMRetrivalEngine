          <h2 class="contentheading"> Family Vacation Resorts</h2> 
          <p class="buttonheading"> </p> 
          <p class="article-img"><img src="/images/stories/img-family-vacation-resorts.jpg"></p> 
          <p>When you're planning your next vacation for you and your family, Ocean City, New Jersey should be at the top of your list of family vacation resorts. Year after year, OCNJ is ranked as one of the leading vacation destinations for families, and a trip to Ocean City has become a family tradition for thousands of visitors across the country. If you're looking for a great place to spend your vacation this year, come to Ocean City, NJ to see why we're known as “America's Greatest Family Resort.”</p> 
          <p>The beach is the main attraction for any family vacation resort, and the beaches at Ocean City are some of the finest on the East Coast. Stretching eight miles, the island's white sandy beaches attract vacationers from around the world. Spend a relaxing day lounging in the sun, building a sandcastle, swimming in the ocean – the beach at Ocean City is perfect for families of any size and kids of all ages. Lifeguards are stationed along the beach during the summer to protect beach-goers and maintain a fun, safe atmosphere for everyone during their time at the shore.</p>
          <p>The Ocean City Boardwalk is famous the world over. For decades, the Boardwalk has been a must-see stop for vacationers looking for great shopping, restaurants, live entertainment, and family-friendly activities. With two amusement parks, a water park, arcades, and superb music venues, the Boardwalk has something for the whole family to enjoy. The diversity of the dozens of shops and stores along the Boardwalk offer unique souvenirs and collectibles you won't find at any other family vacation resort.</p>
          <p>During July and August, the merchants of the Boardwalk organize Family Night every Thursday as an expression of appreciation for Ocean City visitors. Family Night includes a unique activity at every block along the Boardwalk, such as live music, magicians, yo-yo demonstrations, face painting, and more. Free of charge and open to the public, Family Night creates an excellent way for a family to spend the evening on the Ocean City Boardwalk.</p>
          <p>On the other side of the island, Great Egg Harbor Bay is a thriving ecosystem perfect for fishing, boating, kayaking, and other outdoor activities. Several rental shops located on the bay side provide everything you need for a day of fishing on the water or from the shore. The bay makes for great fishing, with healthy populations of flounder, sea bass, striped bass, and more. Taking the kids fishing is a great way to spend time with them in a relaxing setting.</p>
          <p>Finding a place to stay in Ocean City, New Jersey is easy with our business directory. In our listings you'll find hotels, motels, condos, rental homes, and bed &amp; breakfasts located throughout the city. Regardless of your price range or room requirements, you're sure to find the accommodations you need for you and your family, whether you plan to stay for a night, a weekend, a week, or even longer. Browse Ocean City's hotels and motels to find the perfect place to stay.</p>
          <p>Ocean City prides itself in being the leading family vacation resort, and our community is ready and eager to make your next visit a truly unique experience. If you're planning your family's next trip, take a look at what Ocean City, NJ has to offer for families with kids of all ages.</p>
