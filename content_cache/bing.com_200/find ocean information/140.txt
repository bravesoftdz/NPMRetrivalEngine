     <h1>The friendly hotel overlooking beautiful sandown bay</h1> 
     <p><a id="skip"></a>The Ocean Hotel, family run since 1958 offers 96 bedrooms and is conveniently situated between the High Street and the Esplanade overlooking the popular <a title="Popular holiday destination Sandown Bay" href="/sandown-bay/">Sandown Bay</a>, with its six mile stretch of glorious well groomed sand.</p> 
     <p>The long beach only a few steps from the Hotel gardens, is an attraction for all ages and is safe for bathing at all times. Sandown bay can proudly boast of its success, as it is a regular holder of the UK sunshine record.</p> 
     <p>Long walks can also be enjoyed from the hotel, along coastal paths and into the countryside. The Isle Of Wight can be as interesting, <a title="Relaxing short breaks on the Isle of Wight" href="/relax/">relaxing</a> or <a title="Active holidays on the Isle of Wight" href="/get-active/">active</a> as you make it and the Ocean Hotel is an excellent base from which to explore the numerous attractions, countryside and natural beauty.</p>
