       <h1 class="subheaderblack1">Required Information</h1> 
       <br> 
       <h2 class="subheaderblack1"><strong>The following information is the minimum required to check an address:</strong></h2> 
       <ul> 
        <li>Address</li> 
        <li>City and state/province or ZIP or postal code</li> 
       </ul> 
       <h2 class="subheaderblack1"><strong>Things you should know: FedEx® Address Checker</strong></h2> 
       <ul> 
        <li>Provides street level address matches.</li> 
        <li>Contains a database of company listings to improve your results (not all companies are listed).</li> 
        <li>Receives monthly updates to its address database.</li> 
        <li>Checks addresses within the U.S., Puerto Rico, and Canada.</li> 
        <li>Distinguishes between business and residential addresses if an exact match is found. </li> 
        <li>Does not currently verify suite or apartment numbers.</li> 
        <li>Does not match addresses based upon individual or personal names, but can check an address by matching company names that correspond to street addresses.</li> 
       </ul> 
       <h2 class="subheaderblack1"><strong>How does FedEx Address Checker work?</strong></h2> 
       <ol> 
        <li>Checks if the street exists in the city and state/province entered, or in the ZIP or postal code entered.</li> 
        <li>Checks if the street number is within a valid range for the street entered.</li> 
        <li>Provides an exact match (or possible alternatives) based upon the street number, street name, city, state/province and ZIP or postal code entered.</li> 
        <li>Informs you if no possible alternatives can be found based upon the street number, street name, city, state/province, and ZIP or postal code entered. 
         <ul> 
          <li><b>Use correct spacing:</b> Make sure spaces are placed correctly, and avoid unnecessary spaces.</li> 
          <li><b>Use correct spelling:</b> Avoid any spelling and typographical errors. Also, make sure you have the correct usage of the number zero (0) and letter O.</li> 
          <li><b>Avoid special characters:</b> Please refrain from using special characters not required for the address, such as periods after abbreviations (Ave vs. Ave.).</li> 
          <li><b>Provide additional address and Street Information:</b> - By providing additional address information, you can increase the accuracy of address results. 
           <ul> 
            <li>Building or House number - 1, 1A, 1½, One</li> 
            <li>Street Name - Main, George Washington, 42nd</li> 
            <li>Street Suffix - Road, Avenue, Rd, Ave</li> 
           </ul> </li> 
          <li><b>Enter city, state/province and ZIP or postal code:</b> Providing all three will increase the accuracy of your address results. The +4 portion of the ZIP Code is not necessary to check an address.</li> 
          <li><b>Enter street and address in order:</b> Street address elements usually follow a format starting with the building or house number followed by a pre-directional element, a street name, street type or suffix, a post-directional element, apartment designation, apartment number and/or private mail box designation and number. 
           <ul> 
            <li>Different orders as well as language differences apply to addresses in Puerto Rico.</li> 
            <li>For more information, please see <a href="#diff">Differences Between Addresses in English and Spanish.</a></li> 
           </ul> </li> 
          <li><b>Use correct abbreviations:</b> The U.S. Postal Service and Canada Post has standard abbreviations for state/province, street suffix and apartment or unit designations. 
           <ul> 
            <li>A non-standard abbreviation may cause poor search results.</li> 
            <li>If you are unsure about an abbreviation, do not use it.</li> 
           </ul> </li> 
          <li><b>Company:</b> Entering a company name may improve your results. 
           <ul> 
            <li>Some addresses have specific company names assigned to them.</li> 
            <li>By entering the company name, FedEx Address Checker can search for that company and the address.</li> 
           </ul> </li> 
          <li><b>Multiple address results:</b> You will receive multiple address results when an exact match was not found. 
           <ul> 
            <li>You should confirm an address for accuracy before using it to ship a package.</li> 
            <li>To narrow your results, you can provide more specific address information and check the address again.</li> 
           </ul> </li> 
          <a name="#diff"></a> 
          <li><b>The difference between addresses in English and Spanish:</b> The Spanish representation of an address can differ significantly from English in the spelling and order, and additional information may be required. Other considerations for addresses in Puerto Rico include: 
           <ul> 
            <li><b>U.S. Example:</b><br> 651 Roosevelt St<br> Apt 1A<br> San Juan PR</li> 
            <li><b>Puerto Rico Example:</b><br> 651 Callejón Roosevelt<br> Apto 1A<br> San Juan PR</li> 
           </ul> </li> 
          <li><b>Common Spanish address translations and abbreviations:</b> 
           <ul> 
            <li>Apartamento = Apartment</li> 
            <li>Avenida = Avenue</li> 
            <li>Calle = Street</li> 
            <li>Callejón = Street</li> 
            <li>Cond = Condominium</li> 
            <li>Piso = Floor</li> 
            <li>Sr = Mr.</li> 
            <li>Sra = Mrs.</li> 
            <li>Srta = Miss</li> 
           </ul> </li> 
          <li><b>Urbanization</b> - An urbanization name denotes an area, sector, or residential development within a geographic area. This descriptor, commonly used in urban areas of Puerto Rico, is an important part of the addressing format as it describes the location of a given street. 
           <ul> 
            <li><b>Examples 1:</b><br> Sr Osiris Gonzáles<br> Urb Royal Oak<br> 123 Calle 1<br> Bayamón PR 00961-0123</li> 
            <li><b>Example 2:</b><br> Sr Pedro Rivera<br> Urb Hermosillo<br> 123 Calle 1<br> Bayamón PR 00961-1212</li> 
           </ul> </li> 
         </ul> </li> 
       </ol>
