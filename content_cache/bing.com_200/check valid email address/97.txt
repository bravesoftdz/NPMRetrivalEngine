       <h3>Address Verification and Correction Software</h3> 
       <p style="line-height: 15.6px; background-color: #ffffff;">SmartSoft offers address verification software solutions and API for you to capture, clean and correct your address data with our USPS&nbsp;<span style="font-size: 12px; line-height: 15.6px; background-color: #ffffff;">CASS Certified™ solution.</span></p> 
       <p style="line-height: 15.6px; background-color: #ffffff;">Available as&nbsp;<span style="font-size: 12px; color: #800000;">standalone products, APIs and toolkits&nbsp;</span>that integrate into your existing address-dependent applications, our SERP and CASS Certified™ address correction software validates, updates and standardizes the postal addresses in your database, and even provides you with additional data tied to each and every address.<br><br><a href="http://www.smartsoftusa.com/accumail-frameworks.html" style="font-size: 12px;">AccuMail frameworks</a>&nbsp;is our industry-leading next generation batch address verification and correction software, available as&nbsp;<i style="font-size: 12px;">either standalone software or in toolkit form</i>&nbsp;for simple integration into your existing applications. CASS Certified™, it is also available with a PAVE Certifed presorting module, as part of our&nbsp;<a href="http://www.smartsoftusa.com/accumail-mailing-software.html" style="font-size: 12px;">AccuMail frameworks Mailers Edition</a>.<br><br><a href="http://www.smartsoftusa.com/accumail-verify-web.html" style="font-size: 12px;">AccuMail Verify&nbsp;</a>API&nbsp;offers real-time point-of-entry address validation and correction for services, CRMs and other applications, validating addresses before they go into your database. USPS&nbsp;<span style="font-size: 12px; line-height: 15.6px; background-color: #ffffff;">CASS Certified™ address verification and correction as well as Canadian address verification is available with our APIs.&nbsp;</span><br><br><a href="http://www.smartsoftusa.com/canadian-addressing-mailing-software.html" style="font-size: 12px;">frameworks Canada</a>&nbsp;is SERP Certified address correction and validation software for Canada.</p> 
       <h4><img title="Address Correction Software" alt="Address Correction Software" src="http://www.smartsoftusa.com/uploads/image/envelopes.jpg" height="130" width="480"></h4> 
       <h4>Why Invest In Address Validation?</h4> 
       <p>The value of reliable customer contact data cannot be overstated. Best practices for your contact data management should include an address hygiene program. You rely on that data to fulfill your orders, deliver marketing messages to your customers, to bill and receive payments on time, and more.<br><br>The facts are, however, that this data is often incomplete or incorrect from the moment the data is captured. Vital address information may be missing when a customer places an order from a catalog, they may transpose numbers in a ZIP Code when filling out an online order form and/or spell a street name incorrectly, or errors may occur during data entry at a call center.<br><br>Furthermore, even if all the data is captured correctly, the quality of this information will still deteriorate over time. Customers may move without telling you, ZIP Codes may be changed or reassigned, streets get renamed etc.<br><br><a href="/the-cost-of-bad-data.html">The Costs of Bad Data</a> can be very high too:<a href="/the-cost-of-bad-data.html"><img style="float: right; margin: 5px;" alt="The Cost of Bad Address Data" src="http://www.smartsoftusa.com/uploads/image/cost-of-bad-data.jpg" height="191" width="250"></a><br><br>- Material and packaging costs<br>- Lost time and labor costs<br>- Costs of mailing and shipping<br>- Cash flow issues caused by late or non-existing payments<br>- Negative impression generated by lost or late mail/packages<br><br>It’s easy to see why the size of the problem for U.S. businesses is estimated in the hundreds of billions of dollars every year.</p>
