     <section class="blurb ui-block" id="advantages"> 
      <header> 
       <h1>Take the pain out of developing mobile apps.</h1> 
       <p>Simply upload your <b>HTML5</b>, <b>CSS</b>, and <b>JavaScript</b> assets to the <b>Adobe® PhoneGap™ Build</b> cloud service and we do the work of compiling for you.</p> 
      </header>

     <section class="blurb ui-block" id="advantages"> 
      <section class="content"> 
       <div class="advantage"> 
        <figure class="icon-phonegap"></figure> 
        <h2>Take advantage of the strengths of PhoneGap.</h2> 
        <p>Build great apps powered by open web standards. Cut down on development time by re-using your existing web dev skills, frameworks and tools. Get all the benefits of cross-platform development while building apps just the way you like.</p> 
       </div> 
       <div class="advantage"> 
        <figure class="icon-compile"></figure> 
        <h2>We compile for you.</h2> 
        <p>Get app-store ready apps without the headache of maintaining native SDKs. We do the work for you in the cloud and your app will always be built with the most up-to-date SDK for the platform you are targeting.</p> 
       </div> 
       <div class="advantage"> 
        <figure class="icon-devices"></figure> 
        <h2>Support multiple platforms.</h2> 
        <p>Maximize your productivity while minimizing production time. Target iOS, Android™ and Windows® Phone 8 all with a single codebase.</p> 
       </div> 
       <div class="advantage"> 
        <figure class="icon-team"></figure> 
        <h2>Work together.</h2> 
        <p>Add team members and create roles within your PhoneGap Build projects. Speed up collaboration.</p> 
       </div> 
       <div class="advantage"> 
        <figure class="icon-cycles"></figure> 
        <h2>Quick deployment cycles.</h2> 
        <p>Use the Hydration feature to get faster debug and build cycles. By compiling builds faster, your updates will be pushed directly to your testers' previously installed apps, ensuring everyone is working on the most up-to-date version.</p> 
       </div> 
       <div class="advantage"> 
        <figure class="icon-share"></figure> 
        <h2>Easily share your apps.</h2> 
        <p>Promote your projects to a bigger audience—review prototypes with colleagues, comps with clients, or share with friends and family. When your app is ready, just send a link. People will be able to download and install the app without connecting to a computer.</p> 
       </div> 
       <b id="faq-link"> Want to know more? <a href="http://docs.phonegap.com/phonegap-build/faq">Check out our FAQs</a> </b>

       <h1>What is Adobe® PhoneGap™?</h1> 
      </header> 
      <section class="content"> 
       <p>Write a PhoneGap app once with HTML and JavaScript and deploy it to any mobile device without losing features of a native app.</p> 
       <p>Adobe PhoneGap is a standards-based, open-source development framework for building cross-platform mobile apps with HTML, CSS and JavaScript for iOS, Android™ and Windows® Phone 8.</p>
