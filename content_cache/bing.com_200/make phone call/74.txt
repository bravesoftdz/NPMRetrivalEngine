     <div class="content"> 
      <p>Yes, you really can make free phone calls using the internet! Free internet phone programs let you make free phone calls, sometimes&nbsp;only to the US and Canada, using special software.</p>
      <p>The free phone call applications I list below are available in four different forms:</p>
      <ul>
       <li><em>PC to phone</em> programs are ones that&nbsp;can make a free call from your computer to a real telephone number so you don't even need a telephone yourself.</li>
       <li><em>App to phone</em>&nbsp;software are ones that make free internet calls from a mobile device to a real number.&nbsp;These services support making phone calls to any number, even landlines and mobile phones that aren't using the app.</li>
       <li><em>PC to PC</em>&nbsp;internet calling is when a computer makes a free phone call to another computer.</li>
       <li><em>App to app</em>&nbsp;services run entirely on mobile devices and results in&nbsp;phone calls that can only work&nbsp;if the recipient has the same application installed. This method&nbsp;can&nbsp;<em>not</em>&nbsp;be used to call landlines or other mobile phones that don't have the proper software.</li>
      </ul>
      <p>No matter how it works, it's a free call!</p>
      <p>To make a free call using an internet phone, you might have to download free phone call software or use a <em>softphone</em> from an internet phone provider website.</p>
      <p><strong>Important:</strong> A free internet phone can not be used to make a 911 or similar emergency call. If you need to call 911, use a traditional land-line or mobile phone, or a VoIP telephone service that's approved for 911 use like Vonage.</p>
