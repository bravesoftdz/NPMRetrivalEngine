         <h3>Understanding VoIP</h3> 
         <p>Voice over IP (VoIP) lets people make telephone calls via the internet. VoIP converts the voice signal from an ordinary telephone into a digital signal, which travels over the internet, and then converts it back. With our VoIP service customers can make and receive calls UK and worldwide through the internet.</p>
