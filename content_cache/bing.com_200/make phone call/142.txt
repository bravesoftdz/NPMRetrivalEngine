     <h2 class="us-tab-title"> <a class="us-tabs-nav-mainlink" href="#explain" data-target="#explain" target="_self"> Broadband deals explained 
       <svg role="presentation" aria-hidden="true" class="us-tabs-nav-chevron us-icon us-icon--small us-icon--after us-icon--inputgrey">

     <div class="us-tab-content"> 
      <div class="content-overflow"> 
       <p>Sign up for home phone and landline deals and youll benefit from cheap domestic, international and mobile calls, potentially cutting hundreds of pounds off of your monthly bills. Youll also be able to access a raft of handy calling features, including a block on sales calls and free voicemail. </p> 
       <p>Below is all the information you need to compare phone deals at our website, including our best phone line rental and home phone deals from all the major providers.</p> 
       <h3>Home phone and landline providers</h3> 
       <p>BT was once the UKs sole home telephone operator, but a number of rivals in the telecoms space have joined BT in the landline provider sector, including Virgin Media and TalkTalk.</p> 
       <p>The heightened levels of competition for your business mean that the market is now awash with excellent deals. Here well look at what some of the providers offer to give you a better idea of what you can expect if you sign up for a home phone and landline as a standalone option or add one to your existing services. </p> 
       <h3>BT home and landline</h3> 
       <p>Take a BT landline for 12 months and you'll have the option of signing up for the unlimited weekend calling plan or the unlimited anytime calling plan. </p> 
       <p>With the unlimited weekend calling plan, you get unlimited calls to UK landlines and inclusive calls to 0845 and 0870 numbers at weekends. The unlimited anytime calling plan lets you make unlimited calls to UK landlines, as well as 0870 and 0845 numbers, at any time of day. You'll also qualify for half-price calls to mobile phones. </p> 
       <p><strong>See the full range of BT broadband and home phone and standalone landline deals by heading to our <a href="/broadband/providers/bt/" title="BT">BT</a> page.</strong></p> 
       <p><strong>Looking for a BT landline only deal instead? Head to our <a href="/broadband/products/bt_phone_lines/" title="BT Phone Lines">BT new lines</a> page.</strong></p> 
       <h3>Vonage home and landline deals</h3> 
       <p>Vonage isn't a conventional home phone provider. Instead, it uses VoIP technology to let you make phone calls over your broadband connection, which can work out much cheaper. </p> 
       <p>Vonage also stands out for its commitment-free one-month contracts. </p> 
       <h3>Combining home phone and landline with broadband</h3> 
       <p>Taking multiple services from a single provider will get you substantial discounts on your monthly expenditure. But not only is broadband and home phone bundle a great way to save money, youll also get a single bill so youll be able to keep track of your outgoings more easily. </p> 
       <p><strong>To find out more about adding the home phone option to your broadband and digital TV bundle, See our <a href="/broadband/compare/bundles/" title="Broadband Bundles&quot; &quot;arrow">broadband bundles</a> page for more bestselling broadband bundle packages.</strong></p> 
       <p><strong>Need more information about home phone and broadband offers? Head to our <a href="https://www.uswitch.com/broadband/compare/broadband_and_home_phone/" title="Best Phone Deals">best phone deals page</a>.</strong></p> 
       <p>Looking for internet without landline? Check out our best <a href="/broadband/compare/broadband_without_phone_line/" title="Broadband Deals Without Phone Line&quot; &quot;arrow">broadband deals without phone line</a> page.</p>
