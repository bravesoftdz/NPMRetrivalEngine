       <h2>iOS</h2> 
       <p>Follow these instructions to activate and enable JavaScript in iOS on the iPhone and iPad.</p> 
       <ol> 
        <li>Select the <strong>Settings</strong> application/icon on the iPhone home screen.</li> 
        <li>Select <strong>Safari</strong> on the following screen.</li> 
        <li>Select <strong>Advanced</strong>, located near the bottom of the following screen.</li> 
        <li>Move the <strong>JavaScript</strong> toggle to the <strong>right</strong> so that it highlights <strong>green</strong>.</li>
