        <h1 class="entry-title">Create HTML link That Starts A Skype Call</h1> 
       </header>
       <!-- .entry-header --> 
       <div class="entry-content"> 
        <p>Update – 2012.01.15</p> 
        <p>You can get officially generated skype buttons for your web page at&nbsp;<a href="http://www.skype.com/intl/en-us/tell-a-friend/get-a-skype-button/" target="_blank">http://www.skype.com/intl/en-us/tell-a-friend/get-a-skype-button/</a>&nbsp;which offers a button that shows your current status!</p> 
        <hr> 
        <p><img class="aligncenter" src="https://mikegrace.s3.amazonaws.com/geek-blog/skpe-me.png" alt="" width="600" height="108"><br> To create a link that visitors can click on to call a phone number or skype username follow this syntax.<br> <em>Replace the asterisks with a valid username or number.</em></p> 
        <h2>Phone Number:</h2> 
        <pre lang="html4strict"><a href="callto://+***********">Link will initiate Skype to call my number!</a></pre> 
        <h2>Skype Username:</h2> 
        <pre lang="html4strict"><a href="skype:********?call">Link will initiate Skype to call my Skype username!</a></pre> 
       </div>
