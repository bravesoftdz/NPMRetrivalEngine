          <h2>What is a collect call?</h2> 
          <p>The person receiving the call is billed instead of the person making the call.</p> 
          <h2>Making a collect call</h2> 
          <p>To make a collect call using Bell Home phone, dial <strong>0</strong> and follow the automated voice prompts. The long distance charges will be billed to the person receiving the call, so they must accept the charges before the call can be connected. You'll be charged a small connection fee for long distance collect calls.</p> 
          <p>Charges are as follows:</p>

                this.goToAdventureStep(this.currentStep + 1);

                this.goToAdventureStep(this.currentStep - 1);

                        this.sendOmnitureTag(data.model.Content);
                        this.$dialogBox.animate({ scrollTop: 0 }, "fast");
                        this.show();
                    }, this))
                    .error($.proxy(this.handleError, this))
                    .fail($.proxy(this.handleError, this));
            },
            finishAdventure: function () {
                this.dataService.finishAdventure();
                this.close();
