    <div id="mw-content-text" lang="en" dir="ltr" class="mw-content-ltr">
     <p><a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="https://www.gnu.org">GNU</a> is a Unix-like operating system that is <a href="/wiki/Free_software" title="Free software">free software</a>—it respects your freedom. You can <a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="https://www.gnu.org/distros/free-distros.html">install</a> Linux-based versions of GNU which are entirely free software. The GNU System provides a collection of applications, libraries, and developer tools, plus a program to allocate resources and talk to the hardware, known as a kernel. </p>
     <p>For a complete list of GNU packages and more, visit <a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="https://www.gnu.org/software/">gnu.org/software</a>. </p>
