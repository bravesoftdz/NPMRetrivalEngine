             <p><img style="display: none;" alt="feature" title="feature"></p> 
             <p>Today we’re pleased to announce that we’ve integrated with <a rel="nofollow noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" href="https://coinbase.com/">Coinbase</a>, giving Shopify merchants more ways to <a target="_blank" href="http://www.shopify.com/bitcoin">accept bitcoin payments online</a>.</p> 
             <p>Coinbase is an international bitcoin wallet and platform service already used by companies like Overstock.com, Expedia, and Google. Our new Coinbase integration can be used alongside other payment services, like <a target="_blank" href="http://www.shopify.com/payments">Shopify Payments</a> and PayPal.</p> 
             <p>Coinbase offers a two-click checkout experience and simple refunds that make accepting bitcoin payments easy and convenient. All Shopify merchants can currently use Coinbase to accept bitcoin and anyone with a U.S. bank account can convert their bitcoin to USD.</p> 
             <p>A select group of Shopify merchants have been beta testing our new Coinbase integration including <a rel="nofollow noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" href="http://www.soylent.me/">Soylent</a>, <a rel="nofollow noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" href="http://store-us.leapmotion.com/">Leap Motion</a>, and <a rel="nofollow noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" href="http://www.iographer.com/">iOgrapher</a>.</p> 
             <p>Since <a target="_blank" href="/blog/10446157-shopify-merchants-can-now-accept-bitcoin">enabling bitcoin support earlier this year</a>, thousands of Shopify merchants have enthusiastically adopted it as an alternate payment method.</p> 
             <p><a target="_blank" href="http://docs.shopify.com/manual/settings/checkout-and-payment/payment-gateway#coinbase">Start accepting bitcoin with Coinbase</a>.</p> 
             <h2>The Benefits of Bitcoin for Ecommerce</h2> 
             <p>In addition to giving your customers more flexible payment options, bitcoin offers other important features:</p> 
             <ul> 
              <li>Customers from around the world can check out with digital currency.</li> 
              <li>Transactions cannot be reversed by the buyer (unlike credit cards or PayPal).</li> 
              <li>Transactions are secure and meet the highest standards for <a target="_blank" href="http://www.shopify.com/pci-compliant">ecommerce security</a>.</li> 
             </ul> 
             <h2>What is Bitcoin?</h2> 
             <p>Many people find bitcoin somewhat confusing. Quite simply, bitcoin is a peer-to-peer online financial network that people use to send payments from one person to another and it’s also a digital currency (also called bitcoin). The bitcoin network functions without the intermediation of a central authority. The managing of transactions and the issuing of bitcoins is carried out collectively by the network.</p> 
             <p><a rel="nofollow noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" href="http://www.vox.com/cards/bitcoin/what-is-bitcoin">This short video</a> helps explain:</p>
