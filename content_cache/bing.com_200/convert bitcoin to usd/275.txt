                <div class="post">
                 I'd love to see a pure-Javascript front-end GUI for bitcoind developed.
                 <br>
                 <br>I'm tempted to write one myself, but I've got a lot of other things on my TODO list right now.&nbsp; Here's what I'm imagining:
                 <br>
                 <br>+ Open source, pure JavaScript interface to bitcoin that communicates with a running bitcoin/bitcoind using the JSON-RPC api.
                 <br>
                 <br>+ Open up the index.html page that is the GUI and you'd be asked for the host:port (default: localhost:8332), username and password.
                 <br>
                 <br>+ From there, you'd have a nice Javascript/HTML GUI showing all your wallet transactions (using RPC listtransactions).
                 <br>
                 <br>+ And it'd show your default receiving address, have a Send Bitcoins button, etc.
                 <br>
                 <br>+ And it'd poll bitcoin/bitcoind every, oh, minute or so to look for new transactions.
                 <br>
                 <br>I'm imagining shipping a webGUI/index.html (plus associated CSS/javascript/etc) as part of the bitcoin(d) source package.
                 <br>
                </div> </td>
