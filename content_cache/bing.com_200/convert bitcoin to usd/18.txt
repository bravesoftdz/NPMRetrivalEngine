    <p>CoinWorker lets you earn bitcoin using only your web browser.</p> 
    <blockquote> 
     <p><b>Step 1:</b> <i>Introduce yourself.</i><br> <a href="/accounts/register">Register</a> and then enter your bitcoin receive address.</p> 
     <p><b>Step 2:</b> <i>Think a little.</i><br> Earn points by completing short analytical tasks which can be done in your browser. </p>
     <p><b>Step 3:</b> <i>Profit!</i><br> After your account hits 200 points, points convert to bitcoin (as if USD cents) and are paid directly to your address, usually within minutes. </p>
    </blockquote> 
    <p>Your pending balance and recent earning history is always visible in the public records of this website, as seen below.</p> 
    <p>Need to learn more about Bitcoin? You can get an introduction and free software at <a href="http://bitcoin.org">Bitcoin.org</a>, home of the original Bitcoin program. (That's one way to get a receive address usable here.)</p> 
    <p>CoinWorker is like a bitcoin faucet you can pump with mental effort. Or mining with your own mind rather than a GPU.</p>
