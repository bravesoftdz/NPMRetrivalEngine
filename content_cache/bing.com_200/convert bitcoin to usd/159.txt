      <h1 id="welcome-to-the-coinbase-digital-api">Welcome to the Coinbase Digital API</h1> 
      <p>Our API makes it easy to integrate bitcoin, litecoin and ethereum into both new and existing applications.</p> 
      <p>Coinbase’s APIs enable a variety of capabilities; from simply gathering read-only data, to building something that’s never been done before.</p> 
      <p>Capabilities:</p> 
      <ul> 
       <li>Generate bitcoin, litecoin and ethereum wallets and addresses</li> 
       <li>Buy/sell and send/receive bitcoin, litecoin and ethereum</li> 
       <li>Securely store bitcoin, litecoin and ethereum</li> 
       <li>Retrieve real-time or historical price information</li> 
       <li>Receive notifications when payments arrive</li> 
       <li>Accept and request bitcoin payments as a merchant</li> 
      </ul> 
      <p>We offer a variety of client libraries and mobile SDKs.</p>
