     <h3>How to identify legitimate emails from the GoUrl.io -</h3> 
     <p>GoUrl.io will never ask you to provide personal information or sensitive account information (such as passwords). All legitimate emails from <b>GoUrl.io</b> pass Sender Policy Framework (SPF), email-validation system and are designed to detect email spoofing. You should see in email header something similar to - <b>Received-SPF: PASS (domain of ....@gourl.io designates 51.255.140.174 as permitted sender) client-ip=51.255.140.174;</b></p>

All in automatic mode - no manual actions are needed</li>
