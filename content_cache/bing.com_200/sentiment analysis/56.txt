                    <h3><a name="TOC-What-is-Sentiment140-"></a>What is Sentiment140?</h3>
                    <h3><a name="TOC-Sentiment140-allows-you-to-discover-the-sentiment-of-a-brand-product-or-topic-on-Twitter."></a><span style="color:rgb(0,0,0);font-family:Arial;font-size:13px;line-height:normal">Sentiment140 allows you to discover the sentiment of a brand, product, or topic on Twitter.</span></h3>
                    <h3><a name="TOC-How-does-this-work-"></a>How does this work?</h3>
                    <h3><a name="TOC-You-can-read-about-our-approach-in-our-technical-report:-Twitter-Sentiment-Classification-using-Distant-Supervision.-There-are-also-additional-features-that-are-not-described-in-this-paper.How-is-this-different-Our-approach-is-different-from-other-sentimen"></a>
                     <div style="font-size:13px;color:rgb(0,0,0);font-family:Arial;line-height:normal">
                      You can read about our approach in our technical report:&nbsp;
                      <a href="http://cs.stanford.edu/people/alecmgo/papers/TwitterDistantSupervision09.pdf">Twitter Sentiment Classification using Distant Supervision</a>. There are also additional features that are not described in this paper.
                     </div>
                     <div style="font-size:13px;color:rgb(0,0,0);font-family:Arial;line-height:normal">
                      <font color="#444444" face="Arial, Verdana, sans-serif"><h2><a name="TOC-How-is-this-different-"></a>How is this different?</h2></font>
                      <div style="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px;direction:inherit">
                       Our approach is different from other sentiment analysis sites because:
                      </div>
                      <ul>
                       <li>We use classifiers built from&nbsp;<a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Machine_learning" style="color:rgb(85,26,139)" title="machine learning">machine learning</a>&nbsp;algorithms. Other products use a simpler keyword-based approach which may have higher precision but lower recall.</li>
                       <li>We provide transparency for the classification results of individual tweets. Other sites only surface aggregated metrics, which&nbsp;makes it difficult to assess the accuracy of their classifiers.</li>
                      </ul>
                     </div></h3>
